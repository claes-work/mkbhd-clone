# The Studio (channel + production operation)

The behind-the-scenes side of MKBHD — both the physical **studio in Kearny, New Jersey** and
"The Studio" YouTube channel (@TheStudio) that documents the team, gear, and process behind
the main channel.

## Physical studio [press-corroborated]

- ~**7,000 sq ft** facility at **Kearny Point**, a rehabbed shipyard in Kearny, NJ (shared
  with logistics firms and a helicopter-tour service). Filled with high-end production gear.
- The operation grew here after Brownlee graduated Stevens (May 2015) and formalized the
  business (2015–2016). Earlier "Studio tour" videos (e.g. "Inside MKBHD's $1,000,000 Studio")
  document the build-out. [source: fastcompany.com/90978935]

## The Studio channel [press-corroborated]

- A secondary YouTube channel (@TheStudio) for team Q&As, day-in-the-life, gear and
  process content — the "how the sausage is made" complement to the polished main channel.
- Part of the MKBHD channel family alongside the main channel, [[waveform]] (WVFRM),
  [[auto-focus]], and MKBHD Shorts.

## Relationship to the business

- The studio and team are the operational core of [[mkbhd-inc]]; named team members include
  [[andrew-manganelli]], [[david-imel]], and [[adam-molina]] (all CONTEXT people).
