# Waveform: The MKBHD Podcast

Marques Brownlee's weekly technology podcast — the audio/video counterpart to his YouTube
work, created to add news commentary and behind-the-scenes discussion beyond the polished
review videos.

## Founding & format [press-corroborated]

- **Founded 2019** — announced ~**July 31, 2019**, first full episode ~**August 9, 2019**.
  [source: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Waveform_(podcast)]
- **Weekly**, episodes ~45–130 min, plus monthly bonus episodes.
- Started **audio-only**; added **video on May 7, 2021**, with a separate clips channel
  (WVFRM / Waveform Clips on YouTube, @Waveform).
- Focus: consumer-electronics news, industry trends, and discussion of MKBHD videos; regular
  guest interviews (past guests include Mark Zuckerberg and Craig Federighi).
- By **November 2025**, **300+ episodes** released. Theme music by 20Syl.
- Brownlee was a **finalist for Best Podcaster at the 12th Shorty Awards (2020)**.

## Network history [press-corroborated]

- Launched in partnership with **Studio71**.
- Moved to the **Vox Media Podcast Network in August 2021** (distribution, sales, marketing).
  [source: tubefilter.com/2021/08/13/marques-brownlee-waveform-podcast-vox-media-podcast-network]
  > ⚠️ CONTRADICTION with bootstrap brief: the brief said "2022 / acquired by Vox." Correction:
  > the move was **August 2021**, and it was a **distribution partnership, not an acquisition.**
