# MKBHD, Inc. / JMMS, LLC — the business

The company behind Marques Brownlee's media operation: a video-production and creator
business headquartered in a studio in **Kearny, New Jersey**. The public-facing brand is
**"MKBHD, Inc."**; the registry-verified legal entity behind the intellectual property is
**JMMS, LLC**.

## The Studio (Kearny, NJ) [press-corroborated]

- ~**7,000 sq ft** production facility at **Kearny Point**, a rehabbed shipyard in Kearny, NJ,
  shared with logistics and helicopter-tour tenants; filled with high-end camera/lighting gear.
- The operation formalized here from ~2015–2016, after Brownlee graduated Stevens and went
  full-time. [source: fastcompany.com/90978935]
