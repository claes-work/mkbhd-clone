# Auto Focus (car-review channel)

Marques Brownlee's dedicated automotive YouTube channel (@AutoFocus) — the same
review sensibility as MKBHD, applied to cars (heavily EVs).

## Launch & growth [press-corroborated]

- **Launched August 2022**; **first video featured the Rivian R1T.** A running gimmick: much
  of it is **shot on smartphones**, tying back to his camera-testing beat.
  [sources: autoevolution.com/news/...-196972.html; grandtournation.com/...]
- Passed **1,000,000 subscribers by ~mid-2024** (he noted the milestone, still shot on phones).
  [source: threads.com/@mkbhd/post/C7496eAPL4h]
