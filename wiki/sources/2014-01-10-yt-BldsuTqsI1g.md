# Samsung Galaxy NotePro & TabPro at CES 2014! (@mkbhd, 2014-01-10)

## Summary
A CES floor clip on Samsung's new Pro-series tablets (NotePro 12.2" + TabPro 8.4/10.1/12.2").
Low persona yield, but restates a couple of recurring taste notes (the **no-front-facing-
speakers disappointment**, the **high-pixel-density praise**) and reads the new TouchWiz as a
preview of the Galaxy S5.

## Key claims
- [2014-01-10] **Request-driven + interviewed-Samsung**: "you guys told me you wanted to see their
  new tablets… the people at Samsung I spoke with said the only difference between the Tab Pro 12.2
  and the Note Pro is the stylus." Audience-programmed + an industry-access note.
- [2014-01-10] **The no-front-facing-speakers disappointment, restated**: "unfortunately no front-
  facing speakers to be found on any of these tablets." His recurring taste gripe.
- [2014-01-10] **High-pixel-density praise — pixel-person**: "something really nice is they all
  have 2560×1600 displays, including the 8.4" Tab Pro, which I think makes it the highest pixel
  density in any mid-size tablet form factor." His display-primacy lens.
- [2014-01-10] **Credits the four-way multitasking as genuinely useful**: "the larger ones allow
  four-way multitasking — drag an app to each corner and use them all at once… with all that
  screen real estate, you can probably get some serious work done." Honest credit for a Samsung
  feature.
- [2014-01-10] **Reads it as a KitKat-TouchWiz preview of the S5**: "these are the first devices
  running KitKat with TouchWiz on top, so we'll likely see a lot of these enhancements in the
  Galaxy S5 this year." His read-the-signal-for-what's-coming instinct.
