# Samsung Galaxy Note 5 Impressions! (& GS6 Edge+) (@mkbhd, 2015-08-14)

## Summary
First impressions of the Galaxy Note 5 — "big, bright, crispy, really dense, and **slippery as hell**." A
dope spec sheet (Exynos 7420, 4 GB, 5.7" QHD Super AMOLED, 16 MP OIS f/1.9, fast + wireless charging), and
a shape that improves on the Note 4 (curved-glass back, Note-4 footprint) — but his **least-favorite part
is the feel in the hand** (all the S6's slipperiness magnified in a bigger body), plus it's now **sealed
(no removable battery, no microSD)**. The improved click-out S Pen keeps it a Note.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-08-14] **Strong spec sheet.** Exynos 7420, 4 GB, a 5.7" QHD Super AMOLED "that looks pretty
  outstanding," a 16 MP OIS f/1.9 camera, fast + wireless charging, NFC, LTE-A, AC Wi-Fi, and a titanium-gray
  color. "Very complete on paper, but in practice not all perfect."
- [2015-08-14] **The shape is better, the feel is worse (his least-favorite part).** It moved to the S6's
  all-metal-and-glass thin-railed look, with **curved glass on the back** for one-handed fit, in the Note 4's
  footprint — "the shape is great." **But "it's super slippery, not really comfortable to hold — all the
  things that were slippery about the S6, magnified in an even bigger body. The feel in the hand is
  definitely my least-favorite part."** (Judge by hold, not just the spec sheet.)
- [2015-08-14] **Now sealed.** No removable battery and no expandable storage — "a pretty big deal for some
  people; I don't mind those as much, but I care a lot about how it feels in the hand."
- [2015-08-14] **Improved S Pen** — more metal/weight, and a **click-out** button-eject mechanism.
