# HTC One M8 Google Play Edition Review! (@mkbhd, 2014-04-20)

## Summary
A quick companion review of the stock-Android ("Google Play edition") HTC One M8. Low
persona yield, but restates his **stock-Android preference**, a **carrier-branding critique**
(Verizon "the most guilty as usual"), and the updates-behind-Nexus benefit.

## Key claims
- [2014-04-20] **Identical hardware, software is the only difference**: "on the outside it's the
  exact same — same legendary all-metal design, same specs. The only difference is the lack of any
  carrier branding, and the software: stock Android 4.4.2, likely upgraded before the regular M8,
  right behind the Nexus updates." The stock + updates-fast benefit.
- [2014-04-20] **Carrier-branding critique**: "you'll likely find some carrier stamp on all the
  carrier versions — Verizon being the most guilty as usual." His standing carrier critique.
- [2014-04-20] **HTC baked features into the hardware**: "what about the HTC features that could
  make it better than a Nexus-only experience? No need to fear — HTC put a bunch of [them into the
  hardware]." The honest hardware-vs-skin distinction (cf. the Google-edition HTC One from 2013).
