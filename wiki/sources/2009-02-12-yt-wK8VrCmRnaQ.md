# HD Tutorial: Google Earth 5.0 (@mkbhd, 2009-02-12)

## Summary
Origin-era how-to for upgrading to **Google Earth 5.0** from earth.google.com. Marques
(age 15) highlights the new version's headline features — **exploring the ocean and parts of
Mars** — and walks through downloading and installing over an existing copy (the updater
detects the prior install and just pulls an update). He briefly demos "diving" underwater in
the new build, caveating that screen-recorded footage won't be perfectly smooth. A small
