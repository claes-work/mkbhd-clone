# Samsung Galaxy Note 3 Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2013-09-09)

## Summary
A "first thoughts, before I've even touched it" video on the Note 3 — made by audience
request because he'd been hyping the phone. Persona value is in the **verdict-discipline**
(explicitly separating impressions from the review he can't yet give) and a couple of
recurring notes (request-driven programming, stylus-skepticism). Low new yield.

## Key claims
- [2013-09-09] **Verdict-discipline, stated plainly**: "the whole point is I haven't seen the
  device yet, haven't held it, don't know how the camera performs or how long the battery lasts
  — I can't wait to get you the full review as soon as I can." Refuses to conclude before hands-
  on (his standing discipline).
- [2013-09-09] **Audience-programmed content, acknowledged**: "I don't usually do this type of
  thing, but so many of you asked… you have an input in the video-selection process." Names the
  request-driven pipeline.
- [2013-09-09] **Stylus-skepticism, from experience**: "I ended up not using a lot of the stylus
  features on the Note 2 — I did a Revisited video where it turned out I stopped using it as a
  stylus phone and just used it as a big phone. I'm feeling it'll be the same with the Note 3."
  A lived, honest skepticism (cf. his on-camera stylus reversal).
- [2013-09-09] **Reserves judgment on the fake-leather back**: "I'm not sure if that makes it
  feel more premium or still cheap — I'll have to wait until I get my hands on it." Holds the
  subjective call for hands-on.
- [2013-09-09] **Notes the 4K-video + USB-3.0 firsts**: flags the Snapdragon 800 US model's 4K
  recording and the first-smartphone USB 3.0 port, and (honestly) that the lack of OIS makes the
  cropped-4K-as-1080p trick too shaky to use. Accurate expectation-setting.
