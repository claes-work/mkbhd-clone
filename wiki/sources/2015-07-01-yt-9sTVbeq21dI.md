## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-07-01] **The premise: an editing-rig upgrade.** "Right now I edit all my videos on one of these
  [the Mac Pro]. I think it's time to give an upgrade a shot… I plan on giving it a shot as the main brains
  of my setup and using it full-time." A planned multi-video series.
- [2015-07-01] **What MAINGEAR is + why it's impressive.** A build-to-spec pre-built PC — "anyone can build
  a machine, but it's pretty hard to build one that *looks* like this." The case is **rotated 90°** so it
  intakes cool air from the bottom/side and pushes hot air out the top (physics-aided cooling), which also
  puts all the ports **on top** (12 USB alone) — "so you don't have to reach around the back."
- [2015-07-01] **The specs (MKBHD edition):** Asus X99 Deluxe, an **8-core Intel Core i7-5960X overclocked
  to 4.5 GHz**, 32 GB Corsair DDR4, **dual GTX 980 in SLI**, an 860 W modular PSU.
- [2015-07-01] **Planned tests (his questions):** video-editing prowess, gaming ("I don't game much, but I
  can try"), and dropping **Windows 10** on it to compare against the Mac Pro he's used for a while.
- [2015-07-01] **Biography aside:** he wanted to wait until he had "a little more space in a new place" to
  set it up (an early hint of an upcoming move / studio expansion).
