# Blackberry Priv Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-11-20)

## Summary
A review of the BlackBerry Priv (an Android BlackBerry with a vertical slider keyboard) — "**my favorite
BlackBerry ever, but in the vast array of smartphones out now, pretty middle-of-the-road average**." The
standout is its **super-grippy carbon-fiber build**: "this is the phone that does not need a skin or a case
— probably the most secure-feeling phone I've ever held." Near-stock Android with BlackBerry additions
(Hub — "overwhelming"). Meh battery, a good-but-not-best display.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-11-20] **Favorite BlackBerry, average phone.** "I said before it would take a lot for me to review
  a BlackBerry, but this one's actually not bad — my favorite BlackBerry ever, but pretty middle-of-the-road
  in the wider market."
- [2015-11-20] **Grippy = no case/skin needed.** The carbon-fiber back + grippy side rails mean "this is the
  phone that does not need a skin or a case — it's never slid out of my pocket once, never off a desk;
  probably the most secure-feeling phone I've ever held." (No-cases satisfied by grip.) One gripe: the thin
  back cover feels soft and flexes ("since it's a sealed phone, use whatever material you want").
- [2015-11-20] **The slider works.** He got used to the one-handed slide "in a matter of minutes"; it feels
  more balanced open (weight spread out, not top-heavy); solid spring mechanism.
- [2015-11-20] **Specs mid-to-high-end, refreshing in a BlackBerry.** Snapdragon 808, 2 GB, 32 GB +
  microSD, a 3410 mAh battery (wireless + quick charging), a 5.4" QHD AMOLED (curved edges — "not the best
  QHD AMOLED I've seen, could be brighter/more saturated, but a quality panel"). Battery "meh, B-minus"
  (~3 hr screen-on). A weak but front-facing speaker.
- [2015-11-20] **Software: near-stock + BlackBerry extras.** Android 5.1, "vanilla Android with BlackBerry
  enhancements, kind of like what Motorola does with the Moto X." BlackBerry Hub "is sort of overwhelming"
  (Google Now searches the device fine anyway).
