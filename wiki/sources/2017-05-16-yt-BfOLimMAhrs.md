# The Dell 8K Monitor: Gaming in 8K? (@mkbhd, 2017-05-16)

## Summary
A look at Dell's UP3218K — the first 8K monitor you can actually buy — which finally lets him see his own 8K
footage at native resolution. The clone-relevant threads: the recurring **"a YouTube video can't do it
justice" pipeline problem**, the pro-editing use case (a native 4K editing window pixel-for-pixel), and a
bit of overkill 8K gaming.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-05-16] **He's never seen his own 8K files at native res until now.** "People aren't going to watch
  my 8K State-of-4K video in 8K because nobody has an 8K monitor — even *I* had never seen my own 8K video
  files in 8K until this." A concrete bleeding-edge-ownership beat.
- [2017-05-16] **The pipeline problem, restated.** "It's so hard to do this justice through a YouTube video —
  it goes through my camera, downscaling, my editing, YouTube, your internet, and your screen. The best I can
  do is describe it." His recurring you-can't-show-the-bleeding-edge-through-the-medium caveat.
- [2017-05-16] **The real use case: pro photo/video editing.** "The most frequent use I'd imagine is photo
  editing / Photoshop — seeing that much more detail — or video editing: a **native full-4K window inside
  your editor, pixel-for-pixel**, something you've never had before. If you do that work, you'll appreciate
  not just the pixels but the corner-to-corner color accuracy and even lighting." Grades it by who it's for.
- [2017-05-16] **Honest about the panel's one weakness.** "It's not the brightest — understandably so at
  this resolution — but it is *so* sharp; 8K files look almost like looking through a window, but not quite."
  Names the tradeoff.
- [2017-05-16] **8K gaming = glorious overkill.** "Dirt Rally at 7680×4320, every setting on ultra, on a
  Titan XP that crushes it — it feels like watching a really good video, unrealistic in a weird way; I'm just
  admiring the tiny details. This obviously isn't how normal people play." A knowingly-excessive demo.

## Notable verbatim quotes
