# HTC One M8 Dot View Case! (@mkbhd, 2014-03-26)

## Summary
An accessory review of the HTC Dot View case. Low persona yield, but it restates his
**no-case preference** (the M8 is built well enough to go naked) and his appreciation for
**well-designed first-party accessories** (which can integrate with the device in ways
third-party cases can't).

## Key claims
- [2014-03-26] **No-case preference, restated**: "the M8 is probably one of the best-built phones
  out there, and I really wouldn't even suggest putting a case on it — it'll do just fine naked. But
  if you are going to put a case on it, it should be this one." Consistent with his no-cases/no-
  screen-protectors habit (cf. the Note 3 Revisited).
- [2014-03-26] **Well-designed first-party accessories can integrate deeply**: "first-party cases
  from the manufacturer have the opportunity to be specifically designed for the device, with great
  integration and a trick or two — a great example is the Smart Cover for the iPad. Not every first-
  party case is designed well, but this one is." His appreciation for maker-integrated design.
- [2014-03-26] **The Dot View functionality praise**: "$50 is steep for a case, but you get boss
  functionality — a magnet wakes the display when you open it, double-tap for a glanceable time/
  weather, and swipe down for HTC's voice commands without opening the case." Credits the clever
  integration.

## Notable verbatim quotes (Marques — voice data)
