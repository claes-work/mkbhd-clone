# NEW YouTube Video Watch Page [November 2010] (@mkbhd, 2010-11-04)

## Summary
A screencast commentary on a YouTube watch-page layout update: related videos moved
fully to the right column, like/dislike and share/embed controls consolidated
underneath the video on the left, and expanding elements (description, stats) no
longer shift the related-videos column. Praised as an unambiguous improvement.
