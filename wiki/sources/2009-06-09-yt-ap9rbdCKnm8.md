# Unboxed: Wrapsol Shield for LG Voyager (@mkbhd, 2009-06-09)

## Summary
An unboxing of a Wrapsol protective film for his LG Voyager, sent by the company. Three
things worth keeping. (1) He **justifies the existence of the unboxing video** rather
than defaulting to one: "the only reason I'm doing an unboxing video is because [they
don't] just give you one Invisible Shield, they actually give you a lot of stuff" — the
earliest explicit statement that an unboxing needs a reason to exist. (2) He **catches a
spec error in the manufacturer's own packaging and website** (the phone is the VX10000,
not the "VX1000") and says he will email the company about it — the correct-the-spec
instinct already visible in the iPhone-OS commentary of the origin era. (3) He **states a
purchasing value directly**: "I appreciate companies that do include a lot in their
packages." He also explicitly separates unboxing from verdict, promising a full review
once installed, and self-deprecates about the preceding GelaSkins installation video.
★ L3-candidate.

## Key claims
- [2009-06-09] **Unboxings need a justification**: "the only reason I'm doing an unboxing
  video is because Wrapsol doesn't just give you one Invisible Shield, they actually give
  you a lot of stuff with the Invisible Shield."
- [2009-06-09] **Spec correction against the manufacturer**: "on both on their website and
  on this package here it says VX1000... I might email Wrapsol about that, because it is
  in fact the VX10000." (Marques is right: the LG Voyager is the VX10000.)
- [2009-06-09] **Packaging generosity treated as a product attribute**: "I appreciate
  companies that do include a lot in their packages" — the box contents (cleaning
  solution, application instructions, microfiber towel, squeegee, recyclable box) are
  itemized as value, not filler.
- [2009-06-09] **Review sample disclosed on air**: "thanks to Wrapsol for sending this out
  to me" — consistent with the sample-disclosure habit already dated to
  [[2009-05-30-yt-3qbUOlftiX8]].
- [2009-06-09] **Unbox and verdict kept separate**: "I would definitely get to you guys
  with a full review video... after I've installed the Wrapsol" — the unbox→review
  two-step continues; he also states there will deliberately be **no** installation video
  this time.
- [2009-06-09] **Self-critical about his own previous video**: "you might be watching this
  after the GelaSkins installation video and obviously that wasn't the most exciting video
  in the world" — an early, unprompted admission that a video of his underperformed as
  entertainment. (The GelaSkins installation/review is [[2009-06-13-yt-ClRH1aQuP_Q]],
  published four days later; the unboxing is [[2009-05-04-yt-6l3RGnwYAiU]] — so the
  remark refers to the not-yet-published or freshly-shot installation piece.)
  > ⚠️ CONTRADICTION (minor, sequencing): this video (2009-06-09) refers back to a
  > GelaSkins installation video that appears on the channel on 2009-06-13. Either the
  > publication order differs from the shooting order, or he is referring to the
  > installation footage he had already recorded. Not resolvable from the transcripts.
- [2009-06-09] **Shipping friction narrated**: FedEx "tried to ship it to a PO box but
  they couldn't... so it was redirected to my house" — the PO-box arrangement first noted
  in [[2009-04-10-yt-uJM_vj9RW7E]] fails again.
- [2009-06-09] **Practical read of the accessories**: the squeegee "reminds me of any
  other squeegee — you'd use it to get out your air bubbles as you're applying the film,"
  and of the microfiber, "I have about six microfibers now."
