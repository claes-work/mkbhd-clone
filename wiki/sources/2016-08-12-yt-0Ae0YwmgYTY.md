# The iPhone 7 Plus Model! (@mkbhd, 2016-08-12)

## Summary
A hands-on with a pre-release iPhone 7 Plus **dummy unit** (built from leaks) — the counterpart to the
Nexus-hype video. Three visible changes he flags (antenna lines, the missing headphone jack, dual
cameras), plus two forward-looking wishes that later prove prescient: **stereo speakers** and an **OLED
display** for the iPhone.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-08-12] **The three visible changes.** "There are three main features visibly new about the
  iPhone 7/7 Plus from this body: the antenna lines (now just top and bottom, nothing across the back —
  looks cleaner), the headphone jack (or lack thereof), and the dual cameras." Reading a phone from its
  industrial design.
- [2016-08-12] **Hopeful on stereo speakers.** "You get two speaker grilles now — I'm going to be
  optimistic and hope that means stereo speakers like the iPads. It's very possible it's just a second
  grille for a single speaker, but please give us stereo audio." (The 7 does add stereo — a good call.)
- [2016-08-12] **The OLED wish (prescient).** "I'm going out on a limb — I'm just hoping something we
  haven't seen in the mockups: a new display. They could double the resolution and finally incorporate
  an OLED display in the iPhone 7/7 Plus. It's about time we give the bigger iPhone a much nicer screen."
  (The iPhone doesn't go OLED until the iPhone X in 2017 — a forecast that lands a year early.)
- [2016-08-12] **Dual cameras — "we know it has two, we don't know what they'll do."** Points back to his
  Dual Camera Explained video ([[2016-05-04-yt-XVL0ybe18yI]]): could be switchable wide+normal, or two
  identical combined for detail — "we don't know what Apple's doing yet." Honest about the limits of leak
  analysis.
- [2016-08-12] **Reads the SMART-connector rumor.** Notes this dummy lacks the smart-connector pins seen
  on other mockups, tying it to the rumored "iPhone 7 Pro" (iPad-Pro-style accessory connector) vs. a
  plain 7 Plus. Otherwise "pretty similar design to the 6/6s — same shape, thickness, buttons, bezels."

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "I'm going to be optimistic and hope that that means stereo speakers like the iPads now… please give
> us stereo audio."
> "I feel like they could also finally incorporate an OLED display in the iPhone 7 and 7 Plus… it's
> about time we give the bigger iPhone a much nicer screen."
