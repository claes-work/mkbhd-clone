# iPad Pro Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2015-09-10)

## Summary
First impressions of the 12.9" iPad Pro — "an absolutely massive tablet, the biggest traditional tablet
out there." A huge 2732×2048 display (more pixels than the 13" MacBook Pro), four (non-front-facing)
speakers, a beastly A9X (demoed editing three 4K streams), plus the new Smart Connector accessories — a
Surface-like **Smart Keyboard** and the **Apple Pencil** (which he frames with a Steve-Jobs-callback
joke — "nobody wants a stylus… well"). Also his recurring RAM-transparency gripe.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-09-10] **Massive, with a gorgeous display.** 12.9" 2732×2048 — "more pixels than the 13" Retina
  MacBook Pro… everything looks great." Four corner speakers ("still not front-facing, but a bigger
  soundstage"), ~10 hr battery.
- [2015-09-10] **A9X beast + a RAM gripe.** The new 64-bit A9X "will probably benchmark off the charge"
  (demoed **three streams of 4K video** smoothly, iOS 9 multitasking). "I don't know how much RAM is in
  this tablet — I hope it's more than 2 GB at this point" (his recurring spec-transparency complaint).
- [2015-09-10] **Smart Connector + Smart Keyboard.** The iPad Pro's new magnetic **Smart Connector**
  powers/pairs a folding **Smart Keyboard** ("reminiscent of a certain other fold-out keyboard case by a
  certain other manufacturer — the Surface Pro"), using the new-MacBook short-throw keys, no backlight/
  battery, $169.
- [2015-09-10] **The Apple Pencil — Jobs-callback humor.** "Who wants a stylus? You have to get them and
  put them away and you lose them. Yuck, nobody wants a stylus, so let's not use a stylus… well" — a
  self-aware nod to Steve Jobs's old anti-stylus line.
