# HD Tutorial: Change Rocket Dock Icons (@mkbhd, 2009-02-06)

## Summary
Origin-era customization screencast. Marques (age 15) shows how to change and rename the
icons on RocketDock (the macOS-style dock for Windows he runs on his Vista machine). He
downloads icon packs from rocketdock.com's "get add-ons" page, extracts them to a
"program documents" folder inside his documents, then right-clicks a dock item → "icon
settings" → "+" to point at the new image, and renames the item. He demonstrates both a
multi-icon pack (CamStudio) and a single icon (Internet Explorer). Notes the add-ons
site has 48 pages of icons.
