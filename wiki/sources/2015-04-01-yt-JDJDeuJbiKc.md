# Logitech MX Master Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-04-01)

## Summary
A review of the Logitech MX Master mouse — a **production/gear** datum. As "a student and someone who edits
a lot of video," Marques spends all day at the mouse; the MX Master (successor to the Performance MX he
loved) wins him over with **big-hands ergonomics**, a **horizontal side-scroll wheel he uses constantly for
his Premiere/After Effects timeline**, and multi-device Bluetooth switching. Includes a signature voice tic
(the Magic Mouse is "like the spawn of Satan") and a nod to his USB-C thesis (surprised it isn't USB-C).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-04-01] **Big-hands ergonomics.** A big arching spine puts a large hand in "a more ergonomic resting
  position for better long-term comfort" — "which is why the Magic Mouse is like the spawn of Satan" (a
  callback to his standing Magic-Mouse dismissal).
- [2015-04-01] **Horizontal scroll = an editing superpower.** The side-scroll wheel is back from the MX
  Revolution — "you don't know how useful horizontal scrolling is until you don't have it… I use it most
  while video editing, moving through my timeline in Premiere Pro and After Effects. Horizontal scrolling is
  **dope**."
- [2015-04-01] **Multi-device switching + good software.** Built-in Bluetooth 4.0 (connected to his Mac Pro
  in ~5 s) and a three-device memory button (desktop / laptop / third) — "really useful if you have two
  computers side by side." The Logitech Options software (gesture pad, smart-scroll toggle, battery
  indicator) is "actually pretty good," which "most mouse software is not."
- [2015-04-01] **Nitpicks + a USB-C nod.** The back/forward buttons sit a bit too far back; the battery is
  now sealed (was removable) but lasts weeks — "I'm kind of surprised it's not USB type-C" (his USB-C-is-the-
  future thesis). Worth the $99; "my mouse game is strong again."
