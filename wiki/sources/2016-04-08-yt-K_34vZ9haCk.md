# MKBHD Update 13.0: The Studio! (@mkbhd, 2016-04-08)

## Summary
A vlog-style channel-update: he finally has **The Studio** — the first dedicated, separate-from-home
video space of his career, after ~5 years shooting every video in his apartment. Rich biography +
production material: the failed home purchase (banks and the self-employed), the comfort-zone
motivation, the studio's echo/audio problem as his "number one challenge," and the invitation for NYC-
area YouTubers to collab there. Confirms his daily driver is the S7 Edge.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-04-08] **First dedicated studio, separate from home.** "This is a dedicated video space, and
  for the first time in my life it's going to be in a separate room from where I live and sleep." He is
  *not* living there. The end of the ~5-year apartment era: "I've shot videos in there for the past 5
  years — you've seen every possible angle of that apartment." (Biography milestone.)
- [2016-04-08] **The failed home purchase — self-employment friction.** "I found a place, I bought a
  place — or at least I thought I did — and then that fell through because of the way banks handle the
  person that's self-employed." A concrete data point on the financial reality of being a full-time
  creator in 2016.
- [2016-04-08] **The comfort-zone motivation.** "I've read a bit about your comfort zone recently and
  stepping outside it… if anything is my comfort zone, it's shooting videos in my apartment." Started
  shooting around town / renting studio space out of boredom before finding this place. Explicit
  growth/creative-variety framing for the whole move.
- [2016-04-08] **Audio, not video, is the challenge.** "My number one challenge is making this place
  audio-ready — a good solid 1.5–2 second echo; it used to be I'd snap and not stop hearing it for a
  solid 6 seconds." Foam in the ceiling already helps; couches, desks, carpet, furniture will dampen it
  over time. (A production-craft node: room acoustics.)
- [2016-04-08] **What the space unlocks — collabs + variety.** "I can host things now, bring people in
  to work alongside me for a project or long-term (wink wink)… if you're a YouTuber in the NYC area,
  you're invited." Goal: "make better videos… a better space, will look better, eventually sound
  better, allow more variety of the type of stuff that comes out of this channel." Notes most
  behind-the-scenes setup has been going on **Snapchat and Twitter**.
- [2016-04-08] **Daily driver confirmed.** "My daily driver right now is a Galaxy S7 Edge, titanium
  silver — although that might change in the next couple days because of the amount of phones coming
  out right now." (Personal-device data point.)
