# YouTube Gear #1 | Blue Yeti! (@mkbhd, 2015-11-26)

## Summary
The debut of a new franchise, **"YouTube Gear"** — creator-focused gear recommendations answering "I want
to start making videos, what gear should I get?" His framing is a canonical creator principle: **"it's not
all about the gear — content first, the stuff you make videos about is what matters — but having quality,
versatile, working gear is surprisingly important."** The first pick is the **Blue Yeti** mic — which he
notes he **used full-time in his own origin (3–5 years ago)**, a biography/production datum.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-11-26] **The franchise premise + content-first principle.** "YouTube Gear — awesome gear for
  YouTube… one of the most common questions I get is 'I want to start making videos, what gear?' **It's not
  all about the gear — content first, the stuff you make videos about is really what matters, and that's the
  whole reason to start a channel — but quality, versatile, working gear is surprisingly important.**"
- [2015-11-26] **The pick: the Blue Yeti (Pro).** A USB desktop mic with three capsules and switchable
  patterns (stereo / omnidirectional / cardioid / bidirectional — cardioid cancels behind the mic for solo
  voice; bidirectional for a two-person interview), a physical mute switch, and a headphone jack for
  latency-free monitoring. Around $100 on Black Friday.
- [2015-11-26] **Origin/biography note.** "This is a YouTube classic — I was using this microphone full-
  time three, four, even five years ago; it showed up in my videos, I think I made a whole video about it (a
  silver version). It's tried and true, and it's about time I recommended it." (Ties to his 2010-era gear.)
- [2015-11-26] **Production note:** he plugs it into a **Surface Book** in the demo (a dated hint about his
  Windows/laptop use).
