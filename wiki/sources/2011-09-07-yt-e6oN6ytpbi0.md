# Top Reasons OLED Rocks! (@mkbhd, 2011-09-07)

## Summary
A technology explainer (not a product review) on why OLED display tech is
superior, structured as a "how it works, then three reasons" format:
per-pixel illumination (vs. a single shared backlight) explained first,
then three concrete benefits derived from it — energy savings/better
battery life, true/deeper blacks and higher contrast because unlit pixels
are fully off, and thinner device design since no backlight layer is
needed. Cites the Galaxy S2, Nexus S, and other OLED-equipped devices as
examples, and closes with an explicit industry-directed opinion: "I propose
every mobile manufacturer switch over to OLED displays as soon as
possible."
