# Oppo Find 7 Rant! (@mkbhd, 2013-12-19)

## Summary
A rant defending Oppo's teased 2K-display Find 7 against the "why would you want more than
1080p?" backlash — and a strong Oppo-arc node. He counters the skepticism by pointing to the
**exact same reaction to 1080p in his Sept-2012 Oppo video**, which was proven wrong, and
articulates the **resolution-is-more-than-visible-pixels** principle. Champions Oppo as the
risk-taking "icebreaker."

## Key claims
- [2013-12-19] **His own 2012 prediction validated — the self-referential proof**: "rewind to my
  September 2012 'Super Retina Smartphone' video about Oppo preparing a 1080p phone — the comments
  then are exactly like the reactions to Oppo's 2K tease now: 'why would I want that, it'll wreck
  performance and battery.' And at first they were right… but slowly the big guns came (HTC One,
  S4, Xperia Z, Nexus 5) and 1080p became the standard." Uses his own validated call to rebut the
  skeptics.
- [2013-12-19] **Resolution is more than visible pixels — the principle**: "it's like the
  megapixel count now — way more than just pixels. Sit 10 feet from a 1080p 50" TV and a 4K 50"
  TV; you can't see the pixels on either, but 10 times out of 10 you'll say the 4K looks
  immensely better. The same philosophy applies to phones." His higher-resolution-is-visibly-
  better-even-past-the-pixel-threshold argument (cf. the 4K-Explained thesis).
- [2013-12-19] **Champions Oppo as the risk-taking icebreaker — the underdog admiration**: "only
  Oppo would tweet the next-gen specs of an unreleased device… I'm glad to see Oppo pushing the
  limits yet again. They're willing to be the icebreaker, dive in head-first, push the boundary —
  I admire that. Setting standards is not easy." His champion-innovative-outsiders value, applied
  to the Oppo long-arc.
- [2013-12-19] **Predicts the followers**: "I can see the same trend happening — the big players
  get into 2K (Galaxy S5, Note 4, HTC 2, Nexus 6), and then a Motorola phone ships 1080p and
  everyone scratches their heads 'why only 1080p?'" (Largely borne out.) His adoption-curve
  prediction.
- [2013-12-19] **"Don't complain in the infancy" — the growing-pains stance**: "there's always
  naysayers in the infancy of any new technology — growing pains. Don't complain unless it's
  actually hurting you [if you buy a 2K phone and the battery sucks, then please complain, that
  makes the tech better]. Let Oppo take the bold steps." His pro-innovation, let-it-mature stance.
- [2013-12-19] **Honest about the format — a rant**: "I'm probably just rambling, I wrote this
  really quickly and wanted to tell you guys." His transparent, off-the-cuff register.
