# Samsung Galaxy S7 & S7 Edge Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2016-02-22)

## Summary
First impressions of the Galaxy S7 / S7 Edge — "a slightly refined, better-polished S6" (a "**Galaxy S6s**,"
like the iPhone 6s was for the 6). The moment that matters: his **anti-thin / fill-with-battery plea is
VINDICATED** — "**can we all just take a second to appreciate that Samsung actually just made their new
phone 1 mm thicker to accommodate a larger battery? Thank you, Samsung, I haven't been preaching to a brick
wall.**" Samsung fixed the S6's worst parts: added IP68 water resistance (no bulk), a bigger battery, a
reduced camera bump, and the curved-glass back.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-02-22] **Anti-thin plea vindicated (the headline).** "Can we all just take a second to appreciate
  that Samsung actually just made their new phone **1 mm thicker to accommodate a larger battery**? Thank
  you Samsung — I haven't been preaching to a brick wall; it is much appreciated." (His "fill the body with
  battery" campaign paying off.)
- [2016-02-22] **A "Galaxy S6s" — fixed the S6's worst parts.** "Samsung took what was already a pretty good
  phone [the S6, one of the best of 2015] and fixed most of the worst parts about it." Now **fully IP68 water
  resistant** with no added bulk/flaps, the **camera bump greatly reduced** (less of a table-rock), a flatter
  home button, and a **curved-glass back** (like the Note 5) for easier holding. Four colors (finally an
  actually-black one).
- [2016-02-22] **The strengths return.** The best-display crown (bright QHD Super AMOLED) and one of the
  best cameras (rumored 25 MP IMX300) — "kind of like the iPhone 6s was for the iPhone 6." Two phones only
  this year (5.1" S7, 5.5" S7 Edge — no confusing Edge+).
- [2016-02-22] **Aside:** the S6/S7 got "upstaged" by the LG G5 announcement the same week.
