# Tesla Model S P100D Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2016-09-23)

## Summary
A landmark biography node: **Marques takes delivery of his own Tesla Model S P100D** — which he names
**"Apollo"** — reportedly the first P100D on the US East Coast. He frames it as the start of an evolving
video *playlist* and a dual test bed (each entry shot on a different phone's 4K camera; this one on the
iPhone 7 Plus). The clearest statement yet of his car-vs-tech identity, and the ownership root of what
becomes the Auto Focus channel.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-09-23] **He bought a Tesla Model S P100D — "Apollo."** "This is Apollo, at least what I've named
  her — a Tesla Model S P100D. I just took delivery; Tesla told me this is the first P100D on the East
  Coast." A major personal-ownership + biography milestone. (The ancestor of the whole car beat →
  [[../topics/ev-cars/ev-cars]].)
- [2016-09-23] **"I'm a tech guy before I'm a car guy — but this is as high-tech as it gets."** His
  explicit self-positioning: he reviews the car "the same way I talk about smartphones," treating it as
  "a piece of tech." The founding frame of reviewing-a-car-like-a-gadget.
- [2016-09-23] **Why he picked it: the frunk + storage for video gear.** "A big part of picking this
  vehicle over pretty much any other is that it's huge for carrying equipment — video equipment is big."
  A production-driven purchase rationale (form factor serves the channel).
- [2016-09-23] **Decodes the P100D name (teach-the-mechanism).** "P = performance, 100 = the 100 kWh
  battery, D = dual motors, and the little underline signifies it's part of the Ludicrous-mode fleet."
  Explains the electric-drivetrain layout (no engine; motors between the wheels + a floor battery).
- [2016-09-23] **"The fastest production car on planet Earth right now."** "0–60 in exactly 2.5 seconds
  — that technically makes this the fastest production car on Earth; the Bugatti Veyron and Porsche 918
  Spyder aren't really made anymore, so if you're not in a Bugatti, you're getting beaten off the line."
- [2016-09-23] **The dual-purpose-video idea.** Shoots it "live style on the iPhone 7 Plus" to test 4K/
  stabilization while showing the car — "an evolving playlist… lets me test 4K cameras on new phones and
  show new car features as software updates roll out." A characteristic format innovation.
