# iPhone X vs iPhone 8? Ask MKBHD V20! (@mkbhd, 2017-09-26)

## Summary
Monthly Q&A #20. Biography-rich: the **Team Crispy origin** (first met Austin Evans at a Galaxy S3 launch),
his **first video's ~12 views**, a sharp read on **why Apple shipped Face ID** (couldn't get Touch-ID-under-

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-09-26] **iPhone 8 vs X — "I'm going to wait for the X."** "About half of ~2,000 questions were iPhone
  8 vs 10. I actually really like the 8 — people underrate its power and cameras. But if I'm buying, I want
  the best iPhone, and the X seems like that." His own daily-driver logic.
- [2017-09-26] **BIOGRAPHY — the Team Crispy origin.** "Most of us first met online (doesn't really count).
  The first time I met **Austin Evans** in person was a Samsung Galaxy S3 launch event in NYC — we walked into
  a restaurant, 'oh hey, you're that other YouTuber' — a lot of us meet at these tech events (Samsung/LG),
  which host tech press and YouTubers." How the creator circle formed.
- [2017-09-26] **BIOGRAPHY — his first video got ~12 views in 24 hours.** "Probably like 12 — if I put the
  over/under at 15, I'd take the under." A humble origin data point.
- [2017-09-26] **Why Apple shipped Face ID (a sharp read).** "Apple surely *wanted* Touch ID under the glass
  in the same familiar spot, but they didn't get the tech in time — so they worked really hard on the front
  sensors to make Face ID as good a replacement as possible, and as far as we know it's actually better (more
  reliable, lower failure rate, harder to trick). But what if next year they get Touch-ID-under-glass in the
  iPhone 11 — do we go back, or keep both? That's literally a question I'd want to ask Apple." Reads the
  hardware constraint behind the design.
- [2017-09-26] **Porsche Mission E as a Tesla rival.** "Ideally it's a rival — same price point as the Model
  S. But Tesla's so far ahead (Superchargers, clout, mind-share) it's hard to catch. Porsche does have clout
  and **really nice interiors** — attacking Tesla's weak spot." His Tesla-interior-is-the-weak-spot read again.
- [2017-09-26] **Tesla-owner details.** A **PSA to NY-area Model 3 owners** willing to let him shoot their car.
  On **wireless car charging**: "not efficient — phones get warm and charge slower on a pad from heat loss;
  charging a car fast already runs hot (Supercharging on a 95°F road trip, the plug read 109°, hot to the
  touch) — transmitting energy wirelessly near a car isn't a great idea; maybe in 20 years." A physics read.
- [2017-09-26] **Personal + the naming gripe.** Would do Hot Ones with Sean Evans. Keeps saying "iPhone X"
  (Apple employees actively corrected people to say "ten"). The **space-gray-is-always-a-different-color**
  gripe ("Apple invented their own color name and made them all different — it ruins the name"). Reachability
  is gone on the X (no home button). Got a MAINGEAR PC (a possible PUBG stream with Sam Sheffer; "I'm best at
  driving and sports-sim games").
