# Razer Phone Impressions! 120Hz! (@mkbhd, 2017-11-01)

## Summary
First impressions of the Razer Phone — a phone built entirely around gaming (Razer bought Nextbit). The
clone-relevant beats: the first **120Hz phone display** (the ProMotion he loved on the iPad, now on a phone),
the argument for **big bezels *on purpose*** (gaming grip + huge front speakers), and the matte-black stealthy
look he favors — plus "a year where anyone can make a phone."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-11-01] **"If RED can make a phone, anyone can" — the year of new phone-makers.** "Razer, a gaming
  company, bought Nextbit — but hey, if RED can make a phone, pretty much anyone can. It's been a really
  interesting year for new smartphones and new companies." A read on the 2017 influx of unexpected OEMs.
- [2017-11-01] **The 120Hz display — the headline (ProMotion arrives on a phone).** "If I know anything about
  gaming, it's that we want the highest frame rate possible at all times, and Razer knows [that]." The first
  120Hz phone screen — the buttery-smooth ProMotion experience he raved about on the iPad Pro
  ([[2017-06-16-yt-3Dl8wLo1W6E]]), now in a phone (he'd hoped it would come to phones).
- [2017-11-01] **Big bezels *on purpose* — a considered design choice, not a miss.** "You may ask why a 2017
  phone doesn't have edge-to-edge bezels — this phone has big bezels **on purpose**: gaming with an
  edge-to-edge display is hard (nowhere to rest your thumbs), so they give you **massive front-facing stereo
  speakers** flanking the display — a place for your thumbs, and they sound absolutely incredible (loud, full,
  crisp, little distortion) with Dolby Atmos EQ." Reads a counter-trend choice as intentional and justified —
  and gets the front-speakers he's always wanted.
- [2017-11-01] **The gaming build also makes for great everyday use.** "All the hardware/spec/design decisions
  were made for gaming, but a lot of those things also make for pretty great everyday use." Plus the Razer
  matte-black stealthy finish ("I kind of like the stealthy look… fingerprint-resistant, blends in") — his
  matte-black taste. The one downside: the cheap-feeling plastic volume buttons (leftover from the Nextbit
  Robin).

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "If RED can make a smartphone, then pretty much anyone else can too."
> "This phone has big bezels on purpose… gaming with an edge-to-edge display can actually be kind of tough —
> there's not a whole lot to rest your thumbs on."
