# 5 Facts about the LG V10! (@mkbhd, 2015-12-06)

## Summary
A "5 facts" on the LG V10, framed as **underdog advocacy**: "one of the most underrated smartphones of
2015." He argues LG gets unfairly left off best-of lists — despite the **G4 and V10 being LG's two best
phones ever** — while the roundups fill up with Samsung and Apple. The V10 is a slightly-improved G4 with a
few key extras, headlined by a **second display** (a top-bezel always-on ticker) that's "some gimmicky,
some genuinely useful."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-12-06] **Underdog advocacy — the underrated phone.** "This is low-key one of the most underrated
  smartphones of 2015. The roundups inevitably have a lot of Samsung and Apple, deservingly, some Motorola,
  Huawei via the Nexus 6P — but for whatever reason LG gets left off a lot, even though **their two best
  phones ever came out this year, the G4 and this V10.** Hopefully this video can change that." (Champion
  the under-covered.)
- [2015-12-06] **A slightly-improved G4.** "An all-around high-end flagship, but a slightly improved version
  of the G4, with a few key improvements beyond just the specs — what made it worth another release."
- [2015-12-06] **The second display.** A 160×1040 strip in the top-right bezel, always-on (date/time/battery
  even when the main screen is off; swipe for quick shortcuts); when the main display is on it shows
  notifications, app shortcuts, recent apps, media controls, a signature message. "Some is gimmicky, some
  actually kind of useful."
