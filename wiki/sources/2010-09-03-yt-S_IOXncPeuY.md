# Cyber Acoustics 5.1 High Performance Speakers Review (@mkbhd, 2010-09-03)

## Summary
A straight product review of the Cyber Acoustics CA-542 6-piece 5.1 surround speaker
system (~$60), covering the five identical satellite speakers, the control pod
(mute/headphone-out/aux-in/bass and master volume), the subwoofer's build and output,
and the included MP3 cradle/cable used to test highs/mids/lows. Closes with a numeric
rating and an explicit reason he can't demo audio directly on the video.

## Key claims
- [2010-09-03] **Numeric score with a stated caveat**: "if I had to give them a rating
  from 1 to 10 I would give them a 7 and a 2" (i.e., 7.2/10) — reasoning given is
  frequency response typical of subwoofer-based systems (accentuated highs/lows, "not
  going to get the best mids"), not a flaw specific to this unit.
- [2010-09-03] **Names a YouTube-platform constraint on review methodology**: declines
  to play music on camera to demonstrate sound quality, citing "YouTube's compression,
  my video editor, and YouTube's copyright finding" — an early instance of the platform
  itself limiting what a review can show.
- [2010-09-03] **Form factor named as the standout feature**: "the form factor is
  definitely my favorite thing about these speakers" — thin satellites that can sit
  behind/beside a monitor out of sight.
- [2010-09-03] **Placement guidance as practical advice**: recommends placing the
  subwoofer against a wall regardless of floor vs. elevated placement, to make the bass
  more perceptible.
