# MKBHD Setup Tour 2014! (@mkbhd, 2014-05-31)

## Summary
The **post-final-exams 2014 studio/gear tour** — a landmark inventory of the room "most videos really
are shot in and dreamed up." This is the video that documents his **jump to the RED Scarlet (4K)** as
the main camera (the reason recent videos "have been in 4K"), the full lens/audio/lighting/edit rig,
and the **trash-can Mac Pro that "runs the show."** It also fixes the biography in time: he's a
**college student** shooting from an apartment, has a **100,000-subscriber silver plaque** (and notes
the million-plaque is "more than a year late"), and has a new logo on his business cards. High-value
for `production-filmmaking` and `biography`.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2014-05-31] **Main camera is now the RED Scarlet (4K)** with the **Sigma 18–35 mm f/1.8** — "the
  past couple of videos you may have noticed have been in 4K… that's thanks to this guy." He's candid
  it's new to him: "there's a lot that I don't know about this camera already, but I'm loving shooting
  with it." Powered by an Anton Bauer battery backpack.
- [2014-05-31] **The wider camera kit:** Canon C100 and Panasonic GH4 (both used more lately), Sony
  RX100 (the one filming this tour, so it hunts focus), Fuji X100s ("a really quietly awesome camera"),
  Canon 5D (batteries on the charging station). Lenses: Sigma 18–35 f/1.8 (one declicked/rehoused as a
  cine lens with red gaffer tape "because I like red"), Canon 17–55 f/2.8 IS, Tokina 11–16 wide (used in
  the iPhone 5s review and the TV video), Canon 70–200 f/2.8 telephoto for events.
- [2014-05-31] **Support + motion gear:** Manfrotto monopod (self-standing), Manfrotto carbon-fiber
  tripod w/ a 504HD head, an **Edelkrone/"EY footage" shark slider** (counterbalanced, carbon rails) —
  "one of my favorite pieces of gear" — and a high-hat tripod for low shots.
- [2014-05-31] **Audio + edit rig:** Sennheiser **MKH416** shotgun (sits just under the frame) → XLR →
  **Focusrite Scarlet 2i2** preamp or a Zoom H4n; **iKey Audio M505-V2** studio monitors ("I'm a big
  fan of high-quality audio"); the **Mac Pro** (on OS X 10.9.2 — held back from 10.9.3 because it "did
  not play nice with the displays") drives twin **Asus PQ321Q 4K displays** (3840×2160) on wooden
  TV stands.
- [2014-05-31] **Lighting is Philips Hue** (controlled from the iPad Hue app — a red/blue scene, a
  bright-white "video" scene) plus a four-bank of fluorescents.
- [2014-05-31] **Personal/daily devices:** 13" Retina MacBook Pro (with a black decal since day one),
  **Nexus 10** as daily tablet ("I happen to really really want the next version… to come out soon"),
  128 GB iPad mini Retina at the desk, and he carries a couple of phones at a time — currently the
  **GPe HTC One M8 and a black iPhone 5s**; a Nexus 5 wireless charger is nearby.
- [2014-05-31] **Biography anchors:** he's a **student** (backpack "I happen to use every day 'cause
  I'm a student," textbooks cleaned off the shelf after finals, still wearing the No Sleep bracelet from
  the Setup Tour Project shot before exams); a **100k silver play button plaque** ("a couple of months
  ago"), with the **million-subscriber plaque "more than a year late"**; new-logo business cards; and
  Justin Maller facet artwork (the Daft Punk *RAM* logo) on the wall.
- [2014-05-31] **Self-description:** "I tend to say I'm kind of a minimal person, but definitely not
  the most minimal" (the desk isn't the cleanest); imperfect cable management with Bluelounge boxes and
  gaffer tape; "I surprisingly don't game very much."
