# The Razer Blade - Preview (@mkbhd, 2011-08-27)

## Summary
A preview/reaction (not a full review — no review unit yet) to the newly
unveiled Razer Blade, explicitly framed as a follow-up to the prior "Fat
Laptops" rant three days earlier: "it looks like the folks over at Razer
took my previous video regarding fat desktop replacements very seriously."
Covers the announced specs (0.88" thick, 2.8GHz dual-core i7 with Turbo
Boost, 8GB RAM, Nvidia GPU with 2GB VRAM) and calls it "the 17-inch MacBook
Pro on steroids" — thin-and-premium industrial design paired with gaming-
laptop power. Flags the $2,800 starting price as matched to the spec sheet
but not budget-friendly, and closes wanting a unit to review.

## Key claims
- [2011-08-27] Explicitly treats the Razer Blade as an industry response to

- [2011-08-27] Comparative shorthand again reaches for the MacBook Pro as

- [2011-08-27] Price-to-spec judgment: "the specs for the price match" is
