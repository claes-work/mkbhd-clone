# Moto X Style Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2015-07-29)

## Summary
First impressions of the Moto X Style (2015) from a NYC Motorola event — "like the Nexus 6 and the 2014
Moto X had a baby with metal on the back." A strong spec sheet at a competitive **$399** (near-stock, front-
facing stereo speakers, turbocharging, a new 21 MP camera), but two dated concerns: **camera skepticism**
(Motorola's history + "DxOMark says top-3, but I'm still not totally convinced until I test it myself") and
**size** (the first Moto X that feels like a two-handed phone). Also clarifies the confusing Style / Pure
Edition / Play lineup.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-07-29] **A Nexus-6-like Moto X with killer specs at $399.** Snapdragon 808, 3 GB, 64 GB + microSD,
  a 3000 mAh battery with "the fastest turbocharging" (10 hr in 15 min), a 5.7" QHD LCD in an iPhone-6-Plus
  footprint, **stereo front-facing speakers** (an upgrade from last year's single), a 21 MP f/2.0 4K camera,
  NFC ("looking at you, OnePlus"), near-stock software with Moto gestures. "Classic Motorola — a solid,
  competitively-priced, good-looking phone."
- [2015-07-29] **Camera skepticism — don't trust the score until I test it.** "Motorola has struggled with
  flagship cameras… they claim a whole new ball game and early DxOMark reviews put it top-3. I read that
  review, played with it at the event, and **I'm still not totally convinced** — once I get to spend a lot
  of time with it, the camera is definitely something I'll thoroughly test." (Verify claims with his own
  shots.)
- [2015-07-29] **Size concern.** "It's gotten a lot bigger — the first Moto X that actually feels like it's
  going to be a two-handed device… it used to be focused on being small. It'll look huge next to a Galaxy
  S6 or LG G4."
- [2015-07-29] **The lineup, clarified:** **Moto X Style** (US flagship) = **Moto X Pure Edition** (same,
  unlocked/carrier-free) ≠ **Moto X Play** ($100 less, 5.5" 1080p, Snapdragon 615, a huge 3630 mAh battery —
  "would have loved to see that huge battery in some other phones," but not coming to the US).
