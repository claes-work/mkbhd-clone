# Dot View 2 Case for HTC One M9! (@mkbhd, 2015-03-07)

## Summary
A look at HTC's Dot View 2.0 flip case for the One M9 — a rare **case** video, and a **no-cases
exception**: he covered the original because it was "one of the more interesting and most popular
first-party accessories I've seen in a while." The 2.0 adds a clearish back (to show the metal), fuller
rim protection, improved magnets/hinge (lays flat now), and new Dot View app themes/features. Low-yield
accessory content.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-03-07] **A no-cases exception, explained.** "A lot of you know how I feel about cases, especially
  on premium metal phones" — but the original Dot View was interesting/popular enough to cover; the 2.0 is
  "a little more eyebrow-raising."
- [2015-03-07] **Physical improvements:** clearish (slightly blue-shimmer) transparent back to show the
  metal, fuller edge/rim coverage (very snug, hard to remove), and **improved magnets + hinge** so it
  "lays flat and doesn't bounce around" (a fix vs the first version).
- [2015-03-07] **Function:** still a dot-matrix flip cover — magnets wake the screen; see/answer calls,
  notifications, and charging status through the dots without opening it; new Dot View app themes and
  features.
