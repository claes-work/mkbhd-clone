# Unboxed: iFrogz EarPollution Custom Fallouts (@mkbhd, 2009-06-03)

## Summary
Unboxing of the iFrogz EarPollution **Fallout** over-ear headphones — a *customizable* model,
which is why this page matters more than a routine unboxing: asked to pick his own colours,
15-year-old Marques chose **red and black**, "which happen to be my favorite colors." That is
the **earliest dated, first-person statement of the red-on-black preference** that later
becomes the MKBHD brand and the crimson studio (stated as a personal favourite here, not as
a brand decision — do not assert causation). The video also carries an unusually candid
**production-schedule confession**: school essays and finals are throttling uploads, so this
is "one of the last unboxing videos for a while." He proposes his first **comparison review**
— Fallouts vs. the in-ear D33s he reviewed two weeks earlier
([[2009-05-20-yt-PXCPXQrK01Q]]) — and praises iFrogz's turnaround as a vendor. ★ L3-candidate.

## Key claims
- [2009-06-03] **Red and black are his stated favourite colours**: "I did customize these to
  be red and black basically, which happen to be my favorite colors." First-person, dated,
  and about a personal purchase choice — not (yet) a branding statement.
- [2009-06-03] **School is the upload constraint**: "I've just been so busy with schoolwork
  and homework... I have so many essays and everything... I have finals coming up" — this is
  "one of the last unboxing videos for a while," maybe "one or two more tutorials and possibly
  a review video."
- [2009-06-03] **The constraint is framed as temporary**: "I know the school year is winding
  down so this phase won't last very long."
- [2009-06-03] **Ratings as the engagement ask of the era**: "please rate five stars if you
  want these to keep going" (YouTube's pre-2010 five-star rating, before the like button).
- [2009-06-03] **First comparison-review proposal**: "I can't wait to get you guys with a
  comparison video between these much more portable D33s and the over-ear, powerful, louder
  Fallout... a big compare-contrast video probably in one or two weeks."
- [2009-06-03] **He hedges rather than over-promises**: "the review video may or may not
  happen," and the comparison timing is "depending on how really busy I am."
- [2009-06-03] **Vendor-service assessment, unprompted**: "iFrogz — their service is extremely
  extremely fast; they got back to me with the emails within like hours of when I emailed
  them, the order was shipped really quickly." He reviews the *company's* responsiveness, not
  just the product.
- [2009-06-03] **Customization is the product's point**: buyers "choose your colors and your
  styles and designs" on the iFrogz site (link in the description).
- [2009-06-03] **Spec-level description, honestly bounded**: 3.5 mm jack, "just standard
  over-the-ear headphones, not noise-canceling," stereo; on cord length — "I don't know
  exactly how long the cord is but we'll find that out later."
- [2009-06-03] **Packaging pattern-recognition across a product line**: the Fallouts "look
  strikingly similar to the D33s in that the headphones were on top and a little accessories
  kit on the bottom."
- [2009-06-03] **First impression of build**: "they do look like a very sturdy solid build. I
  like the colors that I picked, they look quite nice."
