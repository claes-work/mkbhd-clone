# Jaybird X2 Review: Best Bluetooth Earbuds! (@mkbhd, 2015-08-18)

## Summary
A review of the Jaybird X2 Bluetooth earbuds — the sequel to the BlueBuds X, which he calls "some of my
favorite headphones like ever" (on his top-5-under-$200 list). The X2 is a **refresh** (same product, new
2015 internals) rather than a redesign — new colors (his pick: **the red-and-black one, "called Fire"** —
his brand colors), free foam tips, a better carrying case, and multi-device pairing. An audio/gear datum
plus a red-and-black preference note.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-08-18] **The originals were among his favorite headphones ever.** The BlueBuds X — "I didn't even
  use earbuds that much, but I'd pretty much never leave the house without these" — great sound, forever
  battery, under $200 (on his top-5-under-$200 list).
- [2015-08-18] **The X2 is a refresh, not a redesign** — "remaking the same product with new technology for
  2015" (they dropped the "BlueBuds" name).
- [2015-08-18] **Red-and-black, again.** New colors — "my personal favorite of course being the red and
  black, which is called **Fire**." (Consistent with his stated red-and-black preference.)
- [2015-08-18] **What's new:** three foam tips now **included** (previously a paid accessory), ear fins, an
  improved carrying case (the old hard clamshell was "super annoying"), and pairing with up to 8 devices.
