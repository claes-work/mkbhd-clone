# Youtube Mega-Widescreen Camtasia Studio 6 Settings (@mkbhd, 2009-02-28)

## Summary
A heavily-requested tutorial (Marques, age 15) laying out **the exact encoding preset he uses
to produce every 720p HD video** in Camtasia Studio 6 — the earliest fully-documented MKBHD
production pipeline. He builds a reusable "720p" preset via Batch Production → Preset Manager
and dictates every setting: container **.mov (QuickTime required)**, video codec **H.264**,
**30 fps** (matching keyframe rate), compressor **Best Quality, multi-pass encoding**; audio
**MPEG-4, 48 kHz, 16-bit, stereo**; output frame size **1280x720** (states 1280x720 is the
max YouTube will show, so larger source screens get letterboxed to a chosen background color).
He also shows adding an optional corner **watermark**. This is a rare, concrete origin-era
record of his early quality-obsessed encoding craft — the through-line to the later MKBHD
production reputation. ★ L3-candidate.
