# Jaybird Freedom: Best Wireless Earbuds? (@mkbhd, 2016-05-30)

## Summary
Review of the $200 Jaybird Freedom wireless earbuds (his go-to had been Jaybird BlueBuds). Two clone-
relevant threads: his **spec-honesty instinct** (calling out the "8-hour battery" as really 4 hours),
and his enduring interest in **software/app-tuned hardware** (the MySound app that bakes an EQ into the
earbuds' firmware). Notable pre-AirPods context: wireless earbuds *before* Apple killed the jack.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-05-30] **Battery-honesty call-out.** "Jaybird claims 8 hours from this smaller design — except
  it isn't 8 hours. It's a 4-hour battery, plus another 4 from the charging cradle. I don't think it's
  fair to call this 8-hour battery life — that's like claiming a phone has 3-day battery because you can
  attach an external battery." Insists on the honest number (4 hours) while still crediting it. His
  standing refusal to launder a marketing spec.
- [2016-05-30] **App-tuned sound = "the killer feature."** The MySound app pairs the buds, shows exact
  battery %, and lets you set a sound signature that's written to the **firmware** — "so it follows the
  earbuds around: add a ton of bass on day one, and it'll still be there a month later on a new phone
  with Spotify." Live preview while adjusting. His recurring enthusiasm for software making hardware
  personal.
- [2016-05-30] **Design praise — the size.** 13 g, micro-tapered so they don't stick out of your ears
  ("helmet-friendly, hat-friendly"); "when you're wearing them it almost feels like there's nothing in
  your ear." Multiple fit options (over/under-ear, wing/no-wing, foam/rubber).
- [2016-05-30] **The "you don't need to upgrade" honesty.** "Do you have to get these? No, of course
  not. Unlike smartphones or laptops, headphones don't turn over and become obsolete year after year —
  if you have a good wireless pair now that sounds great, that works for you." Anti-churn advice, rare
  in a review. "But if you're in the market… this would be just about the top-notch pair you can get."
