# Samsung Galaxy S6 Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2015-03-01)

## Summary
First impressions of the Galaxy S6 (MWC 2015) — Samsung's "rebuilt from the ground up" flagship, now in
**real metal + Gorilla-Glass-4 glass** (not plastic-painted-to-look-like-metal). Marques's sharpest
observation is a **"look vs hold"** split: "it's two different things to look at the phone versus holding
it — it's actually one of the best-feeling phones Samsung has made in a while." The QHD AMOLED is "likely
the best display on any smartphone," and TouchWiz — trimmed "40%" but "still not pretty" — is at least
**the smoothest QHD phone he's used**, even beating his Nexus.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-03-01] **Real materials, at last.** Chamfered metal sides + a shimmery glossy glass back (a
  fingerprint magnet), the glass curving to meet the metal so "your hand is basically holding metal all
  the time." Slightly thinner (6.8 mm) and narrower (thin LG-G3-like bezels) — "a 5.1" display in a
  compact, usable body."
- [2015-03-01] **The display is Samsung at its best** — 5.1" QHD AMOLED, 577 ppi, up to 600 nits ("looks
  like a piece of paper," likely the best phone display at launch). The open question is the **2550 mAh
  battery** driving QHD (to be tested).
- [2015-03-01] **Camera:** new 16 MP f/1.9 OIS sensor + double-tap-home shortcut ("under a second"), 4K
  video, wide f/1.9 selfie cam. The protruding rear camera "is right in the middle, so it doesn't affect
  typing on a table — not as much of a big deal as people made it."
- [2015-03-01] **Look-vs-hold (the durable observation).** "This is not the prettiest phone Samsung has
  ever made, but it's two different things to look at it versus actually hold it — one of the best-feeling
  phones Samsung has made in a while," thanks to the materials, tighter bezels, and display.
- [2015-03-01] **TouchWiz: trimmed but still not pretty — yet fast.** Samsung claims 40% less software
  cruft; still has My Magazine, ugly stock icons, pre-loads — "but at least it feels fast… the smoothest
  QHD phone out there, which I can't say about any other, even my Nexus."
- [2015-03-01] **Trade-offs to get there:** lost IP67 water resistance, microSD, and the removable back;
  gained Qi wireless charging, an IR blaster, and fast charging (50% in 30 min).
