# Batman Edition Galaxy S7 Edge! (@mkbhd, 2016-06-24)

## Summary
An unboxing of the limited-edition Batman (Injustice) Galaxy S7 Edge — #396 of only 1,000 — which he's
giving away. Light content, but two clone-relevant notes: his appreciation of tasteful special-edition
hardware (the "stealth black + gold accents" execution) and, contra his usual all-phones-look-alike
complaint, praise for the S7 Edge as an already-distinctive design.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-06-24] **Likes that Samsung keeps doing superhero editions.** "Samsung doesn't have to keep doing
  these superhero-edition designs and custom packages, but I'm glad they keep doing it — keep it up." A
  pro-fun-hardware, pro-variety stance (recall he liked last year's Iron Man edition too).
- [2016-06-24] **Batman = the ideal "murdered-out" execution.** "Batman might be the perfect superhero to
  do a phone with, because you can just go stealth black — this thing is murdered out top to bottom, with
  little gold accents anywhere you'd previously see glossy silver (home-button ring, earpiece, camera
  cutout), plus the gold Batman logo." Notes the always-on display shows a faded Batman logo — "pretty
  sweet."
- [2016-06-24] **Aesthetics honesty about the software theme.** "Not going to lie, it's not the prettiest
  software theme I've ever seen" — but credits it for staying consistent (wallpapers, icon pack, animated
  cape lock screen). You can disable it for a stock theme if it's your daily.
- [2016-06-24] **Counterpoint to his own "all phones look alike" gripe.** "We talk about how phones all
  look similar nowadays, but the S7 Edge — glass front, dual curved edges, glass back — was already kind
  of a unique phone; this takes it to the next level with a fresh coat of paint." A rare it-actually-
  stands-out note.
- [2016-06-24] **The giveaway.** "#396 of only 1,000 they'll ever make, and I'm giving it away — follow
  on Twitter for a shot at this $1,200 super-limited-edition phone." (Audience-engagement / giveaway
  mechanic.) Recurring pet peeve aside: "why they always put the sticker right in the middle of the back,
  I'll never know."
