# iKey Audio M-505V2 Studio Monitors! (@mkbhd, 2012-05-29)

## Summary
A short review of the iKey Audio M-505V2 near-field studio monitors — the speakers that had been
visible in the background of his videos, prompting so many comments that he made a video about them
without ever having mentioned them. Notable as an explicit launch of a **mini-review format**, a
direct restatement of his **red-and-black preference as a stated bias**, and a callback to the
first-ever MKBHD 10/10 (the 8-inch sibling).

## Key claims
- [2012-05-29] **Content is pure audience-demand**: "a product that's gotten a lot of attention in my
  videos — even the videos that haven't been about this particular product… they've been in the
  background of my videos for a little while now and you guys have asked about them." The request-driven
  loop at its purest.
- [2012-05-29] **Mini-review format launched**: "give a thumbs up if you enjoy the sort of mini reviews
  that I'm probably going to start doing more often, in between the more major videos." A new
  short-form review format for the ledger, alongside the 30-second tip and single-feature deep dive.
- [2012-05-29] **Red-and-black stated openly as a preference that colors the verdict**: "these are all a
  nice red and black finish, they look really good… they match the theme of my channel and I just love
  red and black, so I really can't say anything bad about these studio monitors." He names the bias
  rather than hiding it — the same disclosure move as the first 10/10 review.
- [2012-05-29] **Callback to the first-ever 10/10**: "I gave the 8-inch version the first ever 10 out of
  10 I ever gave a product review" ([[2010-09-17-yt-KG5cIynaW-E]]) — self-consistent scoring memory
  across ~20 months.
- [2012-05-29] Technical notes: near-field monitors "made to be on your desk near your ears";
  magnetically shielded (safe beside a monitor and a phone); **internally amplified** ("you don't need
  another amplifier, plug them straight into your computer"); 1" tweeter + 5" woofer; a bass port slot.
- [2012-05-29] **Setup detail, dated**: the Cyborg R.A.T. 9 mouse (red and black) is also in the
