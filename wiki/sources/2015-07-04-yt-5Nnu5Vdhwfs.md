# Top 5 Upcoming Tech! (Mid 2015) (@mkbhd, 2015-07-04)

## Summary
A deliberately **subjective** list of the tech he's most excited about for the rest of 2015 — "no one's
going to watch this two or three years from now, but I'm excited now." Notable takeaways: the **worst thing
about his S6 Edge daily driver is the battery life** (so he's hyped for the bigger-batteried Note 5), the
**OnePlus 2** is moving upmarket (no longer super-budget) with a #hype teaser-marketing campaign, and the
next Nexus is a total unknown (Motorola/LG/Huawei rumors) that "always shapes the direction Android goes."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-07-04] **#1 Galaxy Note 5.** The Note takes the S-series' software in a bigger body with a bigger
  battery — and **"the absolute worst thing about the Galaxy S6 Edge I use as a daily driver right now is
  the battery life."** He hopes the Note 5 keeps real metal (no fake leather); fine with losing microSD/
  removable "if the battery is big enough" (he's on a 128 GB phone).
- [2015-07-04] **#2 OnePlus 2 — upmarket, with hype marketing.** "The OnePlus One was known for being super
  cheap; the OnePlus 2 is not going to follow those exact footsteps." OnePlus teases specs every few days
  (Snapdragon 810 "whether it overheats or not we'll see," USB-C, a fast fingerprint reader, #hype). "More
  of a normal flagship" than a One-owner upgrade, with rumored multiple versions (4 GB / 3 GB / a mini).
- [2015-07-04] **#3 the next Nexus — a total unknown.** "We know almost nothing" (maker rumored Motorola /
  LG / Huawei), "but no matter what, it'll run the latest Android M, be clean stock, probably USB-C, and it
  always shapes the direction Android goes — a showcase for the OS."
- [2015-07-04] **#4 Moto 360 v2.** Excited because the original Moto 360 "became a pioneer" of Android Wear.
