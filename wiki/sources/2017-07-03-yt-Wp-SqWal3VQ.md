# iPhone 8 Rumor Roundup! (@mkbhd, 2017-07-03)

## Summary
A consolidated rumor roundup for the 10th-anniversary iPhone (iPhone 8 / X). Clone-relevant for the framing
("the #1 question I get in real life"), his reasoned reading of the fingerprint-under-glass problem, and
several trackable predictions (OLED, wireless charging, USB-C fast-charging brick).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-07-03] **"The next iPhone is the #1 thing people ask me in real life."** Alongside "what do you do
  with the extra tech," "what phone should I get," and "do you play basketball." A personal/biography-flavored
  frame on his public role.
- [2017-07-03] **Predicts OLED + edge-to-edge (finally).** "We're really expecting an OLED display in the
  next iPhone — the first ever; all previous were LCD. Edge-to-edge lets you fit a ~5.8" display in the iPhone
  7 Plus body." His long-standing OLED-iPhone wish, now expected to land. (Guesses 1080p again "knowing
  Apple's bias," would love Quad HD.)
- [2017-07-03] **The fingerprint-under-glass problem, reasoned in three options.** "(1) reader under the
  display glass = ideal, seamless, no lost function — but the tech is brand-new (Qualcomm just demoed it:
  reads through display/metal/underwater, but slower and less reliable than blazing-fast Touch ID, not mass-
  produced), and Apple tends to wait and implement late/better; I doubt it happens. (2) reader on the back
  (like the S8) — doesn't look as good. (3) no reader, face/iris instead — would suck, Touch ID is core to
  iOS." Ranks by Apple's preference, not likelihood — a careful, source-critical read. (Face ID lands.)
- [2017-07-03] **Wireless charging is coming — and confirms a non-metal back.** "There's a new charging-sound
  file buried in iOS 11 that sounds more futuristic — a wireless-charging vibe. Wireless charging doesn't work
  through metal, so if it's there, the back won't be fully metal (glass, like the S8). And for Apple's mission
  of removing ports, to kill the last (charging) port you need wireless charging." A code-and-physics-based
  prediction.
- [2017-07-03] **Trackable spec predictions.** A11 chip, 3–4 GB RAM; **USB-C fast charging via the brick**
  (a USB-C wall brick + a USB-C-to-Lightning cable in the box — *not* a USB-C phone); a **depth-sensing
  camera** for better portrait mode + ARKit. A colors-wish ("a 10th-anniversary edition passing the torch
  from the iPod to a rainbow of iPhone colors — lowest on my list, but sweet"). Renders-lie caveat restated.

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "The number one thing people really want to know is: what do you know about the next iPhone?"
> "Apple tends to sort of lay back a little, let brand-new technologies get tested, see how the market
> responds, and then implement things hopefully better than they have been."
