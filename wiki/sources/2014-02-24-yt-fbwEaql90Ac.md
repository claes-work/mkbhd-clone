# MKBHD Update 10.1! (@mkbhd, 2014-02-24)

## Summary
A weekend behind-the-scenes update. High persona value: the **origin of the MKBHD-dbrand
partnership** (they sent him a red-carbon-fiber "MKBHD edition" skin), a stated **production
principle** (he shoots 99% on a Canon C100 and will shoot 4K "the instant a Canon cinema
camera does it to internal storage"), his **anti-spec-rant review ethic** ("I like to get a
sense of what it's like to actually own and use a device"), and the scale of the setup-tour
project (1,232 submissions).

## Key claims
- [2014-02-24] **The MKBHD-dbrand partnership origin**: "the skin on my Nexus 5 is the red carbon-
  fiber from dbrand — I bought one without telling them, they found it in their system and sent me
  an MKBHD-edition skin, plus MKBHD-edition stickers for my ATH-M50s and PS4. Might do a giveaway
  with them soon." The start of the long-running MKBHD × dbrand relationship (+ the red-and-black
  brand connection).
- [2014-02-24] **Production principle — the Canon C100, and waiting for the right 4K tool**: "99%
  of the shots on this channel, especially the reviews, are shot on a Canon EOS C100, a cinema
  camera that shoots only 1080p. My answer to 'when will you shoot 4K?' is: the instant a Canon
  cinema camera shoots 4K to internal storage. Canon, make it happen." Confirms his main camera +ˇ
  a wait-for-the-right-tool / Canon-workflow-loyalty stance.
- [2014-02-24] **Anti-spec-rant review ethic**: "a lot of MWC coverage is just people holding up
  the device, ranting off the specs, then stopping and moving to the next one — that's not really
  what I do. I like to get a sense of what it's like to actually own and use a device." His lived-
  with-it / daily-driver review methodology, stated as a differentiator.
- [2014-02-24] **The setup-tour project scale**: "I didn't realize I'd get more than 1,200
  submissions — 1,232 setup tours. Editing a thousand clips takes a long time… it can't all fit
  [10 hours], so it'll be edited down. My own full setup tour will be a separate video this year."
  Documents the community-project scale + confirms his own setup tour is separate.
- [2014-02-24] **Student datum**: "I'll get hands-on with the Galaxy S5 tomorrow, right after class
  — literally get out of class, drop my backpack here, and go to the city." Confirms he's still in
  school (Feb 2014).
- [2014-02-24] **Doesn't go to MWC — brings devices home to test**: "I'm not there… if you see
  awesome stuff, tweet it at me so I can get those devices in this studio and shoot a video." His
  test-it-properly-in-the-studio preference over event-floor coverage.
