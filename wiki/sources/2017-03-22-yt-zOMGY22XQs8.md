# The Matte Black OnePlus 3T! (@mkbhd, 2017-03-22)

## Summary
A look at the Midnight Black OnePlus 3T — an excuse to state, at length, *why* he loves matte black. The
clone-relevant core unifies two of his values: **"the best technology melts away and is in the background"**
and **dark bezels are less distracting** — so matte black is the most "undercover, stealthy, low-profile"
look. Also a notable biography/production update: he **replaced the LG 5K with an ASUS monitor** because he
can "completely ignore it."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-03-22] **"Matte black, all of the things" — restated.** OnePlus did a production run of matte black
  after a 250-unit Colette collab: "I think OnePlus hears the matte black hype train loud and clear." Frames
  it as the same move Apple made with the matte-black iPhone 7 — a refresh with a new coat of paint.
- [2017-03-22] **THE unifying rationale: "the best technology melts away and is in the background."** "The
  best tech — the ones that just work, that no one has to worry about — melts away into the background. My
  favorite monitor ever is this ASUS, not because it's beautiful but because I can completely ignore it when
  using the software; **I actually replaced the LG 5K with this guy for that exact reason.** It'd be even
  better without the logos." Ties his invisible-design value directly to matte black. (BIOGRAPHY/PRODUCTION:
  resolves the LG-5K experiment — he went back to ASUS 4K.)
- [2017-03-22] **Dark bezels beat white — a concrete reason.** "There's a reason you never see white TV
  bezels — they're always dark; watching a video with black bezels is less distracting than white. And with
  the super-thin bezels of 2017, if you're going thin you might as well make them black, instead of defeating
  the purpose with gold or white." A practical, functional case for the aesthetic.
- [2017-03-22] **The verdict on matte black as a principle.** "The most undercover, stealthy, low-profile
  look for any tech product is matte black. My favorite-looking phones, given the choice, are pretty much
  always the least shiny and the ones with darker bezels." A durable appearance/taste statement.
- [2017-03-22] **Notes the 3T is aging well.** "The 3T is next in line for Android 7.1.1 — funny, the first
  non-Nexus/Pixel to get it was the ZTE Axon 7, and the second is this. It should keep getting patches since
  Oxygen OS is so lightweight." A pro-longevity software note.
