# Five (More) Facts About Me! (Graduation!) (@mkbhd, 2015-05-11)

A major **biography** landmark: **"today is May 11, 2015, and this is the first video I'm making as a
full-time YouTuber."** He finished the last class of his senior year (graduation ~2 weeks out) and is going
full-time on the channel. Five self-disclosed facts: the **MKBHD name origin**, that he's a **cord-cutter**

  YouTuber… I finished the last class of my senior year, so as far as I'm concerned I'm done." He'd been
  YouTube-part-time / full-time-student "my whole life," always feeling behind; now "I get to really dedicate
  myself" to "making videos I would want to watch." Graduation is ~2 weeks away.
- [2015-05-11] **Fact 1 — the MKBHD name origin.** He signed up as "Marques Brownlee" (one word, hard to
  search/remember), and opened early videos with a long sentence ("Marques Brownlee here, back with another
  high-definition video") that he "crunched down over and over" into "**what's up guys, MKBHD here**" — MKB =
  his initials, HD = high definition.
- [2015-05-11] **Fact 2 — cord-cutter.** "I've lived in this apartment for 3 years and never had a cable
  subscription" — his TV is plugged into a PS4, Xbox One, Chromecast, and Apple TV. He finally bought a TV
  subscription ~a month ago **just to watch the NBA Playoffs** ("let's go Clippers").
- [2015-05-11] **Fact 3 — pro ultimate frisbee, updated.** An amendment to the original Five Facts: the **NJ
  Hammerheads no longer exist**; he now plays for the MLU **New York Rumble** (home games in Queens/NYC, away
  games up and down the east coast). Still a professional athlete.
- [2015-05-11] **Fact 4 — hates the cold.** "I hate it" — the brutal east-coast winter, plus the fact that
  since breaking his hand "it takes longer in cold weather to warm up my wrist" (the ligaments). It "will
  probably have some effect on where I end up living after college" — he'd rather be somewhere "70° all the
  time."
- [2015-05-11] **Fact 5 — done with college.** See the headline. Closes with a joke about the growth curve:
  "instead of 1,000 subscribers, maybe I want 1.3 million… 3 million subscribers, I don't know." And restates
  the ethic: **"even if I'm never first — I already know that — the videos I want to make take a lot of hours."**
