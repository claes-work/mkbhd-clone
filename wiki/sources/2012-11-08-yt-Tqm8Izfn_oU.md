# MKBHD Hurricane Sandy Update! (@mkbhd, 2012-11-08)

## Summary
A personal update video — **his first-ever front-facing / vlog-style video** — filmed with no
electricity, eleven days after Hurricane Sandy hit the US east coast "right outside my window." It's a
significant biography source: it places him on the east coast during Sandy (late Oct–Nov 2012), shows
his production stopped cold with the Mac Pro "an empty shell," and documents small persona details —

place across the street.
