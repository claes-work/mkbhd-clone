# HTC 10 Review! (@mkbhd, 2016-04-29)

## Summary
The full HTC 10 verdict: "a really, really good phone" that "surprised me how underrated it is — the
complete package." A clean example of his **champion-the-underrated** instinct, plus one of his most
quotable durable lines: **"the most underrated piece of hardware that actually makes a smartphone is
good buttons."** Balanced as ever — the camera is the one thing he won't recommend it *for*.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-04-29] **Champion-the-underrated.** "It surprised me how underrated it is — it's really good at
  a lot of things, most anyone would agree it's the complete package, it nails everything you look for
  in a flagship." His recurring instinct to elevate the overlooked-but-excellent product.
- [2016-04-29] **"Good buttons" as an underrated fundamental.** "You know what the most underrated piece
  of hardware that actually makes a smartphone good is? Good buttons, seriously — a textured clicky
  power button and a separate very tactile clicky volume rocker." A durable, characteristic
  evaluation criterion (tactility over spec sheets).
- [2016-04-29] **Sense 8 = a favorite skin ("stockish, but good because it's good").** "Sense 8 is now
  one of my favorite skins I've seen on Android in a long time — close to stock in look and feel, and I
  like that not just because it's stockish but because it's good." Praises removing duplicate apps and
  not overloading with gimmick gestures — "keep it simple." (The de-duped-apps thread from
  [[2016-04-12-yt-5IJWckL5L84]] confirmed at review.)
- [2016-04-29] **Camera = the one caveat, stated plainly.** "The one place it's kind of lacking is the
  camera — I'm not recommending this phone to anyone just for the camera. It's probably top 10 but it's
  not the best; the Galaxy S7s and iPhones will take better photos for sure." Image quality "just above
  average," errs toward overexposure. Names exactly who should look elsewhere.
- [2016-04-29] **Build/materials + battery.** All-metal, flat sides, the light-catching chamfer "that
  characterizes this device"; only ip53 (not ip68) — "the one knock." Battery ~4.5 hrs screen-on,
  "pretty much matches the S7 if a little short — I'm a lot happier with flagship battery life this
  year." Fingerprint sensor "top five, behind Touch ID, Samsung and Huawei." 24-bit DAC drives
  high-powered headphones; AirPlay out of the box "a first for any Android phone."
- [2016-04-29] **Forward-looking note.** Ties the 10's design to the rumored HTC-made Nexus later in
  2016 ("should take a lot of the design cues from this phone — but we'll reserve judgment"). His habit
  of reading a phone as a signpost for what's coming.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> Seriously."
