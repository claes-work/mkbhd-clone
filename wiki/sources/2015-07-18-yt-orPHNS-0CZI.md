# iPod Touch 2015 Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-07-18)

## Summary
A review of the two-year-overdue iPod Touch refresh, framed by his recurring **"who is this for?"**
question. His answer: **"someone who wants an iPhone but can't have one"** (his ~10-year-old cousins). The
refresh finally gives it a current-ish A8 chip (its biggest need), a still-4" Retina display, and an
iPhone-5-class 8 MP camera — but **no Touch ID / NFC**, and gaming ends up being its best reason to exist.
A dated biography note (his cousins John/RJ; he now uses an iPhone + Apple Music).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-07-18] **The needed refresh: the A8 chip.** Now benchmarks "a little below the current iPhone,"
  making performance zippy — "the most-needed part of the refresh." Still a 4" Retina (326 ppi) — "I wish
  the screen was a little bigger."
- [2015-07-18] **Gaming is the best reason to buy it.** "A lot of us '90s kids grew up with Game Boys and
  PSPs — I could see someone buying this in 2015 as their first handheld gaming console."
- [2015-07-18] **Still thin/light and well-built** (6.1 mm, 88 g — "no BendGate here"), a slightly bigger
  battery, and a new **8 MP camera on par with the iPhone 5** (Apple reusing top-of-the-line-a-few-years-ago
  parts as they come down in price — "makes sense to finish out the stock"). It **protrudes a bit — "I wish
  it didn't."**
- [2015-07-18] **What's missing:** the home button is unchanged, so **no Touch ID and no NFC** (no Apple
  Pay / fingerprint) — "kind of wish it were there."
- [2015-07-18] **Verdict + who-it's-for.** From $200 (16 GB, "a good start"; even the 32 GB is fine, but
  above that "you're in iPhone price territory — I'd get an iPhone"). "It's kind of hard to say who exactly
  this is for… basically someone who wants an iPhone but can't have one" — like his ~10-year-old cousins
  John and RJ, who get all of iOS's games/apps for a lot less.
- [2015-07-18] **Biography aside:** "I don't need this one anymore — I have an iPhone I use and I listen to
  Apple Music on it" (so he gives the iPod away).
