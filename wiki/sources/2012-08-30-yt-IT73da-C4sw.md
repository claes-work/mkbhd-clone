# CyanogenMod 10 on the Samsung Galaxy S3! (@mkbhd, 2012-08-30)

## Summary
A walkthrough of CyanogenMod 10 (Android 4.1) running on his Verizon Galaxy S3 — and the video that
**resolves the stock-vs-skin tension** of the summer. He switched to the S3 for its hardware in June,
but he'd loved the Galaxy Nexus's stock experience so much that he **rooted the S3 and put near-stock
CM10 on it**, running it as his daily driver "for a couple months now." The consistent value across the
whole arc turns out to be *control over the software*, not any one skin.

## Key claims
- [2012-08-30] **The stock-vs-skin resolution, stated as the motive**: "I've enjoyed my Galaxy Nexus so
  much that I wanted a pure, almost near-stock Android experience on my Galaxy S3." He keeps the S3
  hardware but replaces TouchWiz with CM10 — reconciling the June TouchWiz-over-stock concession
  ([[2012-06-26-yt-4JEZ2rrf2N4]]) with his stock preference: he picked the S3 for hardware, then made it
  near-stock in software.
- [2012-08-30] **Daily driver confirmed, dated**: "it's been my daily driver for a couple months now,
  actually — it's a really really solid ROM." Confirms the S3-on-CM10 as his carry phone through
  summer/fall 2012.
- [2012-08-30] **Names the concrete benefit of near-stock**: "you'll actually find that you use less RAM,
  because TouchWiz does use a little bit of the RAM" — a specific, functional reason for the choice, not
  an ideological one.
- [2012-08-30] **The GPS-lock praise, dated**: "the Galaxy S3 actually has some of the fastest GPS lock
  of any device I have ever used" — a specific hardware strength that helped justify the switch.
- [2012-08-30] **A reusable performance tip**: in Developer Options, set the **animation scale to 0.5×**
  — "you'll notice your device just feels a lot snappier." He prefers animations on-but-fast rather than
  off. A concrete, transferable tweak he says he uses "on all the 4.x devices."
- [2012-08-30] **New-apartment setup, on record**: "I have no idea if this is the final angle for what
  I'm going to be doing videos at — I'm still sort of messing around with things getting it set up." Dates
  the move into the new apartment ([[2012-08-10-yt-_hhEz4Uo79k]]) and a filming-setup reset.
- [2012-08-30] **Self-identifies the audience for the video**: "if you're a really hardcore Android
  person you can go in and customize and change everything" — pitched at the modding/rooting segment,
  which he places himself in.
- [2012-08-30] Camera restated as a switch reason: "another big reason that I've been using the Galaxy S3
  is that 8 megapixel camera… instant shutter."
