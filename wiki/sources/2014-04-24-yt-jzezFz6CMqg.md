# OnePlus One Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2014-04-24)

## Summary
His first coverage of OnePlus — the origin node of a brand he'd cover for a decade. Persona
value: the **value-shock** (flagship specs at $299/$349, "undercuts the Nexus 5, half the
price of the S5/HTC One"), the **"flagship killer"** framing, the **CyanogenMod 11S**
partnership (control-over-software), and his champion-the-underdog curiosity tempered by a
"why so cheap? what's the catch?" skepticism.

## Key claims
- [2014-04-24] **The value-shock — the reason everyone's talking**: "the most impressive thing and
  the real reason people are talking about it is the price — 16 GB for $299, 64 GB for $349 [only
  $50 more for 4× storage, unlike other makers]. It undercuts our beloved Nexus 5, and it's half
  the price of the S5 or HTC One with similar specs. You can get two 64 GB OnePlus Ones for the
  price of one 64 GB iPhone 5S and have $50 left over." His value-as-a-first-class-axis lens, at
  full volume.
- [2014-04-24] **The "flagship killer" framing**: "is this the real flagship killer they're saying
  it is? I'm hesitant because I don't know how they got it down to this price when the M8 continues
  to be $600 — but I'm excited to see it." Uses OnePlus's own term while flagging the skepticism.
- [2014-04-24] **"Why so cheap? What's the catch?" — the honest skepticism**: "the natural
  question is why is it so cheap, what's the tradeoff — is it going to fall apart in two years, is
  there a design flaw we don't know about? It's mind-blowing that it can have the same specs as an
  S5/HTC One at half the price." His don't-assume-it's-too-good skepticism.
- [2014-04-24] **The CyanogenMod 11S partnership — control-over-software**: "on the software side
  you get a custom CyanogenMod 11S built for this device — unique features like data encryption, a
  new camera app, and switching between software and hardware capacitive buttons on the fly, which
  no other phone lets you do. That's the advantage of working with Cyanogen on a custom OS." Ties
  to his control-over-software / CyanogenMod thread.
- [2014-04-24] **Champion-the-underdog curiosity**: "a relatively new Chinese manufacturer, not
  sure what to expect… I'm really curious to get my hands on it and review it in full detail." His
  disposition to spotlight an unknown innovator (cf. the Oppo arc).
- [2014-04-24] **Discloses he doesn't have the phone + credits a peer**: "I don't physically have
  the phone yet, so the hands-on footage is from Josh at Android Authority — he makes excellent
  videos." His honesty about the limits of his coverage + peer credit.
