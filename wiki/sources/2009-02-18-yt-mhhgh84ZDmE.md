# HD Skype Review (Request) (@mkbhd, 2009-02-18)

## Summary
A **request-driven** origin-era tutorial/overview (Marques, age 15) of **Skype 3.8**, one of his
early "review" framings. He covers downloading from skype.com, docking the shortcut in his
**RocketDock**, text chatting, the contacts/status system, and free **Skype-to-Skype** calling
and **video calling** (given a webcam + microphone). He walks through personalization
(avatar/skin, profile), notes paid features (Skype Credit to call landlines/cell phones, send
SMS, send money via PayPal), and demonstrates live by chatting with the viewer who requested the
video (a fellow YouTuber whose channel he cross-promotes in the description). He teases upgrading
to **Skype 4.0** and mentions he has a **dual microphone** for better call quality. An early
example of his "here's the whole app, and here's what's actually useful" walkthrough style.
