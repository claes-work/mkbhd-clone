# HD Tutorial: Camstudio Audio and Video Synchronization (@mkbhd, 2009-02-18)

## Summary
A short origin-era **production troubleshooting** tutorial (Marques, age 15) documenting a bug in
his screen-recording toolchain: in **CamStudio**, the audio can run slightly **ahead** of the
video (he hears words before the corresponding mouse/mouth movement). His fix: Options → Audio
Options → **Audio and Video Synchronization**, then delay the video stream — he has it set to
**1.58 seconds** and admits it may need further refining. He explicitly contrasts this with
**Camtasia Studio 6**, in which the problem "does not exist." Another concrete window into his
origin-era production stack (**CamStudio** as the free option vs. paid **Camtasia**) and his
honest, still-experimenting posture toward his own tools.

## Key claims
- [2009-02-18] In **CamStudio**, audio can lead video; fix via Options → Audio Options → Audio
  and Video Synchronization by delaying the video stream (his current setting **1.58s**, "might
  actually need more refining").
- [2009-02-18] States the same **A/V-sync bug "does not exist in Camtasia Studio 6"** — a direct
  free-tool-vs-paid-tool comparison in his production stack.
- [2009-02-18] Uses **both CamStudio and Camtasia** in his origin-era workflow — corroborates the
  broader production-tooling picture across this batch and cluster.
