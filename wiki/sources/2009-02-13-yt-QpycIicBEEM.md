# HD Tutorial: Customizing Autoplay (@mkbhd, 2009-02-13)

## Summary
Origin-era **Windows Vista** how-to for changing **AutoPlay** settings — what happens when you
insert a given type of media or device. Marques (age 15) opens the AutoPlay control panel via
**Start → search "autoplay"**, then walks through assigning a default action per media type
(rip an audio CD, open in Explorer, open in iTunes, ask every time for blank discs, etc.). He
demos a **device-specific** entry: his **LG Voyager (LG VX10000)** phone, which he sets to sync
via **"RhapCity"/BitPim-style" software** on connect (caption garbles the app name as "Rap
City"). He notes an external drive would likewise appear under Devices with its own AutoPlay
action.
