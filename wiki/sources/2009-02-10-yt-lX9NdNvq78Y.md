# HD Tutorial: Zoom in CamStudio (@mkbhd, 2009-02-10)

## Summary
Origin-era **production-tooling** how-to — Marques (age 15) improving his own screencast
workflow. Because **CamStudio** (the free screen recorder he used for these early videos)
can't zoom natively, he installs a free third-party **"magnifying glass"** utility (~0.5 MB
from workerscollection.com), sets a **Ctrl+Shift+Q hotkey**, and tunes zoom factor, window
size, and cursor offset so he can highlight exactly which button he's clicking on screen.
He explicitly contrasts it with an earlier tutorial that used the Windows mouse magnifier,
noting that older method made the screen flash white/black and interfered with his
microphone — so this is a cleaner way to zoom for recordings.

## Key claims
