# RED Raven Impressions: 4.5K! (@mkbhd, 2016-01-10)

## Summary
Impressions of the RED Raven (RED's new entry-level camera) — and, more valuably, **the story of his camera
journey** from Canon to RED. He was **all-Canon for years** (T2i → 60D → 5D → C100), then in 2014 rented a
**RED Scarlet Mysterium-X for 4 days out of curiosity** — saw the crispiness, the dynamic range, and **the
white-balance slider in Premiere** — and "things have never been the same." A production/gear + biography
landmark that documents his switch into the RED ecosystem.

  videos, but I'm a gear head and many of you are too, so might as well."
