# HD Tutorial: Get Aero Shake For Vista or XP (@mkbhd, 2009-02-09)

## Summary
Origin-era how-to. Marques (age 15) shows how to bring the **Windows 7 "Aero Shake"**
feature — grab a window, shake it, and all other windows minimize; shake again to restore
— to **Windows Vista/XP** via a tiny (~200 KB) third-party utility linked from
**Lifehacker**. He installs it, notes it sits in the system tray, and demos minimizing/
restoring a cluster of open windows, framing it as a focus aid. Acknowledges the tool is
still in beta and that his small screen-capture area limits the demo.

## Key claims
- [2009-02-09] **How-to:** a ~200 KB Lifehacker-linked utility replicates Windows 7's
  Aero Shake on Vista/XP; it runs from the system tray after install.
- [2009-02-09] Aero Shake is useful to **focus on one window** by shaking away the rest;
  notes alternatives exist (e.g. Win+D, maximize).
- [2009-02-09] Flags the tool as **beta** and imperfect — an early instance of caveating
  a recommendation.
