# Samsung Galaxy Note 5 Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-09-18)

## Summary
The full Note 5 review — most of the change is the **new S6-style design language** (all metal + glass,
curved-glass back), which is "much prettier but also potentially much uglier when you inevitably get a
bunch of fingerprints on it." His headline gripe: it **"takes the crown for the slipperiest phone of all
time"** ("slipped out of my pocket in cars more than any other phone"). His fix, consistent with his
no-cases stance: a **skin, not a case** (dbrand — change the texture without adding bulk to an already-big
phone).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-09-18] **The big change is the design.** All-metal-and-glass with chamfered rails and a curved
  all-glass back — "much prettier, but a fingerprint magnet." Four colors; his pick is **titanium gray**
  "since it shows the least fingerprints." Sealed (no removable battery / no microSD), like the S6.
- [2015-09-18] **Slipperiest phone of all time (the crown).** "With its larger size, the Note 5 takes the
  crown for the slipperiest phone of all time, both in the hand and in the pocket — it's slipped out of my
  pocket in cars more than any other phone; it's like a bar of soap."
- [2015-09-18] **Skin over case (no-cases, applied).** "You'll probably want to change the texture on the
  back… a lot of people might think about a case, but it's already such a big phone you probably don't want
  it bigger — so the easiest way to change the texture without adding bulk is a **skin**" (a dbrand skin,
  pick texture + color).
- [2015-09-18] **The good:** a smaller footprint than the Note 4 (thin bezels), and excellent
  headphone-jack/fingerprint/button placement.
