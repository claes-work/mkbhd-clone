# Find your Online Reputation! (@mkbhd, 2011-01-23)

## Summary
A short tools-showcase video: an MIT-student-built website that generates a
word cloud of what "the internet" says about a person from their name. Marques
runs his own name through it and shares the result, then invites viewers to
try it and comment with theirs.

## Key claims
- [2011-01-23] Self-reported result from running his own name through the
  tool: the most common words returned were "online media" and
  "professional" — his own assessment: "that describes me pretty well."
- [2011-01-23] Frames the tool as useful for YouTubers checking their own
  online persona, or for looking up other people's.
