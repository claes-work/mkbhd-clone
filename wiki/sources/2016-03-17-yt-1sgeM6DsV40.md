# Samsung Galaxy S7 Review! (@mkbhd, 2016-03-17)

## Summary
The full S7 review, structured entirely as "everything that's new and different vs. the S6" — his
signature incremental-review scaffold. The headline moments for the clone: the **anti-thinness plea
paying off** (the phone got *thicker* and he calls that "the upgrade"), a **microSD prediction he
made coming true**, the **waterproofing → worst-speaker-downgrade tradeoff** named honestly, and the
new dual-pixel camera crowned "my favorite camera on any smartphone right now."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-03-17] **ANTI-THIN VINDICATION — "thicker is the upgrade."** "Most people hear 'upgraded
  body' to mean it got thinner, but this phone is actually slightly **thicker — and I'm calling that
  the upgrade**," because it enables a much smaller camera bump, a smaller home-button bump, and a
  much larger 3,000 mAh battery. The literal payoff of his long anti-thinness campaign. (Strengthens
  the pass-13 doctrine; see [[../../persona/beliefs.md]].)
- [2016-03-17] **His microSD prediction came true.** "The S7 got the microSD card slot back — I did
  not expect that to actually happen; I threw it out there in an older predictions video as an oddball
  feature that might happen, and you do actually have it now." (A tracked-prediction hit.)
- [2016-03-17] **Waterproofing tradeoff, named honestly.** ip68 with no flaps over the ports/jack
  ("I love that the design stays as it gets waterproofed"), BUT: "pretty sure the S7 just got the
  **biggest downgrade of any speakers on a smartphone** — tinnier and more distorted — and you can
  blame that fully on the waterproofing." Naming the specific cost of a headline feature.
- [2016-03-17] **"My favorite camera on any smartphone right now."** 12 MP (fewer than last year,
  but "12 is plenty and these pixels are now larger"), f/1.7, OIS, **dual-pixel autofocus** — "no
  shooter makes me feel as confident as a photo and video taker as the S7." The confidence-of-the-
  shooter criterion again drives the verdict, not spec sheets.
- [2016-03-17] **Balanced camera critique.** Auto mode = "very typical Samsung: bright punchy colors,
  saturation, contrast, sharpening"; f/1.7 blows out backgrounds easily even in daylight; **weaknesses**
  = overexposes in daylight (more blown highlights than crushed shadows) and it's beaten by the Nexus
  6P in low light. "The autofocus is the best part… the fastest smartphone camera alongside the iPhone."
- [2016-03-17] **Still the slipperiest phone ever → skin, not case.** Reiterates the slipperiness;
  "looks and feels better with a case, or even better a nice skin" (links a dbrand matte skin) — the
  recurring skins-over-cases + fingerprint-hiding thread.
- [2016-03-17] **Battery + display verdict.** Larger 3,000 mAh battery → "B+ / B" screen-on time of
  ~5–5.5 hours, "a big improvement over the notably bad S6 battery life"; the 5.1" Quad HD Super AMOLED
  is "the best display in any smartphone"; fingerprint reader "second only to Touch ID."
