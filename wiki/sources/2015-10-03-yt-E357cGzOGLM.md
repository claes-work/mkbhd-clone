# iPhone 6s Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-10-03)

## Summary
The full iPhone 6s review — same design as the 6 (with a subtle **BendGate fix**: 7000-series aluminum,
internally reinforced around the volume buttons where it bent), a blazing A9, and the headline feature
**3D Touch** ("seems a little gimmicky when it's this early, but it'll get cooler as developers adopt it —
it'll be important soon"). His verdicts land two dated positions: **Live Photos "kind of is a gimmick"** (a
"big beautiful demo for 3D Touch"), and a storage rant — **"16 gigabytes? Really. 16-gig phones need to
die — and it's not just Apple, everyone stop it."** Camera praise centers on **warm, accurate colors and
image processing over sharpening**.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-10-03] **BendGate fix.** "Apple's now using a 7000-series aluminum for a stronger body… reinforced
  internally around the volume buttons where it bent last year." Rose gold "is pink" (a bit; "it's rose
  gold" — a skit). Still rounded/slippery — "you already know what to do" (a skin). Thicker/taller/wider,
  so last year's cases won't fit.
- [2015-10-03] **Touch ID "ridiculously fast — some might say too fast"** (you can miss your lock-screen
  notifications because it unlocks on a tap). A9 is "blazing" (Geekbench on par with laptops).
- [2015-10-03] **The battery is technically smaller (1715 mAh) but life is about the same.** The 6s is
  "okay" (a full day, heavy days need a charger); **the 6s Plus is a battery champ** (best standby, "I've
  failed to kill it in a day").
- [2015-10-03] **3D Touch = the biggest feature, gimmicky-now-important-later.** Peek-and-pop + home-screen
  quick-action shortcuts. "Right now, when it's early, it seems a little excessive/gimmicky — but it'll
  become very important in the future once developers build it in meaningfully."
- [2015-10-03] **Camera: image-processing over sharpening; warm/accurate colors.** 12 MP (finally enough
  for **4K video**) — "you won't notice a ton of detail difference over 8 MP; the improvements are in
  colors and image processing, not a lot of sharpening." Warm tones that are "a good representation of the
  actual subject, as opposed to cooling the colors, which might look better at first but is less accurate."
  Great exposure/dynamic range (auto-HDR is never too aggressive). "The camera interface is some of the
  best balance of control and ease of use in any smartphone."
- [2015-10-03] **Live Photos "kind of is a gimmick."** "I tried really hard to give it a chance… it's
  basically a really big beautiful demo for 3D Touch" (can't share them, mostly captures him pocketing the
  phone).
- [2015-10-03] **The storage rant.** "My biggest problem is 16 gigabytes. Really? With 4K video and Live
  Photos, it's 2015 — **16-gig phones need to die, and it's not just Apple, everyone stop it.**"
