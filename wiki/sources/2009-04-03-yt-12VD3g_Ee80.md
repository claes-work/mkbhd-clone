# HD Tutorial: Explore Mars in Google Earth 5.0 (@mkbhd, 2009-04-03)

## Summary
A short feature-tip screencast (Marques, age 15) showing that **Google Earth 5.0** can switch
between **Earth, Sky, and Mars** via the planet button in the toolbar. He flies to the famous
**"Face on Mars,"** points out Mars-specific layers (a **Traveler's Guide to Mars**, **Rovers
and Landers**, NASA rover imagery), and notes Mars is less detailed than Earth "because we
don't live on Earth [sic] and we're not discovering new things every day." Cross-references
his own **"how to download and install Google Earth 5.0"** tutorial. Notably he flags a
**production constraint**: the capture is **"framy"** because he's **running Google Earth and
Camtasia Studio 6 at the same time** on his machine — an honest, early look at the hardware
limits of his origin-era setup.
