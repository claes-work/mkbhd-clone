# HD Tutorial: Firefox 'Superbar' (@mkbhd, 2009-02-08)

## Summary

had unified them into one "omnibox." Marques (age 15) shows installing the **Google
Toolbar** add-on so any text typed where a URL goes (e.g. "Boeing 767") is auto-Googled,
approximating Chrome's single-box behavior — a "superbar." Notably, he says he'll be doing
a **review of Google Chrome shortly** — one of the earliest on-record mentions of the
