# HD Tutorial: Change Video Speed in Camtasia Studio (@mkbhd, 2009-03-19)

## Summary
A production-tooling screencast (Marques, age 15) showing how to **change or reverse clip speed
in Camtasia Studio 6**. He imports media to the timeline, selects the clip with the key-frame
selector, right-clicks → **Clip Speed**, and explains the numeric options: **200%** halves
duration/doubles speed, **800%** is unrealistically fast, **-100%** plays the clip **backwards**,
and values like **150%** give modest speed-ups. He flags a Camtasia limitation — the sped-up
result is **only visible after producing/rendering the video**, not in the preview window —
and contrasts that with **Sony Vegas**, promising a Sony Vegas version of the tutorial once he
learns it. Ends with his standard request-driven call to action and a teaser for an upcoming
"sweet backgrounds/wallpapers" video.

## Key claims
- [2009-03-19] In **Camtasia Studio 6**, clip speed is set numerically via right-click → Clip
  Speed; **-100%** reverses the clip, **200%** doubles speed, etc.
- [2009-03-19] **Camtasia limitation:** speed changes are **not previewed** in the editor — you
  only see them after producing the video — which he notes is **unlike Sony Vegas** (a tool he
  intends to learn and cover next).
- [2009-03-19] Continues building out his **editing-workflow tutorial** series and his
  request-fulfillment engagement mechanic.

## Notable verbatim quotes (Marques — voice data)
> "you can also set it to negative 100% which would play your video backwards"

> "the new clip speed is only visible after the video has been produced so it's not like Sony Vegas where you actually see the preview"
