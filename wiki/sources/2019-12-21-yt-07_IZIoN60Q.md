# Dope Tech: The World's Largest 8K OLED! (@mkbhd, 2019-12-21)

## Summary
Year-end 2019 episode of Dope Tech, Marques's recurring segment for awesome, fun, or interesting pieces of tech he's discovered, bought, or experienced and thinks are worth sharing. He runs through four items: the Star Wars Edition Samsung Galaxy Note 10 (a special-edition phone he praises for fully committing to the theme — red camera ring, First Order logo, dark software theme, Star Wars sounds, and a red Kylo Ren–style stylus); a pair of ColorWare matte-black-painted AirPods Pro (custom repaint job where the look in your ear is the point, the painted case a bonus); the Oculus Quest standalone VR headset (praised as a happy medium between PC-tethered and phone-slot VR, with inside-out tracking, the Guardian boundary system, low latency, high frame rates, and good hand tracking — sponsored portion); and the headline item, LG's 88-inch 8K OLED TV — the largest OLED display in production you can buy, a 33-megapixel (7680×4320) panel costing $30,000. Marques frames the TV as clearly next-level and bleeding edge but not maximizable by almost anyone today (HDMI can't carry 8K over one cable, almost no 8K content exists), making it a high-end statement piece whose real value is size, brightness, contrast, and LG's upscaling of sub-8K content. He closes noting it'll be the first piece of tech moving into the new studio space planned for early 2020.

## Key claims
- [2019-12-21] Dope Tech is his segment for "some of the most awesome, incredible, or just fun or interesting pieces of tech" he's discovered, bought, or had an experience with that he thinks are worth sharing. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] The Star Wars Edition Note 10 is a "super legitimate" special-edition phone that fully leans into the dark side — red camera/button ring, First Order logo on the back, a dark software theme, red-and-black helmet wallpaper, Star Wars sounds, and a red stylus like Kylo Ren's lightsaber. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] He typically mutes his phone as soon as he takes it out of the box, so he doesn't use the phone's sounds. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] He's excited for next year's "potentially high refresh rate" Galaxy Note 11. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] ColorWare precisely takes apart, repaints, customizes, and reassembles electronics; he had his AirPods Pro (case and earbuds) done in matte black. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] The unpainted parts are the inside of the case and the rubber ear tips, which creates a color contrast; the main reason to paint AirPods is so they look different in your ears, with the painted case as a bonus for telling yours apart on a table. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] The Oculus Quest is a standalone VR headset — a "happy medium" between PC-tethered headsets like the Rift and phone-slot headsets — with built-in outside sensors (no external room trackers), USB-C charging, built-in near-ear speakers, and two headphone jacks (one per side). (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] Because there are no external trackers, you can set up in any open area; the setup uses pass-through camera view to let you draw your play space as a safe zone called the "Guardian," which then puts up virtual walls and calibrates the floor. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] In-game the Quest impressed him: latency still pretty low, frame rates high, good field-of-view fill, stable tracking he never noticed losing, and controllers whose finger tracking mimics real hand/finger positions. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] His recommended first VR games are Beat Saber and Superhot; the Oculus store has ~50+ more titles you add via the phone app. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] The LG 88-inch 8K OLED is the largest OLED display currently in production that you can buy, at 88 inches diagonal. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] It's 8K — 7680 across by 4320 down — roughly 33 million pixels, i.e. a 33-megapixel screen. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] It won't be maximized by almost anyone: current HDMI can't carry 8K over a single cable, and there's almost no 8K content on TV, YouTube, or Netflix — so at $30,000 it's more of a high-end statement piece and bleeding edge. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] Its practical value today is size, brightness, contrast, color, and LG's upscaling of sub-8K content, which he says he can actually tell the difference on; LG's website explanation of the upscaling isn't very scientific (two diagrams with "two levels of enhance"). (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] It's only the second 8K display ever in his studio; the first was the Dell 8K monitor, the first screen on which he could see the 8K footage they shoot at native resolution. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] Plugging an 8K display into a computer to game brings quirks — scaling can be weird in some games and most graphics cards "refuse to cooperate" — but it still looks great upscaled. (Marques)
- [2019-12-21] The TV will be the first piece of tech moving into the new studio space they're planning for early 2020. (Marques)
