# Dope Tech: Nothing Phone 2 Redesign! (@mkbhd, 2023-07-03)

## Summary
A three-segment solo Dope Tech. (1) **Virtual production / XR stage** at zero space,
Brooklyn — a 50-ft 8K LED video wall with camera-tracked parallax (Unreal Engine
environments), which he frames via his **three levels of production** model (practical /
partly-virtual / fully-virtual). The mind-blower: the wall runs at 120fps so **two cameras
shoot alternating 60fps frames** and each sees a different tracked background at once
(the stage can push to 180fps / six 30fps cameras). Explored with fellow creator **Cleo
Abram**. (2) **Eight Sleep Pod cover** (sponsored segment) — a water-cooled/heated mattress
topper with per-side temperature control, sleep tracking, autopilot and a vibration alarm;
he reports better recovery (more low-heart-rate notifications). (3) **Exclusive first look
at the Nothing Phone 2's Glyph** — more addressable LED zones (33 vs 12; 16 in the top
strip), volume/timer/essential-notification indicators, third-party progress (Uber,
Zomato) and a glyph composer. Solo — persona/voice data; Eight Sleep segment is disclosed
sponsored.

## Key claims
- [2023-07-03] **Three levels of production** framework: level 1 = everything practical/real;
  level 2 = some elements virtual (green/blue screen, animation); level 3 = fully virtual
  (e.g. The Lion King, Avatar). The XR stage "blurs the line between level two and level three."
- [2023-07-03] XR-stage advantages over green screen: actors can see their environment;
  camera position is tracked (Red spy) so the background moves with real parallax; and
  **no green spill** — "there is spill, but it isn't green, it's just light that perfectly
  matches your environment" (plus set/ceiling lights).
- [2023-07-03] The multi-camera trick that "blew my mind": the 8K wall refreshes at 120fps,
  so two cameras each shoot 60fps on **alternating frames** — "one blinks while the other
  shoots a frame" — letting both see completely different tracked backgrounds
  simultaneously (demoed as a wall reading pure blue to one camera and pure green to another).
- [2023-07-03] He openly wants to bring some of this into his own studio ("I'm gonna try to
  figure out a way to make that happen here"), consistent with his production-craft obsession.
- [2023-07-03] **Eight Sleep Pod cover** (sponsored): water-cooled/heated topper, ±10 scale
  (~55°F to ~110°F), dual-zone for partners, sleep tracking (stages, heart/breathing rate),
  a monthly-subscription membership he "doesn't love," autopilot warming, and a silent
  vibration alarm. Personal signal it works: waking with 10–15 low-heart-rate notifications,
  read as better recovery. Code MKBHD for $200 off.
- [2023-07-03] **Nothing Phone 2 Glyph exclusive**: same design language, deliberately not
  dramatically changed ("you can't go dramatically changing it every time" for a young
  brand); **33 addressable LED zones** (up from 12), new volume indicator, a **Glyph timer**
  (good for the Pomodoro technique), third-party progress (Uber/Zomato signed up), an
  **essential notifications** corner light, and a Glyph composer for custom ringtones —
  still "on the borderline between gimmick and useful" but more customizable.
