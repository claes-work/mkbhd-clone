# 13" MacBook Pro 2016 Unboxing! (What's New) (@mkbhd, 2016-10-29)

## Summary
A "what's new" on the baseline (no-Touch-Bar) 2016 13" MacBook Pro. Familiar clone threads: the all-USB-C
"bet on the future" (now with *multiple* ports, a partial win), the dongle reality, laments for MagSafe
and the glowing logo, keyboard-travel preference, the "poop" webcam again — and a strong statement that
the maxed 15" Touch Bar model will be **his next mobile editing machine.**

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-10-29] **All-USB-C: "I agree about the future, but this is the present."** "Forget the full-size
  USB, HDMI, microSD, MagSafe — it's just USB-C and a headphone jack. This is a continuation bet from Apple
  that this is all we'll need in the future, and I agree about the future — but obviously this is the
  present, so you'll need adapters/dongles." The nuanced future-vs-present framing (the two/four ports fix
  his long single-port complaint from the 12" MacBook → [[2016-04-22-yt-amP7GVlUv2I]]).
- [2016-10-29] **Mourns MagSafe + the glowing logo.** "MagSafe was one of Apple's best inventions ever in
  my opinion, and it's gone with a USB-C charger. And there's no more glowing Apple logo on any Apple
  laptop now." Sentimental about beloved details lost to the redesign.
- [2016-10-29] **Keyboard-travel preference (2nd-gen butterfly).** "Gone are the chiclet keys — these are
  2nd-gen butterfly switches from the 12" MacBook; only a couple millimeters of travel, less tactile. I
  still personally prefer the old chiclet keys — I like more travel." A consistent personal-ergonomics
  stance.
- [2016-10-29] **The "poop webcam" persists — still a Pro machine.** "No SD card slot is a real bummer when
  you're calling it a Pro machine, and the webcam is still poop quality — sorry, that's probably not
  changing anytime soon." The long-running Apple-webcam gripe, now on the Pro.
- [2016-10-29] **Praises the industrial design + the brighter display.** "No taper, straight rectangles,
  sharp corners, hard lines — I'm actually a fan of this industrial design." The new display is "67%
  brighter, 67% better contrast, 25% more color — a really huge brightness increase you notice outdoors."
- [2016-10-29] **The Touch Bar 15" = his next editing machine (production).** "I'm definitely waiting for a
  maxed-out 15" MacBook Pro with Touch Bar — I played with it for less than an hour and I'm convinced,
  that's going to be my next mobile editing machine. I'm a Final Cut user; a whole timeline in the Touch
  Bar looks sick." A concrete production-workflow intention.
- [2016-10-29] **Buying logic vs. the Air.** "Even though it doesn't technically replace the MacBook Air,
  it one-ups it in every category — I'd consider this over the Air for a thin-and-light, even with 'Pro'
  in the name; just ignore that." Segments the recommendation.
