# HP Pavilion dv7t Media Center Remote Overview (@mkbhd, 2009-01-01)

## Summary
The earliest MKBHD upload with a usable transcript, opening with the line **"welcome to my
first video."** A 15-year-old Marques gives a **"very very short review"** of the **Windows
Media Center remote** bundled with his new **HP Pavilion dv7t** laptop — the tiny remote that
slots into the laptop's ExpressCard slot. He walks button-by-button through the remote (green
Media Center button, DVD button, media transport controls, an info "i" button he compares to a
BlackBerry menu, a back button "like an internet browser," volume/mute), admits he **hasn't used
every button** and asks viewers to explain the rest in the comments. Closes with an early version
of his signature CTA: **"leave comments and subscribe."** Origin-story material: the review
