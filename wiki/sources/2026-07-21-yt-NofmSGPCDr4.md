## Summary
A full-episode Waveform game show — the return of "Trivia Extravaganza" after roughly
11 months — with hosts Marques Brownlee, Andrew Manganelli and David Imel competing,
produced/hosted by Adam Molina with Ellis Roven and Rufus Milhout keeping score. Three

president, "Team Lawn Sports," "The Phone Zone"). Andrew wins the Podman's Cup with 391
points, eight ahead of David; Marques does not win. **Persona yield is low**: this is an
entertainment/format episode, not an opinion episode, and the auto-captions carry no
speaker labels — only the host's turn-taking callouts ("Marquez," "David," "Andrew")
give partial attribution.
