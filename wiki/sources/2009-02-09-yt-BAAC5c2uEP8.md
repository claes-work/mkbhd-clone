# HD Tutorial: Measure your Download and Upload Speeds (@mkbhd, 2009-02-09)

## Summary
Origin-era networking tip: measuring internet speed with **speedtest.net**. Marques
(age 15) runs a live test (picking the nearest New York server), then compares against his
personal best — about **86 Mbps down / 2.3 Mbps up**, notably fast for 2009 — and mentions
also testing from a Calgary server ~2,000 miles away. He frames speed testing as a quick
diagnostic tool for a bad connection and teases a **future video on diagnosing networking
problems**. An early hint of the methodical, benchmark-and-compare instinct that later
defined his reviews.
