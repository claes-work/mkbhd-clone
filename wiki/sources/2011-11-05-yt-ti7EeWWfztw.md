# Best Free Chrome Apps! (@mkbhd, 2011-11-05)

## Summary
The payoff to the Chrome Web Store tour ten days earlier: seven free apps he actually uses
or rates — Weatherbite, TweetDeck for Chrome, Audiotool, Webcam Toy, Good Food, The
Independent, and Wunderlist. Delivered with more comedic asides than a typical tutorial
(a MySpace runner, a self-deprecating cooking bit, a cutaway gag about getting lost in
Chrome games). Two things make it worth keeping beyond the app list: a **dated channel
milestone — his 401st video** — and a second explicit statement of **minimalism as a
personal aesthetic**, this time applied to software he chooses.
