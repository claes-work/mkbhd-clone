# Reviewed: ExtremePDA Case for LG Voyager [HD] (@mkbhd, 2009-04-23)

## Summary
An origin-era **hardware review** (Marques, age 15) — he explicitly calls it **"one of my first hardware
review videos"** — of the **ExtremePDA hard-rubberized case** for the **LG Voyager** (his Verizon phone),
the unit he had previously unboxed. He walks installation (snap the front and back on until the clicks),
then the case's cutouts: open button areas (send/end/clear), speaker opening, microSD + 2.5mm headphone-jack
slot, and open sides so nothing obstructs the keys. He flags four unexplained holes he couldn't identify
even after checking the maker's site (guesses ventilation or a belt holster), and one **con**: to charge
the phone you must remove the difficult-to-detach back. He concludes it adds little bulk, covers the
Verizon/LG branding, works with his headphones, and makes it his **new default case**. He notes he **is
using noise cancellation** here and his **new microphone**, and promotes his Twitter (updated a couple
times a day).
