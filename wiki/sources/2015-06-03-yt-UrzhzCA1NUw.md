# HTC One M9+ (& S6 Plus!) (@mkbhd, 2015-06-03)

## Summary
A look at the HTC One M9+ that lands a durable industry read: **"Plus is the new Prime."** The M9+ is a
China-only, objectively-better variant of the M9 (bigger QHD display, a fingerprint sensor, the M8's depth
camera) — a callback to his 2014 "Prime Phones: Explained" (market-specific variants you have to import).
He'd have loved to see it in US stores. Also teases the first internet photo of a rumored Galaxy S6 Plus.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-06-03] **"If the M9 and the Note 4 had a baby."** The M9+ adds a **5.2" QHD display** (bigger,
  brighter, sharper than the M9's 1080p), a **front fingerprint sensor** (touch-to-unlock, "as fast and
  accurate as the Apple/Samsung ones" — but not a pressable home button), and the **M8's depth camera** (a
  round-housing 20 MP + depth sensor for Duo-camera effects). Same battery, same BoomSound + excellent build.
- [2015-06-03] **"Plus is the new Prime."** Like the 2014 LG G3 Prime / Galaxy S5 Prime (market-specific
  variants), the M9+ is **China-only** — "if you want it, you'll have to import it" (dbrand helped with the
  import). "As far as HTC is concerned, this is what the Chinese market wants and the M9 is what the US
  market wants." (Consumer-education / market-variant literacy.)
- [2015-06-03] **A dated wish + a scoop.** "I would have loved to see the M9+ in stores here — it's
  basically objectively a better phone than the M9 in a couple of ways." Teases the **first internet photo
  of a rumored Galaxy S6 Plus** (from an anonymous source) — "a souped-up S6."
