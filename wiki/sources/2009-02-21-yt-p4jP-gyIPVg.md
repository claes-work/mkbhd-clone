# HD Vista Lite? (@mkbhd, 2009-02-21)

## Summary
A short origin-era **response video** (Marques, age 15), explicitly NOT a tutorial —
replying to another creator's video titled "Vista Light." On his **15-inch, 3 GB RAM**
laptop he opens Task Manager to show idle memory usage: about **1.4 GB used** while idling
with one browser open, climbing to a "surprisingly large amount" of the 3 GB with his
usual apps running (**Safari, Thunderbird, Firefox preloader**, a screen recorder, and a
Notepad document). He turns it into a **community prompt**, asking viewers to comment how
much RAM they use idling in Windows Vista. A small but characteristic example of his early
**community-engagement / crowd-comparison** instinct and his real-world workload.
