# Oppo Find 7a Review! (@mkbhd, 2014-04-19)

## Summary
A review of the Oppo Find 7a (the cheaper sibling to the Find 7). Continues the Oppo arc —
the build "reminds me exactly of the Find 5," and Color OS's theme store is his make-it-
your-own value applied. Notable for a clean **debunk of the "50 MP" software-stitching mode**
("law of diminishing returns — don't think of it as a 50 MP photo") and praise for VOOC fast
charging, with an honest weak-standby-time verdict.

## Key claims
- [2014-04-19] **The Oppo build consistency — reminds him of the Find 5**: "when the build
  materials are so dense — the whole glass front, the metal frame all around — the phone just
  screams quality, exactly like the Find 5." Continues the Oppo-build esteem (cf. the Find 5 review).
- [2014-04-19] **Color OS themer = make-it-your-own**: "out of the box I really didn't like how
  Color OS looks — but they have a built-in themer, a whole store of free themes for the launcher/
  home screen, so I have a Jelly Bean theme that resembles stock Android. It changes the feel of
  the OS just by refreshing the launcher." His customization value, applied.
- [2014-04-19] **Debunks the "50 MP" mode — law of diminishing returns**: "the 'Ultra HD' button
  is touted as 50-megapixel photos via software stitching. It kind of works — more detail on still
  subjects — but it's not as sharp as an actual 50 MP camera, and it doesn't look much better than
  a Galaxy S5 panorama. Don't think of it as a 50-megapixel mode; think of it as an enhanced-detail
  mode for still subjects, like HDR is an enhanced-dynamic-range mode." His spec-honesty / don't-
  believe-the-marketing-number habit.
- [2014-04-19] **Pleasant camera surprise + genuine credit**: "an excellent camera — I really
  didn't expect it to be this nice, coming from someone who's used the Xperia Z1, iPhone 5S, S5.
  The shutter is near-instant, and I'm a fan of the aggressive HDR." Credits an underdog's camera.
- [2014-04-19] **VOOC fast charging praised — with a teach-the-tech note**: "a beast of a 4.5-amp
  charger [vs 1–1.5 A for a normal phone] throws way more current through the battery — 0% to 13%
  in 5 minutes. You have to use the included brick." His explain-the-mechanism register.
- [2014-04-19] **Honest weak-standby-time verdict**: "the battery life wasn't all that great — the
  standby time was really weak compared to the M8 [which you could forget to charge overnight]. I
  attribute it to the Color OS ROM — they need to work on it." Names the real downside plainly.
- [2014-04-19] **Reads it as a Find-7 preview**: "this gives a good idea of how beastly the Find 7
  will be [2.5K display, Snapdragon 801, 3 GB]. I like the Find 5, I really like the Find 7a, can't
  wait for the Find 7." The Oppo-arc continuation.
