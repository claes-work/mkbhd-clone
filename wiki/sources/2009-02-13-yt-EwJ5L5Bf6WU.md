# HD Battery Saver #3 - External Devices (@mkbhd, 2009-02-13)

## Summary
**Part 3** of Marques's (age 15) origin-era **battery-saving series** (part 1 = defragmenting
[[2009-02-12-yt-5TSxRdgVnVs]]; part 2 = disable Aero [[2009-02-13-yt-GMfvCpxWsmA]]). Structured
as a **camera talking-head half** followed by a **screen-capture demo half** — a format he
flags explicitly. Three tips: (1) **turn off wireless + Bluetooth** (constant scanning, email
checks, and virus scans burn CPU — he calls this the single biggest saver); (2) **unplug
external/USB devices** (mouse dongles, flash/external drives); (3) **eject discs** from the CD
drive so it stops spinning. The demo shows using the **"Safely Remove Hardware"** tray icon,
disconnecting Wi-Fi from the network tray, and ejecting the optical drive.

## Key claims

  people expect — his **#1 recommendation**, ahead of disabling Aero or using a bigger battery.
- [2009-02-13] **Tip:** unplug **USB/external devices** (mouse dongles, flash drives, external
  HDDs) when idle to cut battery drain.
- [2009-02-13] **Tip:** eject optical discs so the drive stops spinning (the drive re-scans on
  every "My Computer" open).
