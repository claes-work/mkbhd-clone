# Moto 360 Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2014-06-27)

## Summary
Hands-on with the **Moto 360** at Google I/O 2014 — "the number one most requested thing" from his
Twitter and "easily the best looking thing that was unveiled at the show." The circular design is the
whole story, and Marques uses it to draw a durable **design-philosophy contrast**: LG/Samsung built
**square** watches for industrial durability, while Motorola chased a **premium, genuine-watch
experience** with a circular face, thin bezel, and a display laminated close to the glass. A clean dated
statement of "design as the reason a wearable succeeds," and his read that the 360 is the one that makes
a smartwatch feel like a real watch.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2014-06-27] **The only circular Android Wear watch**, and the only one not yet finalized/priced —
  Motorola showed "finalized hardware" but withheld price, specs, materials, and battery life (ships
  "later this summer"). Price guess: **$200–$400, likely the higher end** because it's "a more premium
  experience."
- [2014-06-27] **The "flat tire" explained (neutrally):** a small bar across the bottom of the UI houses
  the display drivers, which lets Motorola use a **much thinner bezel** all around than the square watches
  rather than a full surrounding bezel.
- [2014-06-27] **Fit and feel:** "it does look very good and it's super comfortable"; noticeably
  **lighter** than the LG G Watch he'd been wearing; a circle "takes up a lot less room on your wrist"
  while giving a larger diagonal display; one side button; likely wireless charging (no microUSB);
  interchangeable (but custom-tailored, high-quality) bands.
- [2014-06-27] **The design-philosophy contrast (the durable point).** The 360's display "just looks a
  lot better" — beveled edges, laminated closer to the surface — and "feels a lot more natural, like a
  genuine high-end watch." LG told him they went **square for industrial durability**; **Motorola was
  "more concerned about a premium experience,"** trying to get people who like high-end watches to like a
  high-end smartwatch.
- [2014-06-27] **The software demos match the G Watch** (same Android Wear, same demo mode) — the 360's
  differentiation is entirely the circular hardware. "I am very excited for when this comes out."
