# CES 2014 Gear Bag Tour! (@mkbhd, 2014-01-07)

## Summary
A ★★ **production-timeline** landmark — his full CES 2014 kit, itemized. The headline is
the camera upgrade to the **Canon C100** (from the 5D Mark III), plus his shotgun mic
(Sennheiser MKH416, "basically every video I've made in the past four months"), his editing
setup (13" retina MacBook Pro + Adobe Premiere), and the three phones he carries. The most
complete field-production snapshot to date.

## Key claims
- [2014-01-07] **Camera upgrade — the Canon C100 (main camera)**: "the camera I'm recording this
  with, the Canon C100, with a 17–55mm f/2.8 IS — that's everything I'm doing in terms of main
  visuals," plus a Tokina 11–16 f/2.8 for wide establishing shots. The step up from the 5D Mark
  III era (a production-timeline datum).
- [2014-01-07] **Audio chain — the MKH416 shotgun**: "the XLR Sennheiser MKH416 shotgun mic that
  I use for basically every video I've made in the past four months — it's an awesome mic," plus
  a Zoom H4n recorder, a Sanken COS-11D lavalier ("if I conduct an interview or need off-camera
  audio"), and the red-and-black ATH-M50s for monitoring. His itemized audio kit.
- [2014-01-07] **Editing setup — retina MacBook Pro + Premiere**: "the 13" retina MacBook Pro —
  don't judge me, it's a matte-black DecalGirl sticker — is what I use for on-the-go editing… I
  use Adobe Premiere, by the way," with a 2 TB Western Digital USB 3.0 external as a scratch disk
  (the MBP has only 128 GB internal). Confirms his mobile-editing rig + software.
- [2014-01-07] **The RX100 as his "beloved" run-and-gun camera**: "the Sony RX100 Mark I point-
  and-shoot, basically the best portable video I could have in my pocket — I recorded all of CES
  last year with the Mark I. Excellent, proven camera." Corroborates the RX100-as-production-gear
  thread.
- [2014-01-07] **The three phones he carries + Jaybird**: "my Galaxy Note 3 [secondary, for
  sharing on Twitter], plus the Stormtrooper black-and-white Nexus 5 and the iPhone 5S… and my
  Jaybird BlueBuds X, which I listened to while traveling." Corroborates the multi-phone reviewer
  setup + the Jaybird daily-wireless datum.
- [2014-01-07] **The run-and-gun philosophy — lightweight but capable**: "the whole idea of going
  to CES with camera gear is to keep it as lightweight as possible but still have as much
  production capability as you'd like." His minimal-but-capable field-kit ethos (Tenba Roadie 2
  backpack, chosen because it fits a fully-assembled C100).
- [2014-01-07] **Student note**: the retina MacBook Pro is "what I've been using for school." A
  dated college datum.
