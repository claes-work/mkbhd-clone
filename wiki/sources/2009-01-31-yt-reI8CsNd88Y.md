# HD Tutorial: Aerosnap for Vista (@mkbhd, 2009-01-31)

## Summary
A short origin-era **how-to**: 15-year-old Marques shows how to bring the **Windows 7
Aero Snap** window-management feature (drag a window to a screen edge to snap/resize it)
to **Windows Vista and XP** via a third-party utility he sourced from **askvg.com**. He
demonstrates snapping two windows side-by-side to "compare them instantly," maximizing by
dragging to the top, and restoring by dragging back off — praising it as a handy,
"cooler" way to manage windows. He notes the tool is "still a beta." Small production
tell: he says he'll post the download link in the description.

## Key claims
- [2009-01-31] **How-to: get Windows 7's Aero Snap on Vista/XP** using a third-party app
  (found via askvg.com), which lets you "minimize, maximize, and resize a window by
