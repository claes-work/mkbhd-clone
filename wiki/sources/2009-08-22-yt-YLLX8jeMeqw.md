# Tweet Your Youtube Videos [HD Tutorial] (@mkbhd, 2009-08-22)

## Summary
HD tutorial on linking a YouTube account to Twitter via YouTube's auto-share options, so
that uploads (and optionally ratings/comments/favorites) automatically tweet to
followers. Walks through configuring which actions get shared and notes his own privacy
choices.

- [2009-08-22] **Deliberate privacy configuration disclosed**: states he keeps ratings
  and comments set to private so they do not get auto-tweeted, while uploads still post
  — a self-reported, dated data point about his platform-configuration choices, not
  previously logged.
- [2009-08-22] **Cross-platform promotion as an explicit workflow**: frames the whole
  video around making it "a lot easier" for followers to know when a new video is done
  processing, even if they're away from YouTube — an early instance of deliberate
  cross-platform audience-reach strategy, precursor to the later multi-platform
