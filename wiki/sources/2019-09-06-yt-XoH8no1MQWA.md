# Dope Tech #20: Dual Screen Tech! (@mkbhd, 2019-09-06)

## Summary
Marques revives his Dope Tech series (which he frames as episode one of the relaunch) as a rapid-fire roundup of impressive, bleeding-edge, or high-end gear that reaches the studio but doesn't warrant its own dedicated video. He explains the format shift away from single-theme episodes toward a collection of standout items, comparing it to other creators' "favorite tech of the month/quarter" segments. The episode covers seven products: the ASUS ZenBook Pro Duo dual-screen laptop, the LG V50 with its dual-screen case, Sony's WF-1000XM3 wireless noise-canceling earbuds, the Sony RX100 VII compact camera, the Logitech MX Master 3 mouse, and the Shure SM7B podcasting microphone (paired with a Cloudlifter). Marques leans heavily into the dual-screen theme for the first two items, weighing the novel form factors against real-world usability compromises (a tiny relocated trackpad on the ZenBook; extra battery drain on the V50 case), and closes by tying the SM7B shout-out to the launch of his podcast.

## Key claims
- [2019-09-06] Marques is relaunching Dope Tech as a recurring series to showcase impressive tech that reaches the studio but doesn't get its own dedicated video; he calls this episode one of the relaunch.
- [2019-09-06] The ASUS ZenBook Pro Duo has a 15-inch 4K OLED display, an Intel 9th-gen 8-core CPU, RTX 2060 graphics, a 1TB SSD, and 32GB of RAM, plus full-size USB and USB-C but no card reader.
- [2019-09-06] The ZenBook Pro Duo's secondary "ScreenPad Plus" is itself a 4K IPS display measuring 3840 by 1100 pixels, coated in an anti-glare matte finish, and is a touchscreen that appears dimmer than the main display.
- [2019-09-06] Marques says the second screen's best use is window management (e.g., keeping Spotify, Twitter, or a files app persistently below the main workspace), and that ASUS adds software for switching apps between screens, a pinnable dock, and saved app layouts.
- [2019-09-06] The ZenBook Pro Duo's main compromise is a keyboard pushed to the bottom edge and a trackpad crunched into a small "postage stamp" in the corner; Marques notes buyers will likely use a mouse, and the laptop starts at under $3,000.
- [2019-09-06] The LG V50 is a fairly standard, under-the-radar $1,000 smartphone that now has 5G, a quad DAC, and a headphone jack; the phone alone wouldn't have made Dope Tech.
- [2019-09-06] LG's dual-screen accessory is a case with pins that connect to the phone and add a second display; Marques says it works surprisingly well and smoothly, lets you swap or sleep individual screens, and folds fully backward to deactivate the second screen.
- [2019-09-06] Marques says the LG dual-screen case does one thing better than any folding phone in his opinion — the springs, weight, and build quality — and compares its sideways video/text mode to a Nintendo DS.
- [2019-09-06] Marques compares the V50 case concept to the dot-matrix case HTC made for the HTC One M8, which he says was the first video he ever shot on a RED camera.
- [2019-09-06] The Sony WF-1000XM3 wireless noise-canceling earbuds are his new favorite wireless earbuds and, he says, the best-sounding wireless earbuds he has tried, on par with or better than the Jaybird Run.
- [2019-09-06] The WF-1000XM3 have touch controls, auto play/pause via a proximity sensor, multi-device connectivity (which Marques notes the over-ear version he reviewed lacked), NFC instant pairing, and app-based EQ; their main downside is lack of water resistance.
- [2019-09-06] Marques says noise cancellation on the earbuds is minor because most isolation comes from the passive seal, and notes noise-canceling AirPods may arrive possibly by the end of 2019.
- [2019-09-06] The Sony RX100 VII finally adds a mic jack, the one feature Marques says the RX100 line was always missing, making it a better point-and-shoot vlogging camera.
- [2019-09-06] Marques argues Canon has a habit of "shooting itself in the foot" by leaving out individual features that would make a camera amazing, whereas Sony does not.
- [2019-09-06] The Logitech MX Master 3 is a minor visual update over the MX Master 2S but improves the scroll wheel, offers per-app customizable button mapping, and switches to USB-C; he had used it for a week.
- [2019-09-06] Marques says the Shure SM7B (paired with a Cloudlifter for amplification because the mic itself is very quiet) is the mic he is now recording with; he notes it as the one Joe Rogan and many radio stations use, and ties the shout-out to launching his podcast.
