# LG V30: Top 5 Features! (@mkbhd, 2017-09-01)

## Summary
A ranked Top-5 of the LG V30 — "way better than any phone LG's ever made… the opposite of the Essential
Phone in every imaginable way." Where the Essential had *none* of the boxes, the V30 checks *every* box.
Clone-relevant for the checks-every-box framing, a rare "I don't mind this skin," and the **Cine (Log) video
mode** — a feature "literally made for enthusiasts to edit later," which lands squarely in his wheelhouse.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-09-01] **The deliberate foil: "the opposite of the Essential Phone."** "One is a minimal, bare-bones
  stock phone from a new company; this is a big, feature-packed, richly-enhanced flagship from an established
  one. It checks pretty much every box imaginable in a way no other phone this year has, except maybe the
  $1,000 Note 8." Frames it against the phone he'd just panned.
- [2017-09-01] **#5 It checks every box (the anti-Essential list).** microSD, wireless charging, fast
  charging, USB-C, IP waterproofing, a headphone jack **backed by a 32-bit quad-DAC** — "for $700 the
  Essential had literally none of these." And the fingerprint reader is **in the middle of the back, done
  right** ("if you're going to put it on the back, that's where it's reachable, Samsung") + doubles as the
  power button.
- [2017-09-01] **#4 "I really don't mind this skin" (a rare concession).** "I'm not the biggest Android-skin
  guy, so it takes a lot for me to say this — but I don't mind the V30's skin, largely because of how many
  features LG added (again, the opposite of Essential), with genuinely fluid high-frame-rate animations." His
  stock-Android bias, notably relaxed here. (An optional floating shortcut bar, remappable nav buttons.)
- [2017-09-01] **#1 The camera — and the Cine (Log) mode aimed right at him (production).** Dual cameras
  (16 MP f/1.6 OIS main + a **less-distorted** super-wide), a shutter-drag zoom, and a massive mode list. The
  standout: **Cine mode records to a flat log color profile to maximize sensor data / dynamic range so you can
  color-grade it later on a computer** — "if that's not screaming enthusiasm to you, I don't know what is."
  A phone feature that speaks his RAW/log-grading production language. "The ultimate enthusiast photo/video
  camera."
- [2017-09-01] **#2 The OLED display rivals Samsung.** "A 6" 2:1 (18:9 — 'or, since I learned fractions, 2:1')
  OLED that gets crazy bright, rivaling the best Samsung panels — the black bars on 16:9 content don't matter
  since OLED blacks go fully off. This alone would put it on my radar." (Pre-production caveat throughout.)

## Notable verbatim quotes
