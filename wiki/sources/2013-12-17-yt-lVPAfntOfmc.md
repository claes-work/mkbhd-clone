# Nokia Lumia 1520 Review! (@mkbhd, 2013-12-17)

## Summary
A review of the Lumia 1520 — his favorite Windows Phone of 2013 (and the first 1080p one).
Reinforcing, but notable for his **pixel-love driving the purchase**, near-flawless-hardware
praise, an honest "Internet Explorer is actually pretty good" concession, and a statement of
his **breadth-of-taste** (glad his favorite-phones list spans platforms). Also a gaming
datum ("now that I have an Xbox").

## Key claims
- [2013-12-17] **Pixel-love drove the purchase — the first 1080p Windows Phone**: "you guys know I
  like pixels, and yes that has something to do with it — this is the first 1080p Windows Phone, a
  big part of why I picked it up. At 6" 1080p / ~370 PPI it eliminates the pixel-visible problem I
  had on the 1020's 720p display." His display-primacy identity, driving a buy.
- [2013-12-17] **Near-flawless hardware — the honest superlative**: "this is nearly flawless
  hardware, objectively speaking — it even gets the little things right: speaker quality, earpiece,
  the massive 3400 mAh battery, OIS video, the button layout for a big phone. The fit and finish
  you expect from Nokia Lumia build quality." High praise, earned.
- [2013-12-17] **"IE is actually pretty good" — an even-handed concession**: "Internet Explorer on
  Windows Phone is actually pretty good — it may not have as many features as Chrome or Safari, but
  it's extremely fast, I didn't feel the need to scout out another browser." Credit where due, on a
  platform he doesn't use.
- [2013-12-17] **The bigger-display-helps-live-tiles insight**: "with a 1080p 6" display you get a
  lot more tiles — like widgets on Android, you can get all your glanceable info in one billboard-
  style view without scrolling. You flat-out can't do that on any other Windows Phone." Judges the
  spec by the experience it enables.
- [2013-12-17] **Breadth-of-taste — glad his favorites span platforms**: "this is one of my
  favorite phones of the year, and I'm glad it's a Windows Phone — I really love the diversity, the
  breadth of my favorite phones of 2013." His platform-agnostic appreciation.
- [2013-12-17] **Gaming datum — "now that I have an Xbox"**: "the game section is a lot more
  functional now that I have an Xbox." Corroborates the just-got-a-console thread (from a non-gamer).
- [2013-12-17] **The camera — as good as the 1020's experience**: "20 MP, Xenon flash, Carl Zeiss
  glass, the same Nokia Pro Cam app — extremely high-quality photos with a lot of control, and you
  can crop the 20 MP shots. 20 is still a lot of pixels." Optics-love, restated.
