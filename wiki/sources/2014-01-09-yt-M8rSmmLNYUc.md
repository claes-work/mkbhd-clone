# Sony Xperia Z1S & Z1 Compact at CES 2014! (@mkbhd, 2014-01-09)

## Summary
A short CES floor clip on two Sony phones — the Z1S (a T-Mobile-exclusive US Z1) and the
Z1 Compact. Low persona yield, but a notable endorsement of the **no-compromise-mini
approach**: unlike other makers' feature-stripped "mini" phones, the Z1 Compact packs full
flagship specs in a small body — answering a demand he'd noted.

## Key claims
- [2014-01-09] **Praises the no-compromise-mini — answering a real demand**: "a lot of people have
  been asking for a high-end flagship that's not huge — the Z1 Compact is exactly that: all the
  same high-end specs from the Xperia Z in a compact 4.3" body, not making the real sacrifices
  that other companies' 'mini' versions do [where they remove features and make a lower-end
  phone]." Credits Sony for a full-flagship-in-a-small-body, a category he flags as under-served.
- [2014-01-09] **The Z1S is just a US-localized Z1**: "only slightly different from the Z1 — 32 GB
  default, a couple of camera-software tweaks; if you want to know what it's like, watch my Xperia
  Z1 review, it's almost the same phone." Points viewers to his existing review rather than
  repeating.

## Notable verbatim quotes (Marques — voice data)
> "This is what a lot of people have been asking for — a high-end phone in a compact body, not
> making any real sacrifices like a 'mini' version does."
