# LG Optimus G Pro Review! (@mkbhd, 2013-05-27)

## Summary
A review of LG's first phablet — framed as essentially a **"Note 2.5,"** an LG one-up of
the Galaxy Note 2. Mostly a solid-phone verdict, but it restates two recurring beliefs:
**competition drives the industry forward** (the review's takeaway) and his
**change-to-improve-not-just-to-change** critique of LG's skin. He wouldn't personally make
it his daily driver.

## Key claims
- [2013-05-27] **"Competition drives us forward" — the review's thesis**: "these LG touches
  are all shots attempting to one-up the Galaxy Note 2, and I love that — it's the competition
  that drives us forward. This is basically the Galaxy Note 2.5." His pro-competition belief,
  stated as the framing.
- [2013-05-27] **Change-for-its-own-sake critique**: on LG flipping the menu/back buttons,
  "if you're going to change something just for the sake of changing it, it might as well be
  something small like that." And on LG's skin broadly, "LG next to Motorola and maybe Oppo
  have the most unconventional, far-out skin… I'm not a huge fan of the looks." His
  change-to-make-it-better standard, applied critically.
- [2013-05-27] **"So LG" — the one-word verdict, with a light touch**: "if I had to describe
  it in one word it would be LG — this phone is so LG, everywhere there's room for an LG touch
  you can expect one." A characterization device (cf. "it's a very Oppo device").
- [2013-05-27] **Credits full-auto camera performance**: "I found myself taking pretty much
  everything in full auto because auto does a great job on this phone — props to LG." Honest
  credit where earned.
- [2013-05-27] **Road-warrior battery praise**: "a full-weekend-road-trip companion… lasted
  two full days on a charge with normal use." His practical, lived battery testing.
- [2013-05-27] **Personal verdict — good phone, not his daily driver**: "I would recommend it
  if you're into the LG skin, but I personally wouldn't go for it as my daily driver — some
  animations don't feel right, and I'm not a fan of the looks of LG's skin." Separates the
  recommendation from his own preference.
