# Top 5 Most Underpriced Tech! (@mkbhd, 2013-02-19)

## Summary
The companion to the overpriced countdown — the best bang-for-buck deals in tech.
Persona value is in a few durable gear anchors (his own **ATH-M50s owned ~3 years, used
to edit every video**; the **Canon T3i** framed against the **T2i he started on**) and a
restated market thesis (the **Nexus 7 started the race-to-the-bottom** for good cheap
tablets).

## Key claims
- [2013-02-19] **#4 ATH-M50 — his own long-term editing gear, disclosed**: "I've had my
  pair for close to three years now… I use them to edit all my videos, so I know they
  sound really good… and the number one review happens to be my video of it." "$300–$350
  sound for under $180." A concrete production-gear + tenure datum, and disclosed self-
  interest.
- [2013-02-19] **#3 Canon T3i — anchored to his own origin camera**: "I started my video
  production with a Canon T2i, so of course the T3i is going to get super-awesome video
  quality just like the T2i I got… basically a T4i minus the touchscreen, or a T2i on
  steroids." Recommends from his own tool lineage; discloses "I'm not a big Nikon person,
  so check other reviews on the Nikon side" (bias transparency).
- [2013-02-19] **#5 Nexus 7 — the race-to-the-bottom thesis restated**: "the Nexus 7 pretty
  much takes credit for starting the 7-inch tablet movement… and the race to the lowest-
  priced good tablet you could make. Asus and Google nailed it; the Kindle Fire HD totally
  failed at the same price point." Form-factor + price-floor market analysis.
- [2013-02-19] **#2 Nexus 4 — value + honest about the sellout**: "$300 unlocked when other
  Snapdragon S4 Pro phones cost $500–600… and not because Google is manipulating the
  numbers — they're getting higher demand than expected." Names and dismisses a cynical
  read with evidence.
- [2013-02-19] **#1 Klipsch S4i earbuds — the surprise pick, reasoned**: "$60 for what are
  full-featured $130 earbuds… I don't know if they're selling at a loss or if their
  processes are that streamlined. Anytime anyone asks me to recommend earbuds, I say
  Klipsch S4i and they're happy a week later." Recommends from repeated real-world
  outcomes.
- [2013-02-19] **A retrospective-honesty habit**: closes by linking "a playlist of all the
  countdowns I've ever done, so you can go way back in time and see all the things my old
  self recommended." Keeps his past recommendations visible/accountable.
