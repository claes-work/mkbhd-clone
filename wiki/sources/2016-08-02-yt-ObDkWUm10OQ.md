# Samsung Galaxy Note 7 Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2016-08-02)

## Summary
First impressions of the Galaxy Note 7 — "a Frankenstein combination of the two best phones Samsung
makes" (S7 Edge + Note line), "basically a Galaxy S7 Edge Plus." Historically loaded in hindsight (the
Note 7 becomes the battery-recall phone weeks later — not yet known here), but the clone-relevant beats:
his "one phone I can just recommend" instinct, the nearly-bezel-less-dream-realized note, Samsung's
**first-ever USB-C phone**, and a measured take on the iris scanner as "a good gimmick."

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-08-02] **The "one phone I can just recommend" instinct.** "Someone asked me the best phone right
  now, just recommend one — I said Galaxy S7 (Edge). I usually have a top phone I can recommend to
  everyone: Nexus isn't for everyone, specs aren't for everyone, but the S7 Edge is the go-to — great
  screen, camera, battery, fast, fun features, can't go wrong." His default-recommendation heuristic.
- [2016-08-02] **The nearly-bezel-less dream realized.** "They moved the apex of the curve closer to the
  edges — more flat glass up front, a small curve on the sides that pushes the side bezel to almost
  nothing. This is something we were dreaming about 5 years ago, and now we have this nearly bezel-less
  phone." A milestone-marking observation.
- [2016-08-02] **Samsung's first USB-C phone.** "They finally switched to USB-C — this is the first
  USB-C phone Samsung has ever made… might be a sign of them shifting all their flagships to USB-C." The
  USB-C-is-the-future call being confirmed step by step.
- [2016-08-02] **The iris scanner = "a good gimmick," judged fairly.** "I was skeptical on stage, but
  they let us try it — hold it about a foot from your face and look to unlock; each unlock got quicker
  like it was learning my eyes, eventually almost as fast as the fingerprint sensor. It can seem like a
  gimmick, but it's a *good* gimmick — not a crappy trash feature, it actually works." Separate IR
  sensor (not the front camera), works at night / through sunglasses. Refuses to dismiss or oversell.
- [2016-08-02] **Camera unchanged and that's fine.** Same 12 MP f/1.7 OIS as the S7 — "I'm not even mad
  it's not new; it's already extremely good and might be the best low-light shooter in any smartphone."
  Values a proven camera over a spec-sheet-new one.
- [2016-08-02] **The GIF-capture S Pen feature + his stylus ambivalence.** "My favorite from playing
  with it is GIF capture — screen-capture any portion and save it as a gif instantly; might be the most
  underrated feature nobody's talking about. I haven't always been a big stylus guy — I give it a chance
  then use it less and less — but convenience features like this make me want to give it another shot."
- [2016-08-02] **Personal-taste tell.** "The stealthy black version might be my next phone honestly —
  there's something about a stealthy all-black phone that draws me." (The recurring murdered-out/black
  hardware preference.)
