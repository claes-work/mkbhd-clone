# Nexus 6P & 5X Impressions! (@mkbhd, 2015-09-30)

## Summary
Hands-on with the new Nexuses from Google's event — the flagship-hardware for **Android 6.0 Marshmallow**.
The **Huawei-built Nexus 6P** is the standout: an all-metal, chamfered, surprisingly-light (178 g) 5.7" QHD
AMOLED phone that "**fills up the spec sheet and checks a lot of boxes**" — dual front-facing stereo
speakers, USB-C **with fast charging** ("thank you"), a rear fingerprint reader (Nexus Imprint), and a
top-ranked 12 MP camera even without OIS. His one open question: how cases will handle the rear
fingerprint.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-09-30] **Three devices, Marshmallow.** The Nexus 5X, Nexus 6P, and Pixel C, all carrying Android
  6.0. The **6P (Huawei) = the flagship**, successor to the Nexus 6.
- [2015-09-30] **The 6P checks the boxes.** "It fills up the spec sheet and checks a lot of boxes: high-end
  CPU (Snapdragon 810) / GPU, all-metal build, big bright 5.7" QHD AMOLED, **dual front-facing stereo
  speakers**, AC Wi-Fi, LTE-A, NFC, **USB-C with fast charging — thank you**, a 3450 mAh battery despite
  looking thin, and a new 12 MP f/2.0 camera (no OIS) that was just top-ranked by DxO." A big improvement in
  hold over "the whale of the old Nexus 6" — light, chamfered metal, soft-touch, thin bezels.
- [2015-09-30] **Rear fingerprint (Nexus Imprint).** A back circle rated ≤600 ms; "the placement was
  confusing at first but makes sense — a good place for your index finger to rest," and it wakes+unlocks
  from sleep (like the OnePlus 2). "The one question mark: how cases will address this, other than cutting a
  hole in the back."
- [2015-09-30] **USB-C-with-fast-charging = the fix.** Explicitly relieved they paired USB-C with fast
  charging (the thing the OnePlus 2 lacked): "USB-C with fast charging — thank you."
