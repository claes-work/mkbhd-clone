# Sony Xperia Z1 Review! (@mkbhd, 2013-10-21)

## Summary
A review of the Xperia Z1 — "a refined, tuned-up Xperia Z." Reinforcing, but a clean
restatement of two threads: the **design-vs-rugged contradiction** he first named on the
Xperia Z (the IP58 sacrifices), and **display viewing angles as the one-critical-flaw** ("the
one thing you have to look at when you use your phone"). Also a megapixel-myth note (20 MP ≠
top-5 camera).

## Key claims
- [2013-10-21] **The design-vs-rugged contradiction, restated**: "it still has the same problem as
  the Xperia Z — it doesn't know if it wants to be ruggedized or not. To be IP58 [dust/water], it
  covers every port with flaps, has a permanent screen protector, and a much worse speaker… but
  does anyone really need that in a phone? If it weren't IP58 certified, it'd be a lot more
  appealing to me." His diagnose-the-product's-core-tension habit.
- [2013-10-21] **Display viewing angles as the one-critical-flaw**: "the display was the one thing
  I could never get over, because it's the one thing you have to look at when you're using your
  phone — the permanent protector [for IP58] gives it poor viewing angles, washed out by ~30° off
  axis." Display-primacy + one-critical-flaw.
- [2013-10-21] **Megapixel-myth, restated**: "a lot of people look at 20 megapixels and assume
  top-five camera — it's actually not, it's like number six. The sensor is big and Sony makes great
  optics, but the image processing is meh and colors are dull." Spec ≠ result (optics-over-
  megapixels thread).
- [2013-10-21] **Even-handed on the skin + battery praise**: credits Sony's camera manual mode and
  exposure dial ("the perfect button layout for taking pictures like a boss"), and "one of the
  better standby times of any Android device I've used." Praises what's genuinely good.
- [2013-10-21] **Honest consistency — you'll feel the same as about the Z**: "if you liked the
  original Xperia Z you'll love the Z1; if you're in the same boat as me where the software and
  looks weren't for me, you're probably not going to like the Z1 either." Sets expectations by his
  own prior verdict.
