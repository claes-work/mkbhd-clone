# HD Tutorial: Audio Converter [Why YOU need one] (@mkbhd, 2009-07-15)

## Summary
Tutorial for the DVDVideoSoft Free Studio Manager's audio converter, framed around a
concrete production use case: syncing audio recorded in Audacity with video edited in
Camtasia Studio, since Camtasia doesn't accept WAV or WMA input. Along the way he states
a dated audio-quality opinion and confirms his working post-production audio pipeline.

## Key claims
- [2009-07-15] **Dated audio-quality opinion**: "I really do believe that Audacity gives
  you better audio quality than if you were to record with Camtasia Studio 6" — states
  he records unboxing voiceovers in Audacity and imports the audio into Camtasia rather
  than recording directly in Camtasia, a concrete production-methodology preference.

  Maker won't accept WAV/WMA from Audacity or Windows' built-in Sound Recorder — the
  free audio converter bridges the gap by converting to MP3, which both apps accept.
