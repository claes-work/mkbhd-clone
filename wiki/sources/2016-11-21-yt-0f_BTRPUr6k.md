# OnePlus 3T Review: T is the new S! (@mkbhd, 2016-11-21)

## Summary
A "what's new" review of the OnePlus 3T — "T is the new S," an iterational spec bump on the OnePlus 3 six
months later. Clone-relevant: an explicit **restatement of the fill-the-body-with-battery doctrine** ("I've
been preaching about this"), the observation that **OnePlus picks up where Nexus left off** (value flagship),
and the first mention this era of the **Smartphone Awards 2016** being in the works.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-11-21] **"T is the new S" — the iterational-refresh analogy.** "The 3T is to the 3 as the iPhone 6s
  was to the 6 — an iterational spec bump in the exact same design; you wouldn't know it's new by looking."
  A clean framing device (and OnePlus did it on purpose — "T comes one after S, one better than S, one-upping
  Apple; well played").
- [2016-11-21] **THE FILL-WITH-BATTERY DOCTRINE, restated as a personal crusade.** "My favorite change: they
  fit a much larger battery (3,400 vs 3,000 mAh) in the exact same dimensions. That is legit — I've been
  preaching about this: if you can make a phone battery as big as possible, even if the phone's a little
  thicker, you should do it." The anti-thin/battery-first doctrine named as something he actively campaigns
  for. (Same thread as the S7 vindication [[2016-03-17-yt-1sgeM6DsV40]].)
- [2016-11-21] **OnePlus picks up where Nexus left off.** "OnePlus is picking up where Nexus left off before
  Pixel — people were excited for a mid-to-high-end phone with lightweight/near-stock Android for a really
  low price. Now the Pixel is $650, so if you want that Android experience for ~half ($400), the 3T is the
  way to go." A market-positioning read (value flagship niche).
- [2016-11-21] **Anti-planned-obsolescence defense of the OnePlus 3.** "People said OnePlus 3 owners should
  be mad their phone is 'obsolete' six months later — no it's not; it's still one of the best budget phones
  of the whole year, and OnePlus is giving it the Nougat upgrade too." Defends the older model; anti-churn.
- [2016-11-21] **Storage-over-expandable, from personal use.** "128 GB now (double the 3) — that alleviates
  some expandable-storage concerns; I keep my entire Spotify library downloaded for flights, and 128 was
  great for me." His own use case justifying a spec.
- [2016-11-21] **Smartphone Awards 2016 teased.** "The Smartphone Awards 2016 is coming up if you haven't
  noticed — that's something we're thinking a lot about." First signal of the year-end awards this era.
  "Dash charging is still the fastest charging in the world, I'm pretty sure." The 3T gets his thumbs up.
