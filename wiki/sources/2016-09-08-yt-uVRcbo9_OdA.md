# iPhone 7 Impressions: 10 New Things! (@mkbhd, 2016-09-08)

## Summary
His "10 new things" walkthrough of the just-announced iPhone 7 / 7 Plus. A "very familiar iPhone" with
targeted upgrades; the clone-relevant beats are the **jet-black warning**, the **solid-state (Taptic)
home button**, the **7 Plus 2× telephoto dual camera** as his favorite upgrade, **Apple finally killing
the 16GB tier**, and the screen as his biggest letdown.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-09-08] **"A very familiar iPhone" with subtle improvements.** Same shape/dimensions/feel as the
  6/6s; the antenna bands no longer cross the back (cleaner top-and-bottom look) — "this might be one of
  those things where it's the only way you can tell someone has a new iPhone."
- [2016-09-08] **The jet-black warning + the matte-black love.** The new **jet black** is a glossy piano
  finish "I don't think I can seriously recommend anyone — probably the slipperiest iPhone, super prone
  to fingerprints and scratches; one in the demo area already had micro-scratches, and Apple itself
  recommends a case, which I think is lame." But the **matte black** — "something about it just speaks to
  me; I'll be grabbing that." (His black-hardware preference, with a taste distinction.)
- [2016-09-08] **Solid-state home button (Taptic).** "The home button doesn't actually click anymore —
  it's a solid glass pressure-sensitive button with a Taptic Engine behind it, like the Force Touch
  trackpads in the new MacBooks. May take some getting used to, but it's definitely new."
- [2016-09-08] **Dual camera = his favorite upgrade (7 Plus).** The 7 Plus has two 12 MP cameras: a
  normal ~28 mm lens + a ~56 mm lens = **2× optical zoom**, better for portraits/telephoto, with an in-
  app button to switch lenses. "My favorite upgrade is probably the camera on the 7 Plus — which is the
  phone I was going to get anyway." Both phones get OIS, a new ISP, quad-LED flash, and RAW capture.
- [2016-09-08] **Apple finally killed the 16GB tier — "my favorite upgrade" of the base spec.** "They
  killed the 16GB version finally — now it starts at 32GB at the same price, up to 256GB." A direct win
  for his long-running 16GB-needs-to-die campaign.
- [2016-09-08] **Biggest letdown: the screen.** "My biggest letdown is the screen" — same size,
  resolution and IPS tech (now ~25% brighter with a wider color gamut, "so colors pop, almost like an
  OLED — but not"). The OLED wish from the dummy-unit video ([[2016-08-12-yt-0Ae0YwmgYTY]]) went unmet.
- [2016-09-08] **Water resistance, stereo speakers, and the jack removal.** Officially IP-rated water/
  dust resistance; stereo speakers (bottom grille + the earpiece as the second channel — "I put stereo in
  air quotes, weird setup, no idea how good until I test it"); no headphone jack → ships with Lightning
  EarPods + a $9 Lightning-to-3.5mm dongle ("Apple joins Motorola in shipping every phone with an
  adapter"). AirPods introduced at $159. A10 Fusion chip (quad-core, two low-power cores) for "an extra
  hour or two of battery."
