# Audio Technica ATH-M50X Review! (@mkbhd, 2014-02-15)

## Summary
A review of the successor to his long-beloved ATH-M50 ("the best headphones under $200,
period"). Reinforcing — the main change is a **removable cable**; the sound takes an "if it
ain't broke, don't fix it" approach. Restates his long-term M50 use (his monitoring/editing
default) and gives honest upgrade advice.

## Key claims
- [2014-02-15] **The successor to his long-standing pick**: "these are the successor to what I
  called the best headphones under $200, period — the M50s have been among the highest-rated pairs
  out there." Confirms the M50 as his standing recommendation.
- [2014-02-15] **The main upgrade — a removable cable**: "the biggest difference is the cabling —
  the M50x comes with no cable attached and three included cables [1.2m straight, 3m straight, 1.2m
  coiled, my favorite], color-matched to the headphones. That's likely why they cost a bit more
  ($189 vs sub-$120)." A concrete, honest feature delta.
- [2014-02-15] **"If it ain't broke, don't fix it" on the sound — his iteration motto**: "with the
  audio quality and sonic signature they took an if-it-ain't-broke-don't-fix-it approach — the M50s
  had an excellent balanced rich sound, and the M50x have that exact same sound. I love that." His
  standing iteration principle, applied.
- [2014-02-15] **Long-term gear tenure — his monitoring/editing default**: "I've been using the
  M50s for a number of years now — traveling, airplanes, audio monitoring while recording, video
  editing, just about everything. That's a testament to how great the sound is." Corroborates the
  M50 as his production default (cf. the CES gear bag, headphone countdowns).
- [2014-02-15] **Honest upgrade advice — don't upgrade unless you need the cable**: "if you have
  the M50s, this might not be a huge reason to upgrade unless you really want the removable cable —
  and there's a removable-cable mod for the M50s at a lower price." His don't-upsell-you honesty.
- [2014-02-15] **A small Beats jab (built into the design)**: "because of the locking mechanism you
  can't use third-party cables — you can't use those Beats by Dre cables." His standing Beats aside.
