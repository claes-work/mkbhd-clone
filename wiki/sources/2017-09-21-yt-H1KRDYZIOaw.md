# iPhone 8 Unboxing: Silver vs Gold! (@mkbhd, 2017-09-21)

- [2017-09-21] **"I swear this is an iPhone 7s."** "The accessories are exactly the same as last year — a wall

  cases fit. Most changes are inside." Reiterates the honest naming call (cf. the impressions video).
- [2017-09-21] **The glass back = Qi wireless charging (the one big external change).** "Here's the glass-

  gold'; no red, no jet black yet.)

  it's an off-white cream, matching the cream back of the phone; the paperwork inside is still white. The more
  you know." A characteristic small-detail observation. (And the standard fingerprint note: only space gray
  has the black front bezels.)
