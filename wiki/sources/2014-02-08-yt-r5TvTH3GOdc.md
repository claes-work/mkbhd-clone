- [2014-02-08] **The HTC One as a build-quality standard-setter**: "the HTC One was one of my
  favorite phones of 2013, hands down — amazing specs, excellent speakers, and a build quality
  that set a new standard for what high-end smartphones should feel like, that unibody aluminum. So
  the successor has some seriously big shoes to fill." Restates his HTC One esteem (cf. the Top-5-

- [2014-02-08] **The successor's must-keeps**: "it's expected to keep that legendary unibody

  it the successor." Identifies the phone's identity-defining features (build + BoomSound).

  the M8 with software onscreen buttons, which would mean a slight design change [the One had big

- [2014-02-08] **Leak-culture / spec rundown, hedged**: "the rumored specs are a little less than
