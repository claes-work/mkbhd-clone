# HD Tutorial: Remove Background Noise (@mkbhd, 2009-04-02)

## Summary
★ A production/audio-craft screencast (Marques, age 15) teaching **background-noise removal
two ways**. In **Audacity**: select a few seconds of silence/white noise → **Effect → Noise
Removal → Get Noise Profile**, then select the whole track and apply removal, dragging the
amount toward "less" as a rule of thumb and previewing before committing. In **Camtasia
Studio 6**: import the camera file to the timeline, apply his **HD preset**, highlight the
speaking region, then **Edit → Audio Enhancements → auto-detect noise → Remove Noise** (with
a **Restore Noise** undo). He explicitly notes he'll **use this on the very video you're
watching** — an early, self-aware statement of audio post-processing in his own pipeline, and
that the goal is to let viewers **"focus exactly on my audio."**

## Key claims
- [2009-04-02] Documents his **origin audio-post pipeline**: **Audacity** (Get Noise Profile →
  Noise Removal) and **Camtasia Studio 6** (Audio Enhancements auto-noise-detect) for cleaning
  background/white/"computer running" noise.
- [2009-04-02] Audio-craft rule of thumb: **select a pure-noise sample first** so the tool
  learns the profile, and **preview before removing**; drag removal toward "less" to avoid
  artifacts.
- [2009-04-02] Applies noise removal to **his own uploads** ("I'm going to try to use it for
  this video too") so viewers can **"focus exactly on my audio"** — early evidence of
  deliberate production quality.
- [2009-04-02] Confirms the era's toolchain: **Camtasia Studio 6 with a personal "HD
  preset,"** cross-referencing his HD-preset tutorial.
