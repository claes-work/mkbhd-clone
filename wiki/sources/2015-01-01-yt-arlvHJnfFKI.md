# MKBHD Update 10.4: 2015! (@mkbhd, 2015-01-01)

## Summary
A New Year's update that doubles as a **2014 year-in-review** and a statement of his creator philosophy.
Headline stats: **93 videos, 130 million views, passed both 1M and 2M subscribers**, and — his favorite
figure — over **1,000 years of cumulative human watch time**. He sets a resolution that restates his core
ethic: **"make every new video my best work… every new project is an opportunity to do your best work"**
(the "best video, not first video" ethic, positively framed). Also previews his selective CES-coverage
approach and an in-progress **MKBHD.com** website.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-01-01] **2014 by the numbers:** 93 videos uploaded in 52 weeks, **130 million+ views**, crossed
  **1 million and 2 million subscribers**, ~7-min average length, and "the most mind-blowing statistic —
  more than **1,000 years of human life** spent watching the videos I made last year."
- [2015-01-01] **Favorite video of the year:** the **Smartphone Awards 2014** ("a fun, unique thing to
  close out the year").
- [2015-01-01] **Selective CES philosophy.** "I don't cover everything, I don't run around trying to make
  a thousand videos — when I find something cool I'll tweet it, and if it's really cool I'll make a video."
  He leans on viewer tweets to find what to cover. (Quality/curation over volume.)
- [2015-01-01] **The resolution = the ethic.** "My New Year's resolution is to make every new video my
  best work — that might not mean I make a ton of videos, but every new project is an opportunity to do
  your best work." (Positive framing of "best, not first.")
- [2015-01-01] **What's coming in 2015:** an **MKBHD.com website** in progress ("taken longer than I
  thought… I want to get it right") and teased videos in the pipeline.
- [2015-01-01] **CES predictions (tame):** lots of 4K TVs, and the first year of "**beyond 4K**" (6K/8K)
  in quantity; more first-gen wearables.
