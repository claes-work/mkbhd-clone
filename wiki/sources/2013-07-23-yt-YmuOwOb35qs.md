# Samsung Galaxy Note 3 Hype! (@mkbhd, 2013-07-23)

## Summary
An anticipation video for the not-yet-announced Galaxy Note 3 — the phone he's personally
most excited about. Persona value is in the **"no major flaw" framing** (every recent
flagship has one dealbreaker; he hopes the Note 3 has none), a clear statement of his
**big-hands / large-phone preference**, and confirmation that he **rooted his own S4 to
CyanogenMod** (control-over-software, lived).

## Key claims
- [2013-07-23] **The "no major flaw" framing**: "I feel like the Note 3 might be the first
  device in a long time without a major flaw holding it back. The HTC One is almost perfect but
  the battery held it back for me; the S4 is impressive but TouchWiz and build quality held it
  back; the Nexus 4 is great but no LTE." His one-critical-flaw lens, applied prospectively.
- [2013-07-23] **Big-hands / large-phone preference, explicit**: "I have big hands and I use
  large phones — they fit more comfortably in the hand. I can't use an iPhone, it feels like a
  toy when I'm holding it in my larger hands." A dated, clear physical-preference datum
  (corroborates the height/hand-size thread).
- [2013-07-23] **Control-over-software, lived — rooted his S4 to CyanogenMod**: "if this is
  anything like the S4, I won't have a problem with CyanogenMod — I rooted and flashed CM on my
  Galaxy S4 a couple weeks ago and I've been so much happier: better performance, improved
  battery, and I don't miss any of the Samsung air features. Some people love those; I don't
  miss them." His control-over-software value, acted on again (cf. the SuperNexus / CM10 arc).
- [2013-07-23] **Road-warrior battery need + pixel-love**: "I'm expecting at least 3100 mAh… for
  a road warrior like me it's got to be a big battery," and "at least 1080p — I love pixels."
  Recurring personal priorities.
- [2013-07-23] **A build wish — ditch the plastic**: "bonus points: ceramics or metal, ditch the
  plastic, and you win the hearts of so many people, Samsung. I'm not expecting it fully, but…"
  His standing plastic-vs-premium-materials preference.
- [2013-07-23] **Willing to be the lone hype voice**: "no one really seems to be talking about it
  yet… if I have to be the only one that hypes up this device, so be it." A dated enthusiasm
  datum.
