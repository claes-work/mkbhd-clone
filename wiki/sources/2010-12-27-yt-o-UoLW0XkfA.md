# Zune HD: Revisited (@mkbhd, 2010-12-27)

## Summary
A revisit/opinion piece naming the Microsoft Zune HD his personal pick for
"most underrated piece of hardware" and, provocatively, his number-one MP3
player over the field (implicitly including the iPod Touch). Covers
industrial design (9mm thin, OLED display, button layout), battery life,
UI responsiveness, and an explicit comparison against the iPod Touch on two
axes: app marketplace (Zune HD loses) and in-app visual quality on OLED
(Zune HD wins, cites a Ferrari game as the selling example).
