# Tech Channel of the Week #2 — TheCompuGeeks (@mkbhd, 2009-05-10)

## Summary
The second entry in Marques's origin-era "Tech Channel of the Week" series (age 15), spotlighting the Irish YouTube channel TheCompuGeeks. He walks through their channel page on-screen, praises their output cadence, and directs his viewers to go subscribe — a pure creator-to-creator cross-promotion with no giveaway involved. The notable new dimension versus #1 is that he explains the *qualification mechanic* for the series: the best way onto Tech Channel of the Week is to subscribe to him (he then visits each new subscriber's channel to see if they're a fellow tech reviewer), "not asking." This makes the series legible as an early community-building / creator-discovery engine built on reciprocal subscription.
