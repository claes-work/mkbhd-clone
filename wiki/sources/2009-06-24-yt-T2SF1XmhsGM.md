# mkbHD Update 3.0 (@mkbhd, 2009-06-24)

## Summary
The channel's first true "state of the channel" video, opening a new Update numbering
(leaving the 2.x series behind). Four load-bearing announcements: (1) he has just
finished **sophomore year of high school** and is entering summer vacation with ~2
months free to make videos; (2) he plans to **apply for YouTube Partnership this week**
and film the application process as a video; (3) he explicitly **puts a cadence
decision to a viewer vote** — upload once a day (current pace, ~300–450 views/video) or
once every other day — reasoning through the tradeoffs on camera; (4) he pre-announces
a **giveaway of 8–10 products** he's received from sponsors (iFrogz, Griffin, AO, SAA)
as thanks to subscribers, timed to either a partnership approval or a 2,000-subscriber
milestone, "whichever comes first." Closes by thanking subscribers for a **1,500-subscriber
milestone hit "about 3 days ago."** ★ L3-candidate — strongest single source in this
batch for both `biography` and `creator-business`.

## Key claims
- [2009-06-24] **Biography**: "I'm officially done with sophomore year of high school
  and I have about... 2 months of summer vacation" — dates the school-year boundary
  precisely to late June 2009.
- [2009-06-24] **YouTube Partnership pursuit begins**: "I actually plan on applying for
  a YouTube partnership this week," with a planned companion video showing the
  application process itself — content-about-the-channel-business as a format.
- [2009-06-24] **Cadence decision outsourced to viewers**: reports "like a video a day
  for about 2 months now," getting "300 to 450 views per video," and explicitly asks
  "should I upload a video once a day or once every other day... you guys decide" —
  the clearest early instance of a programming/business decision put directly to
  audience vote.
- [2009-06-24] **Content-mix reasoning stated on camera**: acknowledges a split
  audience ("a lot of you guys subscribed for the hardware and a lot of you guys
  subscribed for the software") but commits to keeping both on one channel and
  alternating "hardware, software, hardware, software."
- [2009-06-24] **Giveaway pre-announced**: "a massive giveaway... eight or nine or ten
  a lot of products that I've received" from iFrogz, Griffin, AO, and SAA, tied to
  "a partnership giveaway or a 2,000 subscriber giveaway... whichever comes first."
- [2009-06-24] **Milestone**: "thank you to the 1,500 subscribers that I just got about
  3 days ago" (dates the 1,500-subscriber mark to ~2009-06-21), and mentions he has
  been tweeting the milestone and asks viewers to follow him on Twitter.
- [2009-06-24] **Upcoming-videos-on-channel-page mechanism reused**: repeats the
  practice (first noted 2009-06-08) of listing upcoming videos on the channel page,
  pointing viewers there for the giveaway teaser.
