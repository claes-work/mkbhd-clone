# HD Tutorial: Get Old Menus Back in Vista Explorer (@mkbhd, 2009-02-08)

## Summary
Origin-era tip aimed at users migrating from Windows XP to Vista, which hid the classic
File/Edit/View menu bar in Windows Explorer. The "ridiculously simple" fix: tap **Alt** to
summon the menus for a single action. Marques (age 15) tests whether the same trick works
in **Word 2007**, finds Alt instead surfaces the ribbon's keyboard-shortcut overlays, and
promises a follow-up on restoring classic menus to Office 2007. An early example of him
testing a claim on camera rather than asserting it.

## Key claims
- [2009-02-08] **How-to:** press **Alt** in Windows Vista Explorer to temporarily bring
  back the classic File/Edit/View menus; they persist for one action, then hide again.
- [2009-02-08] **On-camera test (dated):** the Alt trick does **not** restore menus in
  Word 2007 — it triggers ribbon keyboard shortcuts instead; he flags a future video for
  the Office 2007 case.
