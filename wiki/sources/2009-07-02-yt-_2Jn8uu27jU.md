## Key claims
- [2009-07-02] **Dated channel stats**: 230 videos, 202,000 video views, 40,000 channel
  views, 1,570 subscribers — a precise, dated snapshot for the biography timeline,
  landing 9 days after the 1,500-subscriber mark cited in Update 3.0.
- [2009-07-02] **Rejection history disclosed for the first time in the corpus**: applied
  previously with this same account and was denied twice — once at ~90 videos and again
  at ~150ish videos, both times for "Not Enough original content." Also mentions
  applying once with a separate, "completely blank" account and being denied within 24
  hours for "not enough viewership" — three prior denials total across two accounts
  before this attempt.
- [2009-07-02] **Partner-program mechanics explained on camera**: revenue sharing via
  ads, custom channel banners, and raised upload limits (larger files, higher bitrate
  than 256k, videos longer than 11 minutes) as the three benefits motivating the
  application.
- [2009-07-02] **Application-form content disclosed**: reports "more than 100 videos,"
  average length "5 to 10 minutes," upload frequency "more than once a week," self-
  describes his content mix as both hardware and software.
- [2009-07-02] **Cross-reference note for reviewers**: names other creators as linked
  accounts if under 18 ("Tyler's computer show," "the Tolby Tech Man," "Fred") —
