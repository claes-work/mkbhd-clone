# Slate Truck First Look: The $25,000 Modular EV! (@AutoFocus, 2026-06-24)

## Summary
Marques's **first hands-on and first drive** of the **Slate Truck**, a bare-bones,
modular, customizable electric pickup with a ~$24,950 headline price. The core idea he's
drawn to: pick exactly which "bells and whistles" you want (power windows, speakers,
displays, wraps, even bolt-on roll cages that convert it to an SUV) and pay for nothing you
don't. He tours the intentionally minimal "blank slate" base truck, several wrapped/modular
variants, and reports the drive was surprisingly peppy and confident, with well-tuned
one-pedal driving. He's openly "rooting for it." Marques-solo (Slate staff present but not
speaking on camera).

## Key claims
- 2026-06-24: Base "blank slate" **Slate Truck** starts at **$24,950** — slate-gray from the
  factory, rear-wheel drive, ~65 kWh battery charging up to 120 kW on a NACS port, **181 hp,
  ~205 miles range.** No paint/power-windows/speakers/displays by default. (Marques)
- **Modularity is the story**: every truck is the same gray base; all colors are **vinyl
  wraps** (~$500–$700 for a full wrap, vs. "$5–6,000" elsewhere); bolt-on modules add a roll
  cage (with built-in airbags) and a second seat row to turn it into an SUV; the only
  guaranteed hardware is minimal AC controls and one small gauge display. (Marques)
- He got to be "one of the first humans... outside of the company" to ride in and drive it;
  found it "surprisingly peppy" with "plenty of torque," compact and lower to the ground than
  a full-size truck, with **full one-pedal driving on by default, tuned nicely** ("close to
  what Tesla does"). Suspension a touch firmer than expected. (Marques)
- Positioning: "a fleet truck" / great first EV — not for towing or road trips, but ideal for
  someone who wants to start minimal and upgrade later. Calls the buy-cheap-then-customize
  model "legitimately a brand new idea for EVs." (Marques)
- Closed lockable front trunk highlighted as a genuine pickup need the Slate addresses.
  (Marques)
