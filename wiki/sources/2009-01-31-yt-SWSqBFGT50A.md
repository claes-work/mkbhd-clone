# Unboxed: NZXT Cryo LX [HD] (@mkbhd, 2009-01-31) ★

## Summary
Marques's **first hardware unboxing** — he says so explicitly on camera: "this is my
first Hardware unboxing." A 15-year-old Marques unboxes the **NZXT Cryo LX**, an
all-aluminum laptop cooler with three large fans and a four-port USB hub, bought from
**Newegg** to cool his **17-inch HP Pavilion dv7t** (his existing $10 plastic cooler
didn't fit and "does not even passively cool"). Filmed with no dedicated camera yet — he
holds the product to the webcam — the video is a milestone for the **unboxing format**
that becomes a channel staple. Recurring gag: the aluminum is "freezing cold" from
January weather, so he keeps marveling at how cold it is. He promises a follow-up review
with **SpeedFan** temperature comparisons "in the description," and wishes he had "a
camera to review some" properly — an early tell that he already wanted better production
gear. Caption note: he says "120 mm fans" mid-video but "320 mm" once (the smaller figure
is the plausible one for a laptop cooler).
