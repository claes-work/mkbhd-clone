# How to Upload HD Youtube Videos (@mkbhd, 2009-01-31) ★

## Summary
An **origin-defining** how-to from the channel's first days: a 15-year-old Marques walks
through his end-to-end pipeline for getting a **720p HD** video onto YouTube in early
2009, when HD on the platform was still a novelty. The workflow: record the screen with
**CamStudio** (DivX 6.8.7 codec, 100% quality, ~30 fps, 720p profile), assemble in
**Windows Movie Maker**, publish to **Windows Media HD 720p** at **1280×720, ~5.9 MB/s**,
and upload the resulting AVI. He explains that the video first appears in "terrible
quality" until YouTube finishes processing and the **"Watch in HD"** link appears. This
video is the earliest explicit statement of the obsession that becomes MKBHD's whole
brand — the **letters "HD" in the name stand for exactly this**: pushing the highest
picture quality YouTube allowed. He signs off saying he'll "use it to make the rest of my
tutorials." ★ **L3-candidate** — canonical production-quality/origin material for the
next synthesis pass.
