# HD Tutorial: Fastest Youtube Uploader (@mkbhd, 2009-06-23)

## Summary
Tutorial for DVDVideoSoft's free YouTube uploader, pitched explicitly as an alternative
to browser-based uploading for other creators — he names his own audience composition
while explaining the pitch: "a lot of my audience is actually other tech reviewers and
software posters who... would like some alternative to the YouTube uploading way."
Walks through sign-in, video selection, title/tags/description/category entry, and a
live (aborted) upload demo, closing with a concrete speed comparison against Chrome's
native uploader.

- [2009-06-23] **Audience self-description**: "a lot of my audience is actually other

  are creators/reviewers themselves, not just consumers.
