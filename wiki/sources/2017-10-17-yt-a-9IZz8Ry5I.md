# Google Pixel 2 Unboxing! (Best Android 8.0 Features) (@mkbhd, 2017-10-17)

## Summary
A Pixel 2 XL unboxing that doubles as an Android-8.0-Oreo software showcase — his thesis that on the Pixel,
**software is the differentiating factor.** Clone-relevant for the "polish = the little 'oh, that's nice'
things" framing and the standout Now Playing feature.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-10-17] **"The differentiating factor is the software."** "Using this and the previous generations,
  it's obvious the differentiator when picking a Pixel is the software — Android 8.0 Oreo is full of little
  things and features you might call polish, but to me it's just things that make you go 'oh yeah, that's
  really nice, good thing they thought of that.' The hardware isn't the most stunning, but there's a lot of
  cool stuff in software." His software-first read of the Pixel's value.
- [2017-10-17] **Now Playing = "pretty freaking incredible."** "It proactively tells you what song is playing
  around you before you even ask — using the always-on mics + on-device ambient services, matching against
  tens of thousands of songs (usually enough since you hear popular songs in coffee shops). It shows on the
  lock screen; tap to play on YouTube or add to Spotify." An on-device-ML feature he clearly rates.
- [2017-10-17] **The clean Settings app (a small-thing that matters).** "Different skins handle Android's
  ever-growing feature count differently; the Pixel nests the less-used ones — super clean, consistent theme,
  a search that works really well, white-and-blue, easy on the eyes. I'm a big fan." His appreciation of
  reducing-overwhelm.
- [2017-10-17] **The bezels get less ugly — and may be functional.** "The more I look at these bezels, the
  less ugly they look — and I feel they may be necessary for the squeeze feature to activate the Assistant."
  An impressions-vs-use softening + a functional read (defends the Pixel 2 XL's bezels). Notes the soft-touch
  metal "repels fingerprints surprisingly well." Full review incoming.
