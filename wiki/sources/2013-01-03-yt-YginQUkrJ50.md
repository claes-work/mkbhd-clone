# Samsung Galaxy Note 2: Revisited! (@mkbhd, 2013-01-03)

## Summary
A two-months-later revisit of the Note 2, answering the most-asked follow-up questions after it became
**his most popular review video ever** — and confirming it as **his daily driver**. Rich in biography
and voice: a dated height marker, a rare family reference (his mom on camera), the "stay geeky my
friends" sign-off, and a clean restatement of the stock-vs-skin resolution (he'll root it into a
SuperNexus once the black one ships).

## Key claims
- [2013-01-03] **Note 2 confirmed as his daily driver for two months**: "I've carried a Galaxy Note 2 as
  my daily driver for these two months." The device timeline advances past the SuperNexus/S3 to the Note
  2 (Verizon), stated plainly.
- [2013-01-03] **It became his most popular review video ever** — a dated channel milestone: "it's even
  become my most popular review video ever. Damn, that's a lot of people."
- [2013-01-03] **The stock-vs-skin resolution, restated crisply**: "when given the choice I prefer stock
  Android, no skin — but if I have to choose a skin, I'll choose TouchWiz pretty much every time." And the
  plan: "when the black one comes out I absolutely will root and put CyanogenMod 10.1 on it and run a
  SuperNexus with Android 4.2" (this is the titanium one, so not yet). Control-over-software in one line.
- [2013-01-03] **The form-factor-offers-an-experience verdict**: "it's popular for more reasons than just
  specs and what's on the inside — the form factor offers an experience that no other smartphone currently
  does, and I can still recommend it for that reason." The form-factor-matters thesis applied to the
  phablet.
- [2013-01-03] **Height marker (dated, minor discrepancy)**: "I'm about **6'2**" and I've been told I have
  really large hands." (The wiki elsewhere carries ~6'3"; treat as ~6'2–6'3".) He can one-hand the 5.5"
  Note 2; "quite difficult to sneak a text maybe during class" — a college marker.
- [2013-01-03] **A rare family reference (context, not persona)**: he shows the phone "in my mom's hand —
  she's about 5'5" and was quoted saying this is simply too big to be a phone." Used to illustrate the
  hands-size point; **his mother is context, not the subject** (fidelity rule 6).
- [2013-01-03] **Stylus mostly for gesture typing**: "I just now use it occasionally — one for gesture
  typing on the Jelly Bean keyboard, that's been my biggest use… and S Note to take random notes during a
  phone call." Consistent with the on-camera stylus reversal ([[2012-10-12-yt-VdsMwGWvhO4]]).
- [2013-01-03] **The "stranger magnet" observation**: "I've never gotten so many questions from strangers
  about my phone… be prepared to answer questions from strangers in public." A dated cultural note on the
  phablet's novelty.
- [2013-01-03] **Sign-off catchphrase, dated**: "I don't always get asked about my phone, but when I do,
  it's because it's a Note. **Stay geeky, my friends.**" A dated voice marker (a Most-Interesting-Man
  parody).
- [2013-01-03] Camera/battery reconfirmed: "great photos, I share them all the time on Twitter"; "battery
  life still lasts hours beyond my expectations"; and "I did put a sticker over the ugly Verizon home
  button branding — I had to do that."
