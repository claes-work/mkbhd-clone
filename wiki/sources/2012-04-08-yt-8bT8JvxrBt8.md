# Limited Edition ATH-M50 Giveaway (with Duncan33303) (@mkbhd, 2012-04-08)

## Summary
A short collaboration giveaway with Duncan33303 — limited-edition ATH-M50s, cross-entry across both
channels, same mechanic as their December 2011 holiday giveaway. Two things worth recording: a dated
production fact (the M50s are the monitors he edits on, with a video count attached), and an explicit
**impersonation warning** telling the audience exactly how to verify a message from him is genuine.

## Key claims
- [2012-04-08] **Production disclosure with a number**: the ATH-M50s are "beastly studio monitors that
  I've used to **edit my past 100 or so videos**" — a dated statement that his review gear is also his
  working gear, and a rough marker of output since he adopted them.
- [2012-04-08] **Impersonation warning — audience-safety practice**: "I will pick one winner and message
  them **from my personal account**. So if you get someone messaging you from someone who's pretending to
  be me on maybe my second account — **I don't have a second account** — you will have to get a message
  from me on the day that the winner's picked in order to win. I'll let you guys know who won on Twitter
  that day too." He anticipates giveaway scammers and gives a verification rule.
- [2012-04-08] **Cross-entry mechanic repeated**: both channels post on the same day, one comment across
  the two videos is selected, entries open one week (closing April 15th) — the same structure as
  [[2011-12-04-yt-7-2FR0m9DM4]], now an established collaboration format with Duncan33303.
- [2012-04-08] Product note: the limited edition is "basically identical to the standard pair that we've
  used, but with a slightly sexier outer shell" — cosmetic-only difference stated plainly rather than
  hyped.
- [2012-04-08] Peer credit: the M50s are named as "one of the favorites of fellow YouTuber Austin from
  Duncan33303."
