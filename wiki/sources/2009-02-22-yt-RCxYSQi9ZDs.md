# HD Tutorial: Camstudio Lossless Codec v1.4 (@mkbhd, 2009-02-22)

## Summary
An origin-era production how-to (Marques, age 15) showing how to obtain the **CamStudio
Lossless Codec v1.4** from **camstudio.org** for viewers still using CamStudio as their
screen recorder — pitched as a fix for people who had come to him with DivX-codec problems.
Method: scroll ~halfway down camstudio.org, grab the **v1.4 EXE** (he prefers the
executable over the zip — "one step less"), run the installer, and it auto-installs into
CamStudio. He closes by literally showing his on-screen list of pending video ideas,
underscoring the early daily-upload, request-driven cadence.
