# Top 5 Most Overpriced Tech! (Pt 2) (@mkbhd, 2016-03-02)

## Summary
Part 2 of his **"most overpriced tech"** — a value / consumer-education franchise calling out the worst
deals. Headliners: **super-speed 10,000-RPM hard drives** ("the worst of both worlds" now that SSDs exist),
and a **$6,000 "luxury smartphone"** with mid-range specs — "overpriced by a lot… I was going to say it's

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-03-02] **#1 — super-speed (10K-RPM) hard drives.** "Normal hard drives are a pretty good deal, but
  the 10,000-RPM 'super-speed' ones are now literally the worst of both worlds: still slower than any SSD,
  also less durable and reliable than an SSD, and they don't match the density of a big 3.5" HDD they cost so
  much more than. Just make up your mind — a big slow HDD or a fast SSD."
- [2016-03-02] **#2 — the $6,000 luxury smartphone.** He reads off the specs (Snapdragon 801, 3 GB, Android
  4.4 KitKat, 5" 1080p, 20 MP) and the price ($6,000): "**overpriced by a lot** — I was going to say it's
  the Beats by Dre of smartphones, but the markup is even more insane." Their selling point: "uncompromising
  quality in every detail, down to the stitching." (A value-mismatch / paying-for-a-brand critique.)
- [2016-03-02] **New-studio note.** The inspiration: "in the background of my Galaxy S7 Impressions video,
  that easel TV — the crazy super-expensive Samsung S9W TV from CES two years ago." (Confirms he's filming
  in the new studio.)
