# Daily Tech: Mid 2016! (@mkbhd, 2016-07-12)

## Summary
A "what I actually use every day" video (a video-response to Mr Mobile / Michael Fisher). A goldmine of
concrete personal-gear + workflow data circa mid-2016: his two-SIM daily-driver system, why an iPhone
always rides shotgun, his editing rigs (maxed black 15" MacBook Pro + 2013 "trash-can" Mac Pro), the
lens-mount bracelet, and his everyday headphones. Directly persona-eligible biography/appearance/voice.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-07-12] **The two-SIM daily-driver system (definitive statement).** "My main SIM (the number
  everyone has) is AT&T and lives in my main phone; a secondary AT&T SIM goes in whatever phone I'm
  testing. If I test a phone and love it, it gets promoted to the main SIM and the secondary goes back in
  my iPhone. Right now my main SIM is in a **OnePlus 3** — I switched from the Galaxy S7 Edge because I
  preferred OnePlus's software — with the iPhone 6s Plus as the secondary." (Canonical workflow node.)
- [2016-07-12] **Why the iPhone always comes back as secondary.** "The iPhone tends to be better with
  camera-related apps that *aren't* the camera app — Snapchat, Beme, Instagram — anything shot on a phone
  that didn't happen in the camera app works better on iOS. That's why there's always an iPhone in my
  other pocket." A durable, specific cross-platform judgment.
- [2016-07-12] **The editing rigs.** Main laptop = "the 15-inch MacBook Pro, fully maxed out, painted
  black by Colorware (the Dope Tech Customs episode)" — used to edit in Final Cut *on the go* (I/O, WWDC).
  Desktop = "the 2013 Mac Pro… wow, saying that out loud makes you realize how long since they refreshed
  it — this thing is due for an upgrade" (8-core trash-can, 64 GB RAM, dual D700, Final Cut). His main
  editing rig. (Production + a dated Apple-neglects-the-Mac-Pro gripe.)
- [2016-07-12] **The lens-mount bracelet (identity detail).** "The band on my left wrist all the time —
  it's not even tech; it's a Lens bracelet that happens to be pretty much the same size as a Canon EF lens
  mount." A signature personal-appearance detail.
- [2016-07-12] **Smartwatch = a travel/battery-saving tool, not an everyday.** "I don't wear a smartwatch
  that often — when I do it's usually because I'm traveling and trying to save phone battery by checking
  notifications on my wrist. Android side = Moto 360; iOS side = Apple Watch (Android Wear on iPhone isn't
  the same experience)." Reinforces wearable-as-accessory.
- [2016-07-12] **Everyday headphones.** "The red Audio-Technica M50 (M50RD) have been my dailies for a
  very long time — classics, great price, great sound — I use them for editing"; Bose QC35 for flying;
  and "I still use the Jaybird X2 over the new Freedoms for just walking around." (Notable: prefers the
  *older* X2 to the newer Freedom he'd just reviewed — the anti-churn stance in his own habits.)
