# Grado SR80e: Dope Budget Headphones! (@mkbhd, 2015-12-12)

## Summary
A holiday audio recommendation and follow-up to his open-back explainer: the **Grado SR80e** — "a great
first pair of open-back headphones," from **"a pretty underrated brand plenty of people haven't heard of."**
An evolution of the SR80i he reviewed 3 years ago; a hand-built, old-school design that "saved money on the
build and put it into the audio," under $100. An audio/gear datum + champion-the-underrated (Grado).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-12-12] **An actual recommendation, not just a demo.** Unlike the pairs in his open-back explainer,
  "this is an actual recommendation and a good place to start" for open-back headphones.
- [2015-12-12] **Underrated brand, quality sound.** "Grado — a pretty underrated brand; plenty of people
  haven't even heard of them, but they're quality." An evolution of the SR80i (from ~3 years ago), "a bit
  more balanced." The open-back soundstage is wide, detailed, natural, "punchy and rich."
- [2015-12-12] **Build-where-it-matters.** A simple, hand-built, lightweight design (a leather headband
  strip, no big cushion, removable on-ear foam pads) — "you can tell where they saved money on the build and
  put that into the audio quality." Open-back caveats (sound leaks out; ambient sound gets in).
- [2015-12-12] **Sub-$100, a great gift.** "All over the internet at sub-$100"; a slightly cheaper SR60e
  exists (~$20 less). "A great buy and an easy gift for a future audiophile in your life, or yourself."
