# HD Tutorial: Youtube Groups (@mkbhd, 2009-07-23)

## Summary
HD tutorial explaining the (then-new-to-him) YouTube Groups feature and walking through
the MKBHD group he created for new YouTubers seeking exposure. Explains how he moderates
submissions (reviewing, rating, commenting, sometimes subscribing) and how to create a
group, and states current group membership (104 members).

## Key claims
- [2009-07-23] **Explicit audience-development mechanism**: created the MKBHD YouTube
  group specifically "for people who are starting out on YouTube and uploading their own
  videos and who actually want more exposure" — states he reviews submitted videos, rates
  and comments on them, and sometimes checks out and subscribes to the poster's channel —
  a concrete, structured community-building tool distinct from the TCOTW segment format.
- [2009-07-23] **Moderator recruitment as a trust signal**: says he adds people he already
  knows or whose channel he likes as group moderators when they join — an informal
  reputation-based delegation mechanic.
- [2009-07-23] **Dated group size**: 104 members as of 2009-07-23.
- [2009-07-23] **URL/naming constraint noted**: couldn't use `youtube.com/mkbhd` for the
  group URL because that path was already his backup channel, so used
  `youtube.com/channel/MKBHDgroup` instead — a small concrete detail about his footprint of
  backup/secondary YouTube channels at this point.
