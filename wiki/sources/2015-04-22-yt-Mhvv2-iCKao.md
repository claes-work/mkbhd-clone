# Samsung Galaxy S6 Review! (@mkbhd, 2015-04-22)

## Summary
The full Galaxy S6 review — "not only the best Galaxy S yet, actually one of the best smartphones out there,
period." Three headline verdicts: it has **the best camera in any Android phone (tied with the iPhone 6
Plus for best overall, edge for stabilized 4K)**, **the best screen on any smartphone**, and — finally —
**TouchWiz is fast** (performance is no longer the problem it always was, even if the aesthetics still lag).
The cost: a glass sandwich that lands it in his "**top-five slipperiest phones of all time**," and lost
microSD/removable battery.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-04-22] **Best-in-class camera.** 16 MP f/1.9 OIS + 4K; "the best camera in any Android smartphone,
  period — tied for the best overall experience alongside the iPhone 6 Plus, and I even give it the edge
  because it shoots stabilized 4K." Fast, simple UI; double-tap-home opens it in "literally under a second"
  (so fast it fires in his pocket — "plenty of pictures of the inside of my pocket").
- [2015-04-22] **Best display, period.** 5.1" QHD Super AMOLED, 577 ppi — "big, bright, beautiful… almost
  looks like paper," crazy bright outdoors (rare for OLED), with contrast/saturation toggles.
- [2015-04-22] **TouchWiz is finally fast (the turnaround).** Samsung promised the lightest TouchWiz yet and
  delivered — "performance has been a problem with it in the past; that is no longer the case. Everything is
  buttery smooth, the multitasking menu is the fastest I've ever used, near-zero lag." Aesthetics still
  "a face only its mother could love," and gripes remain (S Voice he never wants, the slow Briefing page,
  always-lit capacitive buttons). But the keyboard is now "my favorite of any Android skin," and the once-
  "painfully slow" gallery is fixed.
- [2015-04-22] **Build: real materials, but top-5 slipperiest.** A Nexus-4-like glass front/back with
  chamfered metal sides (great in hand), but 6.8 mm and glossy glass = "probably top-five slipperiest phones
  of all time — it slid out of my pocket sitting down and almost off a tilted binder," and a fingerprint
  magnet ("the fastest I've ever wanted a skin on the back"). Much-improved **touch-ID home button** (touch,
  not swipe). Lost microSD + removable battery (first Galaxy S without them).
- [2015-04-22] **Control-over-software, as the coping mechanism.** "I've become willing to let a lot of the
  minor [TouchWiz] stuff go because this phone is so consistently fast… beyond the way some icons look, I can
  grab a custom launcher, keyboard, icon pack, theme — I can really make this my own and it'll still be crazy
  fast." "It's pretty **dope**."
