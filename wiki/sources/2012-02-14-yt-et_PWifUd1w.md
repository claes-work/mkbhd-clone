# Beats Pro vs Audio Technica ATH-M50! (@mkbhd, 2012-02-14)

## Summary
A head-to-head between the **$160 ATH-M50** and the **$400 Beats Pro** that lands a verdict he
visibly expects to be unpopular: **the cheaper pair sounds better.** Build, adjustability, comfort,
ear-cup shape, cables and accessories are compared category by category before he gets to sound —
and then he separates "swag" from every other axis, conceding the Beats' looks generously while
refusing to let looks carry the verdict. Contains the earliest **"$400 fashion accessory"** critique
in the corpus, 2½ years before the channel's canonical Beats video.

## Key claims
- [2012-02-14] **The verdict, delivered with visible bracing**: "to be perfectly honest, the M50 sound
  better than the Beats Pros. I thought I was going to get punched in the face for saying that."
- [2012-02-14] **The fashion-accessory critique — earliest instance**: "do you know how many people I
  see walking around like this… seriously, they're not even plugged in. You're making these a $400
  fashion accessory, a $400 necklace. Please don't do that." Predates
  [[2014-08-31-yt-ZsxQxS0AdBY]] ("The Truth About Beats by Dre!") by 2½ years.
- [2012-02-14] **Concedes the rival's strength without hedging** — and flags that he's doing it: "okay,
  I got to — I can't do this anymore. These are amazing looking headphones. They are hands down classy.
  Bravo… if you value that category at all, yeah, these are excellent headphones. But in every other
  category it's the M50s that take the top mark."
- [2012-02-14] **Where value actually lives**: "that's really where the value comes in — when you really
  decide how much you're going to spend on headphones and where you're going to put your money."
- [2012-02-14] **A conditional recommendation that states the full price of choosing the rival**: buy
  the Beats Pro if you value the rock-solid build, "don't mind paying a little extra for the name,"
  don't like the M50s' looks, "and you don't mind the slightly overpowering bass or the lack of mids or
  some of the comfort issues and you don't mind the price premium." Every tradeoff named in one
  sentence.
- [2012-02-14] Category-by-category findings: the M50s adjust further and stay comfortable "for hours
  on end"; the aluminium Beats Pro is sturdier but "still hurt my head after listening for a shorter
  period"; the Beats' **rigid circular ear cup "is not the natural shape of the ear"** while the M50's
  oval cup fits better; the Beats win on a **removable cable with two jacks** enabling daisy-chaining.
- [2012-02-14] **A feature dismissed for who actually uses it**: the swivelling DJ ear cups — "a special
  feature that DJs around the world will probably never use… I've honestly never seen someone buy a
  pair of headphones for this feature."
- [2012-02-14] Sound description for the M50s: "very balanced, very accurate, very crisp, punchy sound,
  very rich bass… excellent studio monitors for people who want to listen to music and hear things
  they've never heard in the song before that were mixed in."
- [2012-02-14] **Growth note, unquantified**: thanks "all the people who have subscribed over the past
  couple of weeks — it's been quite an interesting growth pattern."
