# Beats By Dre Studio Review (@mkbhd, 2010-10-13)

## Summary
A deliberately "casual" review (explicitly contrasted with his usual "highly edited
professional videos," which he says are returning soon) of the white Beats By Dre
Studio noise-cancelling headphones, provided by Monster Cable. Covers packaging,
hardware tour (removable cable, AAA-battery noise cancellation, adjustable fit),
compares the bass/sound signature to his earlier Solo HD review, and closes by
naming these his new permanent editing headphones and cross-promoting a fellow
reviewer's video of the same product.

## Key claims
- [2010-10-13] **Explicitly frames this as an off-cadence "casual" review**, contrasted
  with his normal "highly edited professional videos" — an early, direct acknowledgment
  of format/production-quality variance and its cause (limited time / an injured
  shoulder that day).
- [2010-10-13] **Comparative sound judgment against his own prior review**: notes the
  Solo HD's bass was "really hot" — good for music, "not really too good for studio
  monitoring" — while the Studio's lows/mids/highs are "very very balanced," a direct
  product-to-product comparison anchored in a previous review rather than an abstract

- [2010-10-13] **Adopts the product as his own production gear**: "these will be my
  permanent editing headphones for MKBHD videos from now on" — hardware chosen for a
