# The State of 4K: Early 2015! (@mkbhd, 2015-01-26)

## Summary
The annual **"State of 4K"** — a landmark statement of his **4K-adoption thesis**. His verdict: "2014 was
quietly the best year yet for 4K" (not wearables), because 4K got dramatically easier on **both ends —
recording and watching**. He tracks the **commoditization** (his Asus PQ321Q 4K monitors fell from ~$3,500
to ~$1,500 in a year), the 5K iMac, cheap 4K cameras/GoPro, and 4K delivery via Netflix + YouTube. A
production-craft + industry-thesis source (and a nice creator meta-note: last year's "State of 4K" was
itself shot on a 1080p camera upscaled).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2015-01-26] **2014 = the best year yet for 4K** (quietly, while everyone called it the year of the
  wearable). YouTube began officially supporting 4K uploads ~a year prior and "things have heated up a lot."
- [2015-01-26] **Watching 4K got easier + cheaper (commoditization).** His **Asus PQ321Q** monitors were
  ~$3,500 each a year ago; now ~$1,500 — "less than half… that's been the general trend as 4K displays and
  TVs get commoditized." His CES prediction (lots of 4K TVs) came true; he links panels he can recommend.
  The **5K iMac** (5120×2880, "4× a 27" QHD monitor") — "I love it… awesome for video/photo editing."
- [2015-01-26] **Shooting 4K got easier too** — cheaper 4K handycams/action cams, the new 4K GoPro, and
  "a ton of smartphones that can shoot in 4K, so regular people can shoot 4K." (Ties to CES highlights.)
- [2015-01-26] **The pieces connect via Netflix + YouTube** — Netflix streams 4K shows shot in 4K;
  YouTube supports it well ("a ton of YouTubers, including the ones I was hanging out with at CES, are
  rocking 4K cameras and uploading 4K, including yours truly").
- [2015-01-26] **Honest about the bottleneck that remains** — he can't see how many viewers actually
  toggle 4K, and bandwidth/processing + "half of you are watching on your phone" limit real 4K reach.
  Creator meta-note: **last year's State of 4K was shot on a 1080p camera and upscaled.**
