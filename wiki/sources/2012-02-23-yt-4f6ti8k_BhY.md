# Best iPad 3 Features! (@mkbhd, 2012-02-23)

## Summary
A rare pre-announcement rumor video — flagged as an exception to his own practice ("I almost never do
rumor videos") — about why an avowed non-iOS user is nonetheless excited for the next iPad. Two
reasons only, and he says so when he runs out. The page's real value is a single line: **he explains
the "HD" in MKBHD as a statement about loving pixels**, tying his channel name directly to the
display-primacy position promoted at synthesis pass 8.

## Key claims
- [2012-02-23] **The channel name explained as a display statement** — the standout line: "if you
  probably already know me, I'm MKBHD, and the HD, if you didn't already know, stands for high
  definition, which basically means **I love pixels. Lots and lots of pixels.**" A direct link from his
  own branding to the display obsession documented across this era.
- [2012-02-23] **Reason 1 — the retina display**, framed through his own cross-platform history: he
  liked it on the iPhone 4/4S but "the screen was a little bit on the small side," and "apparently
  Google agreed" when the Galaxy Nexus shipped a 4.65" 1280×720 panel. "High pixel densities… are
  inherently awesome, and on tablets they are super awesome."
- [2012-02-23] **The pixel-count correction, done on camera**: the rumored 2048×1536 is twice the
  horizontal *and* twice the vertical resolution of the iPad 2's 1024×768 — "but that's not twice the
  pixels. If you do the math — basic math, people — that is **four times** the pixels. So I'm four times
  as happy for that display."
- [2012-02-23] **Reason 2 — quad-core**, argued from his own recent review: summarizing the Transformer
  Prime "in a word or two: it's fast, really really fast" ([[2012-01-22-yt-WBdJ6X1hp-U]]). He predicts
  Apple will call the chip "the A5X or the A6, one of those two," and notes "Apple is typically the
  flagship for this kind of stuff."
- [2012-02-23] **Names the limit of his own list, rather than padding it**: "number three — actually,
  you know what, I think that's about it. I'm not a huge iOS guy, as you guys probably already know."
- [2012-02-23] **Conditional review commitment**: "will I be picking this up and reviewing it if I feel
  it's worthy? Absolutely. Will I be comparing this to the Transformer Prime that I love so much?
  Absolutely."
- [2012-02-23] **Refuses the era's most reliable easy content**: "if it comes with Siri, will I be asking
  it [silly] questions and hoping that it will get a witty answer that I can post to YouTube? No. No.
  Sorry. Just no." A dated statement of what he won't make for views.
- [2012-02-23] **Format boundary stated twice**: "I almost never do rumor videos, but let's just do this
  one" / "as you know, I don't always do rumor videos."
