# Samsung Galaxy S5 Review! (@mkbhd, 2014-04-16)

## Summary
The full Galaxy S5 review — confirming the "**Galaxy S4S**" verdict from his impressions.
Reinforcing: a solid incremental flagship whose main (and almost only) downside is TouchWiz,
capped by an explicit wish for a **Google Play edition** (his stock-Android preference).

## Key claims
- [2014-04-16] **"The Galaxy S4S" — confirmed**: "right now the Galaxy S5 really is the Galaxy S4S
  that I called it in my first impressions video." His incremental read, validated after full
  testing (the fingerprint-scanner miss notwithstanding).
- [2014-04-16] **TouchWiz is the main downside**: "the only real downside I have with the Galaxy S5
  is TouchWiz and its quirks, and I guess the speaker a little bit too. TouchWiz will continue to
  evolve." His stock-preference, framed as the review's core reservation.
- [2014-04-16] **The explicit wish for a Google Play edition — stock-Android preference**: "I'm
  just going to sit back and quietly cross my fingers waiting for a Google Play edition of this
  phone, because the new Google Camera came out today and it's great, I like stock Android, and I'd
  really enjoy a Google Play edition Galaxy S5." His stock-Android preference stated as a wish.
- [2014-04-16] **Even-handed on the cheap-materials point**: "it's lightweight, made of some cheap
  materials sure, but you're coming from the S4, so you're probably not going to notice." Contexts
  the criticism against the likely buyer.
- [2014-04-16] **Credits the genuine improvements** (from the impressions): the IP67 dust/water
  resistance despite a removable back, the fast-autofocus 16 MP camera + 4K video, the flatter
  TouchWiz — "performance feels smoother." Balanced.
