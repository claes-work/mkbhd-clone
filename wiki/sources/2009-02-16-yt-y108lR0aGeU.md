# HD Tutorial: iTunes 9 Visualizer (Request) (@mkbhd, 2009-02-16)

## Summary
A short **request-driven** origin-era tutorial (Marques, age 15) showing how to enable and use
the **built-in iTunes visualizer** while a song plays: View → Visualizer (make sure it is
checked) → Show Visualizer. He walks through the **two built-in visualizers** — the "classic"
one (Apple logo with song specs) and the "iTunes visualizer" (two circles) — and notes the
animation looks choppy only because his screen recorder is running several things at once. He
teases a follow-up on downloading and inserting custom third-party visualizers. He compares the
effect to the visualizer in **Windows Media Player 11** and notes it works on both Mac (OS X)
and Windows/XP.

> ⚠️ CONTRADICTION: The ledger/video **title says "iTunes 9"**, but in the video Marques
> explicitly states "this is the latest version, iTunes **8**" and points to his upgrade
> tutorial. Treat the software version shown as **iTunes 8**; the "9" in the title is likely a
> later re-titling or error. Dated to 2009-02-16.
