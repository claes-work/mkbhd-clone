# Google Nexus 6 Unboxing + Hands On! (@mkbhd, 2014-11-05)

## Summary
An unboxing / first-impressions of the **white Nexus 6** — "a really really big Moto X running stock
Android." Marques's first-hand read is dominated by size (he compares it against the Note 4, iPhone 6
Plus, and Droid Turbo), notes it's lighter than expected with a Moto-X-like metal band, and flags the
fast **Turbo Charger** in the box. Low review-yield; the setup for the full review.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2014-11-05] **It's a big phone, first and foremost** — a 5.9" display, "definitely larger than most";
  taller and wider than the Note 4 (5.7"), about as tall as the iPhone 6 Plus (5.5") but thicker with a
  much larger battery.
- [2014-11-05] **Lighter than it looks**, with the same metal ring as the Moto X ("feels very similar in
  hand").
- [2014-11-05] **In the box: a fast Turbo Charger** ("what's going to help you get that super-fast
  turbocharging — we'll be testing this in the full review").
- [2014-11-05] **First phone running Android 5.0 Lollipop out of the box** — deeper testing deferred to
  the review.
