## Summary

prank**, which flipped every video (and all the page text) **upside down**. It doubles as a
genuine tip: to view a video right-side-up, **delete the `&flip=1` parameter at the end of
the URL** and hit enter. He demonstrates on a video from the channel **"tektopia"** — a peer
creator he says he's **subscribed to** and recommends viewers check out — and ties the day
back to the ongoing **Conficker worm** rumor, still wondering aloud whether that was "a virus
