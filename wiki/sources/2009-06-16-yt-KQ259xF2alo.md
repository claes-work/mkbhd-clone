# Unboxed: Audéo PFE Headphones (@mkbhd, 2009-06-16)

## Summary
An unboxing of the Audéo "Perfect Fit Earphones" (PFE), and a small but real milestone in
the creator-business thread: this unit did not come from the manufacturer's shipping
department but from **a PR/communications agency** ("this is coming from Fleishman-Hillard
Communications"), and it arrived by **overnight shipping from another state**. A
15-year-old reviewer is now on an agency's seeding list. He treats the shipping itself as
evidence about the company — "I'm definitely impressed with the shipping, they definitely
are into quality service and support" — and restates the in-box-value criterion for the
third time in a week: "I do like headphones and companies that provide a little extra
stuff instead of just the headphones." He also restates the unbox/review split and the
1–2-week turnaround, and names the hardware/software alternation rule in the first ten
seconds. ★ L3-candidate.

## Key claims
- [2009-06-16] **The unit came via a PR agency**: "this is coming from Fleishman-Hillard
  Communications, so I'll check that out afterwards" — he does not yet know who they are.
  First agency-sourced review unit in the corpus.
- [2009-06-16] **Shipping speed read as a signal about the company**: "I am impressed with
  the shipping, because this in fact was overnight shipping from a different state... they
  definitely are into quality service and support at Audéo."
- [2009-06-16] **In-box value criterion, third statement in eight days**: "I do like
  headphones and companies that provide a little extra stuff instead of just the
  headphones — they'll give you a carrying case, they'll give you these little hooks that
  will go on your ears if say you want to use these during a workout." (Cf.
  [[2009-06-09-yt-ap9rbdCKnm8]] and [[2009-06-14-yt-6gJMCu0YpSk]].)
- [2009-06-16] **Standard unbox/review split, restated**: "like the rest of my unboxing
  videos I'll give my first impressions of the product... as well as give a full review
  video in one to two weeks."
- [2009-06-16] **Deferral discipline**: of the spec copy on the box — "I'll definitely go
  over a lot of this in the review video so you guys can tell exactly what you're getting
  should you decide to get this." The unboxing does not pre-empt the verdict.
- [2009-06-16] **Contents itemized**: multilingual booklet and full user guide, carrying
  case, over-ear hooks for workouts, multiple eartip sizes, plus two items he cannot
  identify on camera and promises to cover later.
- [2009-06-16] **Alternation rule stated up front**: "I've been alternating back and forth
  between hardware and software here" — fourth statement of the rule inside two weeks.
- [2009-06-16] **Vocabulary still forming**: "telling you a little bit about the — what do
  you call them — about the earphones" — the 15-year-old audibly reaching for the product
  category name, a marker of the origin-era voice.
