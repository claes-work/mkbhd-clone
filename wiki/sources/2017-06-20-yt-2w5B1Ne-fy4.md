# OnePlus 5 Review! (@mkbhd, 2017-06-20)

## Summary
Review of the OnePlus 5 — an incremental, spec-loaded enthusiast phone. The clone-relevant core is his
principled take on the iPhone-copycat accusations — **"if you're going to copy, copy the good stuff"** — plus
the small-company advantage/disadvantage framing and his **feel-over-looks** design value.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-06-20] **"If you're going to copy, copy the good stuff."** On the iPhone-copycat backlash (dual camera
  in the corner; a camera app that's "a blatant copy of the iPhone's"): "I've always said I don't really mind
  if you copy stuff — but if you're going to copy something, **copy the good stuff.** Copy the iPhone's camera,
  copy the fingerprint reader — don't copy the bad stuff, don't copy Siri." A durable, principled stance on
  copying (cf. "some features are too good not to be shared"). Also source-critical: "the body resembles other
  phones even more than the iPhone."
- [2017-06-20] **The small-company advantage/disadvantage framing.** "OnePlus is a young, small company — the
  disadvantage is they don't have access to the high-end tech Apple/Samsung do (in The Verge's making-of, they
  admitted they'd have liked an Infinity/bezel-less display but don't have the tech). The advantage is they
  can actually listen to their core fanbase and give them that exact product." A structural read of why a
  small OEM makes the phone it does. (Anticipates the later Nothing small-OEM thesis.)
- [2017-06-20] **"It has to feel good more than it has to look good."** "How much do you actually look at the
  back of your phone? It has to feel good more than look good — and the OnePlus 5 is a good-feeling phone
  (7.5 mm, rounded, all metal, the alert slider, front fingerprint reader)." His feel-over-looks design value.
- [2017-06-20] **Matte black + skins-for-color (restated).** "The only colors are black and almost-black,
  which I love — matte black is great — but for variety, grab a dbrand skin: my favorite way to add color
  without the bulk of a case." The recurring matte-black + skins-over-cases thread.
- [2017-06-20] **Camera = hit-or-miss (honest).** "16 MP f/1.7 + 20 MP f/2.6 telephoto, EIS not OIS — the
  sensor is capable of great clean photos, but it's a hit-or-miss experience day to day; I wish it had OIS."
  Names the inconsistency plainly. Notes it skips the bezel-less trend (thin side bezels, not Infinity).
