# Samson MD5 Desktop Microphone Stand [HD] (@mkbhd, 2010-04-28)

## Summary
A short unboxing of a Samson MD5 desktop microphone stand, framed not as a routine
product unboxing but as a **production-workflow upgrade**: the stand lets him sit at
his desk, see his screen, and record/edit voiceovers in one motion, replacing a setup
where he had to stand at a separate vocal booth away from his screen.

## Key claims
- [2010-04-28] **Voiceover workflow bottleneck named and fixed**: prior to this stand,
  recording required standing next to a vocal booth without screen visibility, making
  voiceovers "a little bit more difficult" and, by his account, suppressing upload
  frequency. He frames the stand purchase as directly enabling more (and higher-quality)
  future content.
- [2010-04-28] **Self-funded gear purchases from early on**: describes purchasing the
  stand himself via B&H, consistent with the origin-era pattern of self-funded
  incremental production upgrades (see [[wiki/topics/production-filmmaking/production-filmmaking]]).
