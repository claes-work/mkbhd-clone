# HD Tutorial: Camtasia Powerpoint Integration (@mkbhd, 2009-02-07)

## Summary
Origin-era production-tooling walkthrough. Marques (age 15) shows what happens the first
time PowerPoint 2007 launches after installing Camtasia Studio 6: the Camtasia PowerPoint
add-in loads a sample presentation and a "record" button on the Add-ins tab that captures
a PowerPoint directly (with optional webcam preview). He notes on Vista the camera
preview records by default if it's in the capture area, and remarks the compressed webcam
image is "pretty good quality." References his separate video "on how to get the
software."

## Key claims
- [2009-02-07] **How-to:** the Camtasia Studio 6 PowerPoint add-in adds a Record button
  to PowerPoint's Add-ins tab, recording slides directly with an optional webcam preview;
  on Vista the preview is captured by default when in-frame.
- [2009-02-07] **Production note (dated):** judges the compressed webcam preview as "pretty
