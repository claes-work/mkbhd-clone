# LG Voyager Hardware Overview [HD] (@mkbhd, 2009-04-10)

## Summary
The **first video of an LG Voyager review series** (Marques, age 15) and one of his **earliest phone
hardware overviews** — an origin document for the smartphone/tech-review format he later became
known for. He opens with a ★ **review-methodology origin note**: he's doing the series because of a
**"large number of cases and products I'm getting in the PO box, being sent to me for me to do
reviews."** He then walks the **Verizon LG Voyager** hardware: front touchscreen with send/end/clear
keys, a **2.5 mm (not 3.5 mm) headphone jack**, microSD slot (linking his prior microSD tutorial),
mini-USB charge port, a hard camera button + hold switch + volume/zoom rocker, a **2.0 MP camera
(1600×1200)**, and an inside **full QWERTY keyboard**. He flags an honest **production limitation** —
struggling to lower the screen brightness so he has to hold the phone "at an angle" to film it
clearly — and promises follow-up software reviews, case reviews, and unboxings.

- [2009-04-10] ★ **Review-methodology origin**: he launched the Voyager series because **companies
  and viewers were mailing him cases/accessories to his PO box to review** — the earliest recorded
  "products sent for review" workflow.
- [2009-04-10] **Verizon LG Voyager** specs as he presents them: front resistive touchscreen +

  port; top **hard camera button**, **hold switch**, and **volume rocker doubling as camera zoom**;
