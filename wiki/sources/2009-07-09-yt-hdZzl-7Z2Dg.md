# Youtube CENSORS Comments (@mkbhd, 2009-07-09)

## Summary
An off-format screencast (explicitly not one of his usual HD tutorials) reacting to
YouTube auto-censoring curse words in comments with asterisks. He demonstrates the
behavior live on the comment section of his own [[2009-07-08-yt-DksEwjFH-o4|Fission
tutorial]] video, noting a viewer's angry comment about his partnership denial got
censored, and connects it to a broader complaint about unrequested platform changes
(forced channel 2.0 layout, aggressive copyright takedowns) rolled out "in the past
couple of weeks." Also dates a same-day YouTube outage.
