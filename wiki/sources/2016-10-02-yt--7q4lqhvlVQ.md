# LG V20: Most Underrated Phone?! (@mkbhd, 2016-10-02)

## Summary
A "five best things" review of the LG V20, framed as **the most underrated phone out right now** — a
clean instance of his champion-the-underrated instinct. Notable for the quad-DAC audio focus, its
rare-breed **removable battery + microSD** on a high-end phone, and the distinction of being the **first
phone to ship with Android 7.0 out of the box** (not a Nexus/Pixel).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2016-10-02] **"Their most underrated phone right now."** "It's been a pretty quiet year for LG, but
  this is for sure their most underrated phone — a really good phone." The champion-the-underrated instinct
  applied ([[2016-04-29-yt-9kiECQodMqE]] did the same for the HTC 10).
- [2016-10-02] **The quad-DAC as a real audio differentiator.** "The V20 has a 32-bit hi-fi quad DAC —
  where you'll really hear it is driving higher-impedance headphones; if you used to need an external
  mobile DAC to drive high-end headphones, this handles it all." A specific, credit-where-due audio note.
- [2016-10-02] **Removable battery + microSD = "a rare breed."** "A 3,200 mAh *removable* battery — and
  the release is way cleaner than the G5's method, which force-shut-down the phone — plus expandable
  storage. Those two things on a high-end smartphone by themselves kind of make it a rare breed." Values
  user-serviceability that the market was abandoning.
- [2016-10-02] **Build is a big step up from the G5.** "The fit and finish is a lot better than the G5
  from earlier this year — not that that was a high bar to clear — but this is a seriously well-built,
  surprisingly light unibody aluminum phone." (A pointed callback to the G5's "barely modular" letdown.)
- [2016-10-02] **The second screen — great idea, wants more control.** Praises the always-on secondary
  display for permanent shortcuts, but "I wish I had more control over what shows up — I'd mix my favorite
  apps and toggles" and "I'm not sure about the position at the very top; for smaller hands it's a stretch
  — maybe put it on the bottom." The great-idea-held-back-by-execution critique recurs.
- [2016-10-02] **Dual cameras — "no, the iPhone is not the first."** "LG did dual cameras before, in the
  G5 and V10 — the iPhone is not the first." A 16 MP normal + 8 MP 135° super-wide (the "GoPro look"),
  used independently for recreation, not image-merging. Source-critical about who did it first.
- [2016-10-02] **First to ship Android 7.0 — with amazing standby.** "This is the first phone running
  Android 7.0 out of the box — not a Nexus, not a Pixel. Standby has been seriously impressive: I slept
  it 24 straight hours with the second screen on and woke it up at 96%." A concrete real-world battery data
  point.

## Notable verbatim quotes
