# iPad Pro 2017 (10.5') Review! (@mkbhd, 2017-06-16)

## Summary
Review of the 10.5" iPad Pro — a tablet that got him "using tablets again a lot more often." The headline is
the **120Hz ProMotion display** as the standout upgrade ("a whole layer of butter"), alongside the A10X making
the iPad "almost as powerful as a MacBook Pro," and the recurring wish for **quick charging / USB-C**.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-06-16] **ProMotion 120Hz = the absolute standout.** "The biggest, most impressive new feature is the
  display's 120 Hz refresh — unlike every other Apple display ever (60 Hz). Everything is now 60 fps native,
  twice as close to one-to-one with your finger or the Pencil; it adds a whole layer of butter to the OS. And
  it **dynamically drops the refresh** for slow tasks (reading) to save power. You have to try it to notice
  it, but once you do, you'll notice it — I really hope this comes to the next iPhone." His best-feature call.
- [2017-06-16] **"Almost as powerful as a MacBook Pro" — with the honest caveat.** "The A10X Fusion (12-core
  GPU) + 4 GB RAM benchmarks really high; I couldn't find anything in daily use that pushes it — it crushes
  everything, barely gets warm, and keeps an insane number of apps in memory (I opened an app from *yesterday*
  right where I left off)." Reads the 'as powerful as a laptop' headline as "kind of true."
- [2017-06-16] **The camera is overkill — and he says he won't use it.** "It's the same 12 MP f/1.8 system as
  the iPhone 7 — the best camera in a tablet, super overkill; great for document-scanning-to-searchable-text
  and FaceTime and 4K/OIS video. But I wouldn't endorse using it as your main camera — you just won't see me
  using it." Fair-but-honest about a spec most people won't use.
- [2017-06-16] **Best speakers in any tablet.** "Quad speakers on all four sides — the whole thing becomes an
  echo chamber; loud, crisp, clear. The best speakers in any tablet, also." A concrete best-in-class call.
- [2017-06-16] **The recurring hardware wishes: quick charging + USB-C.** "The battery's great (~10 hrs, ~2
  days), but I wish iPads had **quick charging** — these charge slow for such a big battery — and it's
  Lightning, wish it were USB-C (not expecting it)." His standing USB-C / charging-convenience asks.
- [2017-06-16] **iOS 11 is what makes it interesting.** Ties the review to iOS 11 (the dock, Files app,
  drag-and-drop) making the iPad "reconsider as a primary computer for more people" — the productivity story,
  its own separate deep dive. Frames the review as audience-requested (asked on Snapchat).

## Notable verbatim quotes

> I guarantee you'll notice it."
