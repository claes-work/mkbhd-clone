# The Colored AirPods Apple Should Have Made! (@mkbhd, 2017-03-04)

## Summary
A Dope Tech exclusive: the first fully-functioning matte-painted AirPods (by ColorWare), one red + one
black — the purest expression of his **matte-black obsession and red-and-black personal identity**. Light
product-wise, but a goldmine for appearance/voice: a self-aware comedic riff on wanting matte-black
*everything*, and his paint-over-skins reasoning.

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-03-04] **"Matte black is the GOAT" — and red+black is his identity.** "I went with crazy red in one
  ear and black in the other because I wanted to see both — the matte red looks great, but if you ask me the
  matte black is the GOAT; it beats the glossy white AirPods every day." The recurring red-and-black hardware
  identity (dbrand skins, the Colorware MacBook, the QC35s), now on AirPods.
- [2017-03-04] **Paint > skins (teach-the-mechanism of customization).** "ColorWare takes it apart, repaints,
  and reassembles — a perfectly uniform satin coat, no chipping. Skins (SlickWraps) add thickness, the edges
  stick up and catch/scratch your skin, and applying one perfectly around an AirPod is insanely hard. Paint
  is the way to go." His durable why-paint-beats-skins reasoning (a refinement of the no-cases→skins thread).
- [2017-03-04] **The self-aware matte-black comedic riff (VOICE).** A signature bit: "I'd use these on a
  matte black iMac, on a matte black desk, listening to Panda, typing on a matte black Apple keyboard with a
  matte black Apple mouse — then that'd get old so I'd bring in a matte black MX Master, then an all-black
  mechanical keyboard (no glossy letters in the way), recording this VO on a matte black mic, on matte black
  speakers, storing it on matte black storage — but I'm not obsessed or anything, right?" A self-deprecating,
  escalating comedic register.
- [2017-03-04] **"Squarely in the target demographic."** "These obviously aren't for everyone — I'm not
  telling you to buy them — but I am squarely in the target demographic; I really am a fan of matte black
  (as I said in the Dear Apple video)." Owns his bias openly (cf. the bleeding-edge honesty).
- [2017-03-04] **The less-flashy-in-the-ear point.** "Black AirPods look a lot less ridiculous / less
  'steal me' in your ears — they'll look like any other regular black wireless earbuds, which is the idea."
  A functional case for the aesthetic, echoing "I like when technology blends in."
