# Tech Channel of the Week #5: mtpflyers (@mkbhd, 2009-06-08)

## Summary
The fifth installment of the weekly channel-spotlight series (previous: #4
[[2009-05-31-yt-u_Rp0GS2duE]]). Marques, age 15, tours the channel of "mtpflyers", a
one-man iPhone-case reviewer approaching 2,000 subscribers and 18,000 channel views. Two
things are new for the corpus. (1) He states the **series cadence as loose rather than
fixed** — "every week or weekend or a Monday like this case" — the first explicit
acknowledgement that his own publishing schedule slips. (2) He describes copying a
practice from the channel he is spotlighting: **publishing the week's upcoming videos on
his channel page**, "which I'm actually starting to do." He also restates the discovery
routine that drives the series: he visits and comments on every channel that subscribes
to him, and subscribes back to the good ones. ★ L3-candidate.

## Key claims
- [2009-06-08] **Series cadence, stated loosely**: Tech Channel of the Week "is a
  relatively new series of videos that goes up every week or weekend or a Monday like
  this case" — publication drift acknowledged on air rather than hidden.
- [2009-06-08] **Selection funnel restated**: "every channel that does subscribe to me I
  go ahead and check out their channel and leave a comment saying you know thanks for
  subscribing... and sometimes I see some really nice channels out there and I'll
  subscribe to them as well." Consistent with the routine in [[2009-05-23-yt-oqoHe7xw8uE]]
  and the "subscribe to qualify" rule from [[2009-05-27-yt-b7Nc78QqDs0]]'s era.
- [2009-06-08] **Practice adopted from another creator**: mtpflyers lists his upcoming
  week of videos on his channel — "you can always see on his channel, which I'm actually
  starting to do, the videos that are upcoming this week." Marques notes he keeps his
  own version "on my channel page instead of in my channel description."
- [2009-06-08] **What he praises in a channel**: a mix of hardware and software/app
  videos, playlists, collaborations, interviews with other YouTube reviewers, regular
  uploads — the same criteria he applies to his own programming.
- [2009-06-08] **Subscriber advocacy as the format's payload**: "right now he is gaining
  very quickly on 2,000 subscribers, so an early congrats," and "I think we can get him
  to 2,000 subscribers" — the spotlight is explicitly meant to move numbers.
- [2009-06-08] **He entered another creator's giveaway**: mtpflyers' most-viewed video is
  a new-iPod-shuffle giveaway "which I was unfortunately a bit late for" — Marques as
  audience member on the same platform he is building on.
- [2009-06-08] **Minor design observation**: he closes by noting the channel's
  background, "a nice little background there with little apple logos" — channel-art
  taste already part of the read.
