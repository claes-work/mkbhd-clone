# Unboxed: Sena UltraSlim Leather Pouch for LG Voyager (@mkbhd, 2009-06-14)

## Summary
A short unboxing of a Sena Cases leather pouch for the LG Voyager, sent by the
manufacturer — the fourth Voyager-accessory item in the corpus. Two things are new. (1) He
**states the unbox/review boundary as a rule he holds himself to**: "I don't want to get
too far into a review or impressions or anything here in the unboxing video, because I
save that for the full 100% review video" — the firmest articulation yet of the two-step
he has been running since April. (2) He **puts a question to the audience** rather than
just answering one: "please comment below, what do you think of this package that Sena
gives you — is that good, or is that normal, what do you think?" — the earliest instance
in the corpus of soliciting the audience's *judgment* (not just video requests). He also
sets an explicit **1–2-week review turnaround** and notes he is surprised the accessory
run has continued: "you might have thought the LG Voyager hardware case reviews or
unboxings were over — and in fact, so did I."
