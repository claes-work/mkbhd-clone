# Top 5 iOS 11 Features! (@mkbhd, 2017-06-10)

## Summary
A Top-5 of iOS 11 — "maybe the biggest iOS update yet," both in features and a surprise visual refresh. The
clone-relevant beat is a textbook **impressions-vs-use reversal**: he disliked the redesigned Control Center
on stage, then flipped 180° after living with it. Also notes iOS updates reach far more phones than Android's
(the fragmentation point).

## Key claims (paraphrase, dated)
- [2017-06-10] **The impressions-vs-use reversal (Control Center).** "My initial reaction on stage — you
  probably judged from my tweet — was lukewarm; it looked goofy. But after using it in my actual hands, my
  opinion changed about 180°. The old Control Center was finicky anyway; this is a welcome change: full-screen,
  legitimately **customizable** (add/remove/rearrange toggles — of all the things for Apple to make custom, I
  wouldn't have guessed this), with 3D-Touch for finer control." A clean demonstration of his substance-over-
  first-impressions ethos. #1 feature.
- [2017-06-10] **Screen recording = his favorite (a creator's-eye pick).** "You can now record your screen to
  a video with no cables, with mic audio on/off — super useful in a ton of ways." (Winks that Snapchat doesn't
  yet detect it.) A production-minded favorite.
- [2017-06-10] **iOS reaches more phones than Android (the fragmentation contrast).** "As a bonus, most of
  your iPhones will get iOS 11 — I can't say everyone's phone gets Android 8.0." A recurring platform-update
  contrast (in Apple's favor here).
- [2017-06-10] **Siri got faster — worth re-testing vs. Google Assistant.** "The new Siri is a lot faster and
  marginally more helpful (live translation now); just because of the speed it may be worth re-matching it to
  Google Assistant like I did before. Still in beta — I'll let it bake. Glad they're still working on it."
  Reserves a fresh verdict pending the ship.
- [2017-06-10] **The visual overhaul + screenshot markup.** "A lot of flatter, cleaner, tidier visuals (bars
  instead of dots, new icons, new font, smoother animations)." Plus the new **screenshot flow** (float to the
  corner, mark it up, batch multiple) — "pretty awesome." Small-UX-that-matters appreciation.

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "Since I've spent time with it in my actual hands and used it, my opinion of it has changed about 180°."
> "Of all the things for Apple to make custom, I wouldn't think this would be it — but this is awesome."
