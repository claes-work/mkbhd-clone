# Unboxed: Adjustable Laptop Stand from HandStands (@mkbhd, 2009-04-25)

## Summary
An origin-era **unboxing** (Marques, age 15) of the **HandStands "iDesk" adjustable laptop stand**
($39.99), sent to him for review (he gets to keep it; shipped via FedEx, "halfway across the country in
two and a half days"). The oversized box doesn't fit his framing, so he **relocates the shoot to the
floor** and switches camera angles mid-video to show it. He reads the box art (six adjustable-height
gears, external-keyboard use, a **detachable four-port USB 2.0 hub**), unpacks the styrofoam, quick-start
guide, USB cable, the plastic stand, and power adapter, and gives first impressions: plastic build, a
useful tilt, accommodates up to a 17-inch laptop. He flags one con out of the box — the angle is steep
enough that you'll want an **external keyboard** — and promises a **full review** to follow.
